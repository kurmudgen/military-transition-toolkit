# Indeed for Veterans - Competitive Analysis

## Platform Overview

**Website:** indeed.com/veterans
**Parent Company:** Indeed (Recruit Holdings Co., Ltd.)
**Indeed Founded:** 2004
**Military Program:** Indeed for Veterans/Indeed for Military
**Market Position:** **"#1 job search site for military experienced job seekers"** (per Indeed survey)
**Headquarters:** Austin, TX (Indeed global HQ)

**Scale:**
- World's **largest job site**
- **350M+ unique visitors/month** globally
- **16+ job postings per second**
- Presence in 60+ countries

**Military-Specific Metrics:**
- **9.3M monthly clicks** on job posts from military-experienced candidates
- **130,000+ resumes** updated or added each month (military experience)
- **148,817 military resumes** available (as of January 2024)

**Target Audience:**
- Active military preparing to transition
- Veterans (all service branches, ranks, specialties)
- Military spouses
- Employers seeking military talent

## Feature Breakdown

### For Military Job Seekers (FREE)

**Core Platform Features:**
- Job search with military experience filters
- Resume builder and hosting
- Profile creation with military background
- Job alerts (email notifications)
- One-click apply functionality
- Company reviews (Glassdoor integration)
- Salary data and comparison tools

**Military-Specific Services:**
- **Free Resume Review:** 10-minute personalized video feedback for veterans and military spouses
- **Virtual Workshop Series:** Discover jobs, update resumes, interview prep
- **Military Skills Translation:** Tools to translate MOS/military roles to civilian terms
- **Career Advice:** Dedicated military career advice section
- **Military Skills Badges:** Highlight military experience on profile

**Preparation Resources:**
- Resume writing guidance
- Interview preparation tips
- Job search strategy advice
- Transition planning resources

### For Employers (PAID)

**Indeed for Veterans - Hiring Platform:**
- **Filter by Military Service:** 'Active' or 'Former' military candidates
- **Security Clearance Search:** Surface candidates with active clearances
- **Resume Alerts:** Notifications for new military candidate resumes
- **Brand Management:** Tools across Indeed and Glassdoor for military hiring initiatives
- **Increased Visibility:** Job post advertising to military talent across Indeed, Glassdoor, media network
- **Candidate Screening:** Skills assessments, interview scheduling
- **Centralized Dashboard:** Manage entire hiring process

**Military-Ready Accreditation:**
- Employers can achieve "military-ready" certification (partnership with Hiring Our Heroes)
- Helps veterans identify genuinely supportive employers
- **But:** 22% of veterans say expectations were NOT met (Indeed's own research)

**Metrics (Employer-Focused):**
- **96% of employers satisfied** with Indeed for military talent search

### What They DON'T Have

- **No employer verification:** "Military-friendly" is self-reported
- No transition coaching beyond job search
- No community or peer networking
- No benefits navigation (VA claims, state benefits)
- No comprehensive education guidance
- No placement support or recruiter assistance
- No employer accountability tracking
- No retention data published
- Limited military context (general platform adapted for veterans)

## Pricing Model & Monetization

**For Job Seekers (Military):**
- **100% FREE:** All services including:
  - Job search and applications
  - Resume hosting
  - Resume review (10-min video)
  - Workshops
  - Career advice
  - Company reviews

**For Employers:**
- **Sponsored Jobs:** Pay-per-click model for job visibility
- **Indeed Hiring Platform:** Subscription for premium features
  - Candidate database access
  - Advanced screening tools
  - Brand management
  - Analytics dashboard
- **Pricing:** Not publicly disclosed; custom quotes based on:
  - Job volume
  - Geographic markets
  - Industry
  - Screening/assessment needs

**General Indeed Model:**
- Free job postings (limited visibility)
- Sponsored listings (pay for prominence)
- Tiered subscriptions for high-volume hirers
- No contingency fees (not a recruiting firm)

## Weaknesses & Vulnerabilities

### Employer Authenticity Crisis (Indeed's Own Research)

**Major Problem Identified:**
- **45% of veterans** had experience with "military-ready" or "military-friendly" employers
- **22% said expectations were NOT met**
- **44% of disappointed group** chose not to move forward as candidates
- **This is a MASSIVE credibility problem Indeed acknowledges but hasn't solved**

### Retention Crisis

**Critical Issue:**
- **60-80% of veterans** leave first civilian job before 2 years
- Veterans have trouble getting promoted
- Often feel underutilized and uninspired
- **Suggests poor employer-veteran matching despite #1 platform status**

### Platform Limitations

**Generic Job Board Issues:**
1. **No Specialization:** All industries/roles mixed together (vs. military-focused platforms)
2. **Volume Over Quality:** Millions of jobs create noise and overwhelm
3. **Limited Verification:** "Military-friendly" is self-reported by employers (22% disappoint)
4. **No Placement Support:** Self-service only; no recruiter assistance
5. **Skills Translation Gaps:** Veterans struggle to translate military skills (per Indeed research)
6. **Spam and Low-Quality Jobs:** Open platform includes unverified employers

**Military-Specific Gaps:**
7. **No Transition Coaching:** Job listings without career guidance
8. **No Community:** Transactional platform without peer support
9. **Limited Military Context:** General platform adapted for veterans, not purpose-built
10. **Employer Education Needed:** 96% employer satisfaction but 22% veteran disappointment = gap

### Competition

11. **LinkedIn Rival:** 37% of veterans also use LinkedIn (per Monster/Military.com survey)
12. **Specialized Boards:** ClearanceJobs, military-specific platforms offer targeted experiences
13. **Recruiting Firms:** Orion, Cameron-Brooks provide human touch Indeed lacks

### Data Quality Concerns

- 148K resumes may include outdated/inactive profiles
- No verification of military service claims
- Resume quality varies widely
- No accountability for employer claims
