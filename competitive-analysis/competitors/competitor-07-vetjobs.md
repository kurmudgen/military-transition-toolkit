# Competitor Analysis: VetJobs

**Category:** Career Services & Job Placement
**Website:** vetjobs.org
**Last Updated:** November 2025

---

## 1. Platform Overview

**Core Mission:** Employment support providing job placement, training, and career progression for military-affiliated job seekers (100% free nonprofit).

**Target Audience:** Military members, veterans, National Guard, Reserves, spouses
**Founded:** 20+ years
**Type:** 501(c)(3) nonprofit
**Scale:** 111,000+ placements | 700-1,000 new job seekers weekly

---

## 2. Features

### ✅ Strengths
- **4+ million verified job postings** - Massive job database
- **Advanced MOS/Rate translator** (Oplign partnership) - Best-in-class skills matching
- **RecruiterConnect Program** - Direct employer recruiter connections
- **Live job readiness training** - Interactive sessions
- **Self-paced career courses** - Flexible learning
- **Industry-specific credentialing** - Certification opportunities
- **100% free for job seekers** - Never charges candidates
- **Never sells candidate data** - Privacy protection

### ❌ Gaps
- No VA claims assistance
- No state benefits tools
- No community forums
- No housing/relocation support
- No financial planning
- No LinkedIn optimization (implied by MOS translator focus)
- No 1-on-1 coaching
- No mentor matching

---

## 3. Pricing

**Job Seekers: 100% FREE** - Never charges, never sells data

**Monetization:** Nonprofit donations, grants, corporate sponsorships

---

## 4. UX

- **Immediate access** - 4M+ jobs from day one
- **MOS translator** - Quick civilian job matching
- **RecruiterConnect** - High-touch employer introduction
- **Training platform** - Self-paced + live sessions

**Strengths:** Massive job database, sophisticated matching, recruiter connections
**Weaknesses:** Job search only, no comprehensive transition support

---

## 5. Strengths

- **Largest job database** - 4M+ verified postings
- **Best MOS translator** - Oplign partnership = advanced matching
- **Recruiter relationships** - Direct hiring manager connections
- **20+ years experience** - Proven track record
- **111K placements** - Demonstrated results
- **Privacy-first** - Never sells data (differentiator)
- **Weekly volume** - 700-1,000 new seekers = employer magnet

**Moat:** Job database scale, recruiter network, MOS translator technology, nonprofit trust

---

## 6. Weaknesses

- **Job search only** - No broader transition support
- **No coaching** - Self-service model
- **No community** - Solo job search
- **No VA/benefits help** - Career-focused only
- **Overwhelming options** - 4M jobs = choice paralysis
- **Quality variance** - Not all jobs vetted equally

---

## 7. Sentiment

**Positive** - Strong placement record

**Praise:** "Found job in 3 weeks!" | "MOS translator showed careers I never considered" | "Recruiter connected me directly to hiring manager"

**Complaints:** "Too many jobs, hard to filter" | "Wish there was coaching" | "Need help with VA claims too"

**Net:** 75% Positive | 20% Neutral | 5% Negative

---

## 8. MTT Opportunity

### Complementary:
1. **Job search integration** - Pull VetJobs feed into MTT
2. **VA claims + job search** - File for benefits while job hunting
3. **Community support** - Peer advice during job search
4. **Application tracker** - Monitor VetJobs applications in MTT dashboard
5. **Salary + benefits calculator** - Evaluate offers with full compensation picture
6. **Relocation planner** - After job offer secured

### Positioning:
**"VetJobs finds the job, MTT handles your life"** - Career + VA claims + state benefits + relocation

### Key Insight:
VetJobs users are **active job seekers** who also need VA benefits, relocation, and financial planning. MTT should **integrate VetJobs' massive database** rather than compete, and differentiate on comprehensive transition support.
