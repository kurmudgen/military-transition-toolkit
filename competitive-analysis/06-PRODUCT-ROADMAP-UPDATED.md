### Feature 1.3: AI-Powered MOS Translation ü§ñ TECHNOLOGY EDGE
**Gap:** Existing tools are static keyword matching (ClearedJobs, Walmart, Home Depot)
**Competitor Analysis:**
- Static tools: Limited to direct MOS-to-job mapping
- No personalization: Same output for all E-5s with same MOS
- No career path guidance: Just job titles, not pathways

**Build:**
- OpenAI API integration (GPT-4)
- Input: MOS/rating, rank, years of service, security clearance, education, location preference
- Output:
  - Top 20 civilian career matches (vs. competitors' top 5)
  - Career pathway recommendations (entry ‚Üí mid ‚Üí senior level)
  - Required certifications for each path
  - Salary ranges by location
  - Job market demand data

**Free Tier:** Top 5 matches (competitive with others)
**Premium Tier:** Top 20 matches + career paths + certifications

**Why Critical:**
- AI = better matches than competitors' static tools
- Personalization = higher accuracy
- Premium upsell driver
- Continuous improvement (model learns from user feedback)

**Success Metrics:**
- 90%+ user satisfaction with matches
- 40% use translation tool ‚Üí upgrade to premium
- 1,000+ translations per month

### Feature 1.4: Verified Employer Reviews üè¢ TRUST BUILDER
**Gap:** Military Friendly is self-reported; no Glassdoor equivalent for veteran employment
**Competitor Analysis:**
- Military Friendly: Organizations self-report (potential inflation)
- No accountability: 22-45% of veterans report "military-friendly" employers didn't meet expectations
- No retention tracking: Employers aren't held to outcomes

**Build:**
- Veteran employee review platform
- 5-star rating + written review
- Categories: Skills translation support, onboarding, growth, culture, retention
- Verification: DD214 or military email
- Anonymous option available

**MTT Accountability Score (100-point scale):**
- Veteran review average: 40%
- 1-year retention rate: 30%
- 2-year retention rate: 20%
- Career advancement (promotions/raises): 10%

**Launch Strategy:**
- Target 10 major employers (Walmart, JPMorgan, Home Depot, Amazon, Lockheed, Boeing, USAA, Raytheon, Booz Allen, CACI)
- Recruit 20 reviews per employer (200 reviews total in first 90 days)
- Display Accountability Score on employer pages

**Why Critical:**
- Solves major pain point (employer authenticity)
- No competitor verifies employer claims
- Two-sided marketplace driver (employers engage to improve scores)
- PR opportunity (annual "Best Employers" report)

**Success Metrics:**
- 500+ verified reviews in 6 months
- 25+ employers scored
- Press coverage in Military Times or Stars & Stripes
- 10+ employers pay for premium placement to improve visibility

### Feature 1.5: VA Claims Builder (Guided) üéñÔ∏è HIGH-VALUE PREMIUM
**Gap:** VA.gov is complex; no simplified guided builder exists
**Competitor Analysis:**
- VA.gov: Official but overwhelming (100+ forms, complex process)
- MOAA: Guidance articles only (not interactive tool)
- VSOs: 1-on-1 help but limited scalability

**Build:**
- Step-by-step wizard for common claims:
  - Disability compensation
  - Healthcare enrollment
  - Education benefits (GI Bill)
  - Home loan certificate of eligibility
  - Vocational rehabilitation
- Document checklist (DD214, medical records, service records)
- Form pre-population (extract data from uploaded DD214)
- Submission-ready PDFs
- VSO referral for complex cases

**Free Tier:** Basic guidance + document checklist
**Premium Tier:** Guided builder + form pre-fill + PDF generation

**Why Critical:**
- High-value premium driver (claims worth $thousands)
- Complex = defensible (hard to replicate)
- Ongoing value (veterans file multiple claims over time)
- Partnership opportunity with VSOs

**Success Metrics:**
- 1,000+ claims initiated through MTT in 6 months
- 50% of premium users use claims builder
- 80%+ success rate for claims filed with MTT help
- Partnerships with 3+ VSOs for referrals
