## How to Find Email Addresses

Most YouTube creators who want to be contacted for business opportunities list their email on their channel's About page. To find it:

1. Go to the channel's YouTube page
2. Click on the "About" tab
3. Look for "View email address" button under the channel statistics
4. Click to reveal the email (you may need to complete a CAPTCHA)

**Note:** Not all creators make their email publicly visible. If no email is shown, they may prefer contact through:
- Social media (Instagram, Twitter/X)
- Website contact forms
- Comments on their videos
- Patreon or other creator platforms
