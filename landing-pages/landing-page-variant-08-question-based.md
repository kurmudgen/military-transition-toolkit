You can save your results to a free dashboard, explore related tools, and build a complete transition plan. Or just get your answers and leave—no pressure either way.

## A/B Test Hypothesis

### What We're Testing
**Primary:** Does a question-based approach (engaging curiosity, promising answers) convert better than other messaging angles?

**Secondary:** Does an interactive quiz/tool drive higher engagement and conversion than static content?

**Tertiary:** Does breaking down common questions reduce overwhelm and make the platform feel more accessible?

### Expected Winner
**High confidence this will be a strong performer**, especially with:
- **Search traffic** (users actively searching for answers to specific questions)
- **Mid-transition users** (6-12 months out) who have specific questions
- **Engaged learners** who want to explore before committing

**May underperform with:**
- Users who want social proof first (this page is light on testimonials)
- Users who are very early (18+ months) and don't have specific questions yet
- Users who are skeptical of quizzes/interactive elements ("just trying to get my email")

**Prediction:** Top-5 performer overall, #1-2 for organic search traffic and users with specific questions. Strong engagement metrics (time on page, interaction with quiz elements).

### Success Metrics
- **Primary:** Quiz completion rate (target: 40%+ of visitors who see quiz)
- **Secondary:** Email capture rate from quiz (target: 25%+ of quiz completers)
- **Tertiary:** Free trial sign-up rate (target: 12-15%)
- **Engagement:** Interaction with quiz/poll elements (target: 50%+ interaction rate)
- **Quality:** Trial-to-paid conversion (expect good quality—users who complete quiz are engaged)
- **Search performance:** Track organic search rankings for question-based queries

### Statistical Significance Needed
- Minimum 250 visitors per variant
- 95% confidence level
- Run for 14 days
- Track engagement with interactive elements separately
- Segment by traffic source (hypothesis: search traffic will love this variant)

## Mobile Optimization Notes

### Critical Elements
1. **Hero Quiz:** Mobile-optimized form—large tap targets, simple radio buttons, easy to complete on phone
2. **Question Sections:** Collapse long sections, show question headline + "Get Answer" CTA
3. **Demo:** Short video (30 seconds) instead of animated GIF (better for mobile)
4. **Interactive Elements:** Ensure quiz and poll work smoothly on mobile (critical for this variant)
5. **CTAs:** Sticky bottom bar with "Get Answers" button

### Mobile-Specific Copy Adjustments
- Hero headline: Keep as-is (question format works well)
- Quiz: Simplify to 3 questions on mobile (vs 4 on desktop)
- Question sections: Show 5 questions on mobile (vs 8 on desktop), "See More Questions" button
- CTA: "Get Answers" (short, clear)

### Performance
- Quiz form: Lightweight, <20KB
- Lazy load question sections below fold
- Optimize demo video for mobile (<5MB)
- Preload quiz interaction (instant response)
