## Partnership Models

**Model 1: Fee-for-Service**
- Non-profit pays annual license fee
- All program participants receive access
- Standard implementation and support

**Model 2: Grant-Funded**
- Specific grant funds platform
- Enhanced reporting for grantor
- Pilot or ongoing program

**Model 3: Sponsor-Funded**
- Corporate or individual sponsor
- Named program opportunity ("Powered by [Sponsor]")
- Strong impact narrative for sponsor recognition

**Model 4: Revenue-Share**
- For non-profits with revenue-generating programs
- Platform access tied to program enrollment
- Flexible payment based on actual usage
