# Best Southeast States for Military Retirees 2025

The Southeast represents America's premier region for military retirement due to exceptional tax treatment, extremely affordable living costs, warm weather, and strong veteran communities. This comprehensive guide identifies and ranks the best Southeast states for retiring service members.

## Top 5 Southeast States for Military Retirees

### 1. FLORIDA - BEST OVERALL FOR MILITARY RETIREES

**Why Florida Leads:**
- Military retirement: 100% exempt from state income tax
- VA disability: 100% exempt
- Property tax: 0.85% (lowest in Southeast)
- Homestead exemption: 50,000-75,000 for disabled
- No state income tax: Covers all income
- Healthcare: Extensive VA network + world-class private medicine

**Key Financial Benefits:**
- $0 state income tax on $45K military pension
- Low property tax rate: ~$2,130/year on $300K home
- Homestead exemptions reduce tax further
- Sales tax: 6-7.5% (varies by county)
- No income tax on military or any income

**Best Cities for Military Retirees:**
- Tampa Bay: Growing economy, VA access, affordable ($330K)
- Jacksonville: Very affordable ($280K), naval base presence, large veteran population
- Southwest Florida: Retirement communities, beaches, affordable ($240-300K)
- Orlando: Central location, growing job market, affordable ($310K)

**Financial Profile (Military Retiree):**
- State income tax: $0
- Property tax: ~$2,130/year (vs $5,400 in Texas)
- Cost of living: Very low
- VA healthcare: Excellent statewide
- **10-year savings vs similar Texas location: $20,000-$30,000**

**Recommendation:** Florida optimal for military retirees prioritizing tax savings and affordability.

---

### 2. TENNESSEE - NO STATE INCOME TAX PLUS AFFORDABILITY

**Why Tennessee Excels:**
- Military retirement: Exempt (effective 0% due to no state income tax)
- No state income tax: On any income source
- Property tax: 0.71% (lowest in Southeast)
- Military culture: Strong throughout state
- Healthcare: Growing VA network
- Cost of living: Very low

**Key Financial Benefits:**
- No state income tax whatsoever
- Lowest property tax in Southeast: ~$2,130/year on $300K home
- Very affordable housing (median $310K)
- Growing job market (especially Nashville tech)
- Military installation connection (Nashville, Memphis)

**Best Cities for Military Retirees:**
- Nashville: Growing tech sector, excellent VA, vibrant culture ($380K)
- Knoxville: Mountain recreation, affordable ($300K), good VA access
- Memphis: Very affordable ($220K), strong VA presence, music culture
- Chattanooga: Outdoor recreation, growing economy, affordable ($280K)

**Financial Profile:**
- State income tax: $0
- Property tax: ~$2,130/year
- Cost of living: 3% lower than Florida
- **15-year savings vs comparable state with income tax: $40,000-$50,000**

**Recommendation:** Tennessee best for retirees on strict budgets or seeking no-income-tax advantage.

### 3. SOUTH CAROLINA - MILITARY RETENTION + AFFORDABILITY

**Why South Carolina Attracts Retirees:**
- Military retirement: 100% exempt
- Property tax: 0.57% (lowest rate Southeast)
- VA disability: Full exemption
- Coastal living: 400+ miles of beaches
- Healthcare: Growing VA network
- Cost of living: Low (2% cheaper than NC)

**Key Financial Benefits:**
- Military retirement completely exempt
- Lowest property tax rate: ~$1,710/year on $300K
- Significant military presence (Fort Jackson, etc.)
- Beach recreation included
- Retiree-focused communities abundant

**Best Cities:**
- Charleston: Historic charm, coastal, moderate cost ($420K)
- Columbia: Very affordable ($280K), capital city amenities, strong VA
- Greenville: Affordable ($310K), vibrant downtown, outdoor recreation
- Myrtle Beach: Beach living, affordable ($320K), retirement-focused

**Recommendation:** SC ideal for those seeking military culture + beach lifestyle + low cost.

### 4. GEORGIA - MILITARY RETIREMENT EXEMPT + GROWTH

## Conclusion

The Southeast is **unquestionably America's best region for military retirees** due to:
- Complete military retirement tax exemptions
- No or minimal state income taxes
- Lowest property tax rates nationally
- Most affordable housing relative to income
- Warmest year-round climate
- Strong established veteran networks
- Excellent and expanding VA healthcare

**Florida and Tennessee emerge as top choices**, offering perfect combinations of tax benefits, affordability, and lifestyle. A military retiree with $45,000 annual pension can live comfortably for $35,000-$36,000 annually in these states, enabling travel, healthcare upgrades, and leisure activities.

**For maximum financial advantage: Choose Tennessee or Florida.**
**For best overall lifestyle: Choose Florida.**
**For active, adventure-focused retirees: Choose Tennessee or North Carolina.**
**For those still working: Choose Georgia or North Carolina.**
