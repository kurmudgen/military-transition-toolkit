## Property Tax Exemptions for Disabled Veterans

**Significant Property Tax Relief:**
- 100% disabled veterans: $200,000 property value exemption
- Service-connected disabled (50%+): $100,000 property value exemption
- Applied to primary residence
- Renewable annually

**Financial Impact:**
New Mexico's effective property tax rate averages 0.80%. A disabled veteran with $300,000 home and $200,000 exemption saves approximately $1,600 annually.

**Application:**
- File with county assessor with VA disability determination
- Annual renewal (simple process)
- Retroactive to filing date
- No income limits

**Example Savings:**
Over a 30-year retirement, a 100% disabled veteran saves $48,000+ in property taxes.
