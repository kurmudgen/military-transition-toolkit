## How to Maximize Your Chances

### 1. Emphasize Your Law Enforcement Background

**Don't write:** "31B Military Police, handled security"

**Do write:** "Law enforcement professional with training and experience in enforcement, investigations, security protocols, and leadership"

### 2. Highlight Key Skills

- Law enforcement and enforcement authority
- Investigation and interview skills
- Security protocols and procedures
- Regulatory compliance and procedures
- Physical training and fitness
- Decision-making and judgment
- Leadership and supervision
- Numbers: "Conducted 500+ enforcement actions," "Supervised 20+ personnel," "Zero incidents in 3 years"

### 3. Consider Certifications

- **POST Certification:** Required for state/local (includes police academy)
- **Security+:** Federal security work
- **Certified Protection Professional:** Private security

### 4. Target Federal Agencies

- Higher pay than state/local police
- Better job security
- Federal pension benefits
- Veterans preference significant advantage
- Clearance continuation valued

### 5. Strategic Application

- Apply to 30-40+ law enforcement agencies
- Target federal positions if you want highest pay
- State/local offer better work-life balance
- Private security pays well for federal contractor work
- Build toward supervisory/management roles
