## How to Maximize Your Chances

### 1. Emphasize Technical Troubleshooting

**Don't write:** "94F Computer Repair, fixed computers"

**Do write:** "Computer repair and IT support professional with expertise in hardware troubleshooting, diagnostics, system optimization, and technical support"

### 2. Highlight Key Skills

- Computer hardware diagnosis and repair
- Operating system installation and configuration
- Network troubleshooting basics
- Customer service and communication
- Technical documentation
- Equipment inventory management
- Software installation and updates
- Numbers: "Repaired 500+ computers," "Resolved 100+ support tickets weekly," "Achieved 98% customer satisfaction"

### 3. Get IT Certifications

- **CompTIA A+:** Foundation IT certification ($200 each exam)
- **CompTIA Security+:** For security roles
- **Microsoft Certification:** Windows/Office certifications
- **Cisco certifications:** Network basics

### 4. Target Growth Companies

- Tech companies pay more than traditional service providers
- Better benefits and work environment
- Faster advancement opportunities
- Stock options at some companies
- More challenging technical work

### 5. Strategic Application

- Apply to 20-25+ IT support companies
- Target both technician and manager positions
- Consider corporate IT for better benefits
- Look for advancement to manager/director roles
- Emphasize customer service and technical skills
