## How to Maximize Your Chances

### 1. Translate Your 25B Experience Effectively

**Don't write:** "25B IT Specialist, managed network equipment"

**Do write:** "IT infrastructure professional managing enterprise network systems, security protocols, and technical troubleshooting for mission-critical operations across 500+ user network environment"

### 2. Highlight Specific Technical Skills

- Network administration and troubleshooting
- System administration and configuration
- Security protocols and compliance
- Cloud infrastructure understanding
- Virtualization and storage systems
- Ticketing system and technical documentation
- Windows/Linux system management
- Numbers: "Managed 500+ IT assets," "Resolved 100+ support tickets weekly," "Maintained 99.9% network uptime"

### 3. Get Industry Certifications

- **CompTIA A+:** Entry-level, quick (1-2 weeks), covers your background
- **CompTIA Network+:** Valuable for network-focused roles
- **Microsoft certifications:** Azure, Windows, Office 365 (most valued by employers)
- **Cisco certifications:** CCNA for network-heavy roles
- **Security+:** If cybersecurity interested
- **GI Bill covers exam costs**

### 4. Emphasize Security Clearance

If you have active TS/SCI or Secret clearance:
- Put prominently in profile and resume
- Mention in applications
- Worth $10K-$30K salary premium to cleared employers
- Booz Allen, General Dynamics, Lockheed Martin prioritize clearance holders

### 5. Strategic Job Search

- Apply to 20-30+ positions across multiple companies
- Target roles leveraging your specific experience
- Follow up 1 week after application
- Prepare for technical interviews: Discuss your systems knowledge
- Emphasize reliability, security mindset, and operational excellence

### 6. Consider Geographic Flexibility

- Tech hubs pay premium: Bay Area, Seattle, Boston, NYC ($100K-$200K+)
- Medium cities have good opportunities: Austin, Denver, Charlotte
- Remote positions increasingly available from these top companies
- Consider cost of living vs. salary
