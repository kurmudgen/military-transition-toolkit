# 11B Infantry Veteran: Salaries by Region & Specialization

## BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)

11B Infantry Veterans transition to security, federal contracting, and management roles with significant earning potential. Entry-level security positions: $50,000-$70,000 nationally. Federal contracting (Washington DC area): $100,000-$150,000+. Texas/Austin tech-adjacent roles: $75,000-$120,000 with no state income tax. Management pathways reach $120,000-$200,000+ within 5 years. DC metro offers highest federal contracting opportunity ($120,000-$200,000), while Texas metros offer better after-tax value ($90,000-$150,000 net advantage). Strategic role selection and location choice impact 20-year earnings by $800,000-$1,500,000+.

---

## Career Pathways & Salary Ranges

### National Baseline (Non-Specialized Roles)

- Security officer entry: $50,000-$65,000
- Logistics coordinator: $55,000-$75,000
- Construction supervisor: $65,000-$85,000
- Facility manager: $70,000-$95,000
- National average cost of living: Baseline (100%)

---

## Tier 1: Federal Contracting (DC Metro - Highest Paying)

### Washington DC / Northern Virginia / Maryland

**Security Manager (federal contractor)**
- Salary: $110,000-$160,000
- Requirements: 5+ years security, TS clearance
- Companies: BAH, SAIC, Deloitte, ManTech
- Overtime/bonus: +$15,000-$40,000

**Program Manager (federal contractor)**
- Salary: $100,000-$150,000
- Requirements: PMP or equivalent, leadership
- Federal advantage: +$30,000-$50,000 vs. civilian

**Operations Specialist (federal)**
- Salary: $85,000-$130,000
- GS advancement path to $150,000+
- Benefits: Federal pension, health insurance
- 20-year pension: ~$80,000/year for life

**Cost of Living:** +25-30% vs. national

**5-Year Earning Potential:** $525,000-$750,000
**After-tax (accounting for cost): $400,000-$550,000**

**Why High Salaries:**
- Clearance holders demand premium
- Defense contractor concentration
- Government agency hiring
- Federal benefits attract employers
- Senior leadership opportunities

## Conclusion

11B Veterans should consider federal contracting path for highest earning potential. DC metro offers highest nominal salaries but costs are substantial. Texas metros (Austin, Dallas) offer superior after-tax value with no state income tax. Strategic 3-5 year federal contracting career followed by relocation to lower-cost area maximizes lifetime wealth.

**Key Takeaway:** Federal contracting in DC: $525,000-$750,000 (5-year). Austin/Dallas: $420,000-$580,000 (net, after-tax). Career path and location choice impact 30-year earnings by $1,000,000-$2,000,000+.
