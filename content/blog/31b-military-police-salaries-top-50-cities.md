# 31B Military Police: Top 50 Cities Salary Analysis

## BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)

31B Military Police earn highest salaries in major cities with large police departments and federal law enforcement presence. New York/Los Angeles/Chicago officers: $70,000-$100,000+ base, advancing to $95,000-$150,000+ with specialization. San Francisco/Seattle: $85,000-$115,000+ (highest cost of living offset). Washington DC/Federal law enforcement: $85,000-$140,000+ with federal benefits advantage. Texas cities (Dallas, Houston, Austin): $65,000-$95,000+ with no state income tax (net advantage). Strategic city selection impacts 20-year earnings by $700,000-$1,400,000+ accounting for overtime, benefits, and cost-of-living.

## Strategic Recommendations

**Highest Total Earning:** New York, Los Angeles, San Francisco (but cost-of-living reduces net benefit)

**Best Net Earning:** Texas cities (Dallas, Houston, Austin) with no state income tax advantage

**Best Career Advancement:** Major cities (NYC, LA, DC) with detective/supervisor opportunity

**Best Quality of Life/Earning:** Austin, Denver, Atlanta (growing markets, reasonable costs)

---

## Conclusion

31B Military Police should prioritize Texas cities for best net earnings after cost-of-living and taxes. Major cities (NY, LA) offer highest nominal salaries but costs offset benefit significantly. DC federal law enforcement combines strong salary with federal benefits advantage. Strategic city selection impacts 20-year earnings by $700,000-$1,400,000+.

**Key Takeaway:** Texas cities net: $380,000-$510,000 (5-year after cost/tax). NYC net: $300,000-$425,000 (after cost). DC federal: $550,000-$800,000 (with benefits value). Strategic city selection critical.
