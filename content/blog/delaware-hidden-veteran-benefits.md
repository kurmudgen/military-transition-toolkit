# Delaware Hidden Veteran Benefits: Tax Breaks and Exclusive Programs 2025

Delaware may be small in size, but it's significant in veteran support. This First State offers an often-overlooked collection of tax breaks, employment advantages, and financial assistance programs specifically designed for transitioning service members. Understanding these hidden benefits can result in substantial savings and career advancement opportunities for Delaware's veteran population.

## Delaware Property Tax Exemption for Disabled Veterans

Delaware provides one of the most valuable hidden benefits for disabled veterans:

**Complete Property Tax Exemption:**
- 100% disabled veterans (VA rating): full exemption on homestead property
- Service-connected disabled (50%+): exemption up to $12,000 of assessed value
- Both residential and agricultural property eligible
- Unlimited exemption for 100% disabled veterans

**Financial Impact:**
Delaware's effective property tax rate averages 0.57%. A veteran homeowner with a $300,000 property receives $1,710 annual savings with the 100% exemption. Over 30 years of ownership, this totals $51,300 in tax savings.

**Application Process:**
- File with county assessor along with disability documentation
- Renewal every 2 years
- Simple online filing available
- Processing typically 30-60 days

## Delaware Veteran Employment Preference

**Significant Civil Service Advantages:**
- 5-point hiring preference for all state positions
- 10-point preference for disabled veterans
- Applied to approximately 25,000 state jobs
- Extends to municipal and county positions

**How It Works:**
Veteran candidates with equal qualifications are ranked ahead of non-veterans. This is particularly valuable in competitive hiring situations.

**Career Impact:**
Delaware's government sector includes high-paying positions:
- Average state employee salary: $52,000-$65,000
- Professional positions: $65,000-$85,000
- Administrative positions with veteran preference: $55,000-$75,000

## Healthcare Benefits & Facilities

**Extended Healthcare Benefits:**
