## How to Maximize Your Chances

### 1. Emphasize Aircraft Maintenance Knowledge

**Don't write:** "15T Aviation Mechanic, maintained helicopters"

**Do write:** "Aircraft maintenance professional with expertise in helicopter/aircraft systems, A&P certification knowledge, component replacement, and aircraft airworthiness"

### 2. Highlight Key Skills

- Aircraft systems knowledge
- Mechanical and electrical systems
- Maintenance procedures and documentation
- Quality assurance and inspections
- Troubleshooting and diagnostics
- Equipment operation and safety
- Numbers: "Performed 500+ maintenance events," "Maintained 99.8% aircraft readiness," "Zero safety incidents"

### 3. Pursue A&P Certification

- **Critical for advancement** to higher pay
- Test-out option available (military experience qualifies)
- Exam: $340 if military qualified
- Can get A&P while working
- Increases earning potential $5K-$15K

### 4. Target Major Airlines

- Delta, United, American, Southwest pay significantly more
- Union representation standard at major airlines
- Excellent benefits and job security
- Clear advancement to lead mechanic/supervisor

### 5. Strategic Application

- Apply to 15-20+ companies
- Target both airline and maintenance facility positions
- Consider military contractor positions (good pay, familiar environment)
- Build toward lead mechanic and supervisor roles
- Look for companies with A&P advancement support
