## How to Maximize Your Chances

### 1. Emphasize Signal/Telecom Knowledge

**Don't write:** "25U Signal Support, installed equipment"

**Do write:** "Telecommunications infrastructure professional with expertise in signal systems installation, maintenance, network troubleshooting, and technical operations"

### 2. Highlight Key Skills

- Signal systems installation and maintenance
- Telecommunications infrastructure knowledge
- Network installation and troubleshooting
- Field equipment operation and repair
- Technical documentation and compliance
- Safety protocols and procedures
- Cable and fiber operations
- Numbers: "Installed 500+ signal systems," "Maintained 100+ network nodes," "Reduced downtime 30%"

### 3. Get Industry Certifications

- **CompTIA Network+:** Network knowledge foundation
- **Cisco CCENT/CCNA:** Networking certifications
- **Telecommunications certifications:** Provider-specific
- **Safety certifications:** OSHA 10-hour for field work

### 4. Target Growth Sectors

- 5G deployment creating massive job growth
- Broadband expansion in rural areas
- Fiber optic deployment nationwide
- Infrastructure modernization ongoing

### 5. Strategic Application

- Apply to 20-30+ telecom companies
- Target both field technician and engineering roles
- Consider government contractor positions (higher pay)
- Look for advancement to management
- Build toward senior engineer/manager roles
