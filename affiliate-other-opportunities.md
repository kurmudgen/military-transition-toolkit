## ETHICAL CONSIDERATIONS

### Mental Health Services:
✅ **DO:**
- Feature free VA mental health resources first
- Disclose affiliate relationships
- Present paid options as supplements, not replacements
- Include crisis resources (988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Hide free VA resources
- Make mental health seem like emergency to drive sales
- Prioritize commissions over appropriate care

### Legal Services:
✅ **DO:**
- Explain when LegalZoom-type services are appropriate
- Explain when real attorney is needed
- Provide both options
