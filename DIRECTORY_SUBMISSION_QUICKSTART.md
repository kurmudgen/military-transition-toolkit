Add UTM parameters to track traffic from each directory:

## üí° Pro Tips for Maximum Impact

### Before Submitting

1. **Test your site**
   - Ensure all features work flawlessly
   - Test on mobile and desktop
   - Fix any bugs or broken links

2. **Prepare social proof**
   - Gather testimonials if you have users
   - Prepare case study or success story
   - Have demo video ready (optional but helpful)

3. **Set up analytics**
   - Google Analytics or alternative
   - UTM tracking configured
   - Conversion goals set up

### During Submission

4. **Product Hunt specific:**
   - Launch on Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday (best engagement)
   - Schedule for 12:01 AM PST (Pacific time)
   - Post your first comment immediately when live
   - Respond to every comment quickly
   - Share to Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.
   - Ask friends/colleagues to upvote (ethically - no voting rings)

5. **General directories:**
   - Use consistent descriptions across platforms (builds recognition)
   - Choose most relevant categories
   - Max out tags/keywords (for discoverability)
   - Add as many screenshots as allowed
   - Fill optional fields (completeness helps rankings)

### After Submission

6. **Engage with community**
   - Respond to questions and comments
   - Join relevant discussions
   - Help others in the community
   - Build relationships, not just links

7. **Update listings**
   - Keep information current
   - Update screenshots when UI improves
   - Add new features as you launch them
   - Monitor and respond to reviews (G2, Capterra)

8. **Request reviews** (for review platforms)
   - Ask satisfied users for G2/Capterra reviews
   - Make it easy (direct link)
   - Incentivize if allowed by platform
   - Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)

9. **Analyze results**
   - Track traffic from each directory
   - Identify top performers
   - Adjust strategy based on data
   - Consider paid promotions on top performers

## üõë Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Content Mistakes

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Copy-paste generic marketing fluff
- Use excessive exclamation marks!!!
- Oversell or make unverifiable claims
- Forget to proofread
- Submit with broken links
- Use Lorem Ipsum or test data

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Write authentic, specific descriptions
- Focus on real value and problems solved
- Use concrete examples
- Proofread everything
- Test all links before submitting
- Use realistic screenshots

### Technical Mistakes

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Submit while site has bugs
- Upload low-resolution images
- Ignore image size requirements
- Submit to wrong categories
- Forget to fill optional fields

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Test thoroughly before launching
- Use optimized, high-quality images
- Follow exact dimension specs
- Choose most relevant categories
- Fill all fields, including optional ones

### Timing Mistakes

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Launch Product Hunt on weekends
- Submit to all 15 on same day (overwhelming)
- Forget to schedule Product Hunt strategically
- Ignore time zones (PH is PST)

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Launch Product Hunt Tuesday-Thursday
- Spread submissions over 2-4 weeks
- Schedule PH for 12:01 AM PST
- Plan around your availability to engage
