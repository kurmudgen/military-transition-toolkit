### Core Product Details

**Product Name:** Military Transition Toolkit

**URL:** https://militarytransitiontoolkit.com

**Tagline (60 chars):** Free toolkit helping service members transition to civilian life

**Short Description (140 chars):**
Comprehensive platform for military transition - VA claims, career planning, financial tools, and personalized checklists. Built by veterans for veterans.

**Medium Description (280 chars):**
Military Transition Toolkit helps 200,000+ annual transitioning service members navigate the military-to-civilian journey. Features include VA claims builder, retirement calculators, state benefits comparison, resume builder, job tracking, and 24-month personalized checklists. Free forever, built by veterans.

**Long Description (500-1000 words):**

Military Transition Toolkit is a comprehensive web application designed to help service members successfully navigate the complex transition from military to civilian life. Built by veterans who have personally experienced the challenges of transition, MTT provides a centralized platform that combines planning tools, financial calculators, VA benefits guidance, and career resources.

**The Problem We Solve:**
Each year, approximately 200,000 service members separate from military service. The transition process is notoriously complex, with critical deadlines scattered across multiple agencies, confusing benefits programs, and high-stakes decisions about healthcare, location, and career paths. Veterans frequently miss deadlines, leave thousands in unclaimed VA benefits on the table, and feel overwhelmed by the sheer volume of conflicting information.

**Our Solution:**
Military Transition Toolkit provides a single, organized platform that guides service members through every aspect of transition:

**VA Claims Builder** - Our signature feature helps veterans identify medical conditions, track required evidence, generate personal statements, and estimate the financial impact of their claims. The builder organizes conditions by body system and provides templates for buddy statements and medical documentation.

**Personalized Checklists** - Based on individual circumstances (20+ year retirement, medical board/IDES separation, or early separation), MTT provides customized task lists covering the entire 24-month pre-separation timeline. Users track progress and receive reminders for critical deadlines.

**Financial Planning Tools:**
- Military retirement pay calculators (both High-3 and BRS systems)
- State benefits comparison across all 50 states
- VA disability compensation estimators
- Terminal leave calculator
- Side-by-side scenario planning for relocation decisions

**Career Transition Support:**
- Resume builder with military-to-civilian translation
- Job search tracker with saved positions
- Application and interview tracking
- Offer comparison tools

**Timeline & Appointment Management:**
- Centralized appointment tracking
- Medical appointment reminders
- PEBLO and TAP meeting scheduling
- MedBoard process phase tracking

**Resource Library:**
- 60+ curated resources for VA benefits, healthcare, education, and employment
- Partnership with training providers, financial services, and education platforms
- Blog with transition guides and state-specific benefits information

**Who We Serve:**
- Active duty service members within 24 months of separation/retirement
- Veterans navigating medical board (IDES/MEB) processes
- Service members separating before 20 years (ETS)
- Recently separated veterans optimizing their VA claims

**Our Mission:**
We are committed to keeping MTT free forever through partnership revenue rather than user fees. Core transition features remain accessible to all service members regardless of financial circumstances. Our business model relies on partnerships with veteran-serving organizations (training providers, financial advisors, education platforms) rather than charging veterans during their most vulnerable transition period.

**Technology:**
Built with modern web technologies (React, Tailwind CSS, Supabase), MTT offers cloud sync for data backup while respecting user privacy with local-first storage. The platform works on desktop and mobile devices and includes PDF export capabilities for offline access to transition plans.

**Impact:**
By providing organized, actionable guidance during the critical transition period, MTT helps service members:
- Maximize their VA disability compensation
- Meet critical deadlines and avoid missing benefits
- Make informed decisions about relocation and career paths
- Successfully translate military experience to civilian opportunities
- Navigate complex medical separation processes with confidence

Military Transition Toolkit represents what's possible when veterans build solutions for veterans - practical, comprehensive, and genuinely focused on the success of those who served.

- Schedule Product Hunt launch for Tuesday-Thursday (best engagement days)

- Have clear, professional screenshots
- Write original descriptions (don't copy-paste marketing fluff)

- Follow each directory's specific guidelines

- Product Hunt: Instant (self-publish on schedule)

- Most others: 1-7 days
