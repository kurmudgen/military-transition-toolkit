## Case Study Format

Each case study follows standardized structure (2,000-3,000 words):

1. **Quick Facts** - Branch, rank, MOS, outcome, timeline snapshot
2. **Background Story** - Military career and challenge faced
3. **The Strategy** - Month-by-month action plan with tools/resources
4. **The Results** - Detailed outcomes with financial impact
5. **Key Tactics** - 5-7 specific tactics explained in detail
6. **Obstacles Overcome** - 3-5 major challenges and solutions
7. **What They'd Do Differently** - Hindsight lessons
8. **Timeline Breakdown** - Visual timeline with critical dates
9. **Advice for Others** - Who it works for and getting started
10. **Current Situation** - 18+ months later update
11. **Key Takeaways** - 5 main lessons to remember
12. **Resources Mentioned** - Links to tools, organizations, reading

## Writing Standards

All case studies adhere to these requirements:

- **Authenticity:** Stories feel real with mistakes and setbacks included
- **Detail Level:** Specific numbers (salaries, savings, costs, timelines)
- **Inspiration + Practicality:** Motivating but realistic with tactical lessons
- **Voice:** Story-driven, conversational, emphasis on tactics over motivation
- **Structure:** Scannable with headers, bullets, bold text
- **Research:** Minimum 5-8 web searches per story for realistic data
