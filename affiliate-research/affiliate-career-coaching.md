## INDUSTRY LANDSCAPE

### Key Finding:
The veteran career coaching industry is **dominated by nonprofit organizations** offering FREE services. Traditional affiliate programs are rare because most services are government-funded or donation-supported.

### Alternative Monetization Strategies:
1. **Professional Certification Programs** - Become a certified veteran career coach yourself
2. **B2B Partnerships** - Partner with paid services that work alongside free programs
3. **Complementary Services** - Resume writing, LinkedIn optimization, interview coaching
4. **Educational Products** - Create your own courses for veterans

## CONTENT STRATEGY FOR CAREER COACHING

### Recommended Approach:

Since most veteran career coaching is FREE, position yourself as:

1. **Comprehensive Resource Hub**
   - List ALL free options (builds trust)
   - Explain when free vs. paid makes sense
   - Provide decision framework

2. **When to Use Free vs. Paid Services:**

**Use FREE nonprofit coaching when:**
- Early career/enlisted transition
- Clear career path in mind
- Straightforward transition
- Budget-conscious

**Consider PAID coaching when:**
- Executive/senior officer transition
- Career change (not just industry transition)
- Need specialized expertise (tech, healthcare, etc.)
- Time-sensitive transition
- Failed with free services
- Complex situations (disability + career transition)

3. **Monetize Complementary Services:**
   - Resume writing (better affiliate opportunity)
   - LinkedIn optimization
   - Interview coaching
   - Professional certifications
   - Skills training

4. **Create Your Own Products:**
   - "Executive Officer Transition Guide" - $97
   - "Military Spouse Remote Career Toolkit" - $47
   - "LinkedIn Mastery for Veterans" - $67
   - Group coaching programs

## RECOMMENDATIONS

### DO:
✅ Prominently feature FREE nonprofit options (builds massive trust)
✅ Create your own educational products (highest margin)
✅ Focus on complementary services (resume, LinkedIn) for affiliate income
✅ Position paid services for specific situations (executive transition, complex cases)
✅ Consider getting certified yourself (CVCS)

### DON'T:
❌ Hide free options to push paid services
❌ Rely solely on career coaching affiliates (limited opportunities)
❌ Ignore the military spouse market
❌ Overlook executive officer niche (higher budgets)

### PRIORITIZE:
1. **Resume writing affiliates** - Better commission than coaching alone
2. **Create your own products** - No competition with free services
3. **CVCS certification** - Either become certified or refer others
4. **Executive transition niche** - Higher budgets, less competition from nonprofits
5. **Military spouse market** - Underserved, loyal audience
