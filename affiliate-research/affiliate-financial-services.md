## MILITARY BANKS & CREDIT UNIONS

### 1. Navy Federal Credit Union

#### **Organization Information:**
- **Website:** https://www.navyfederal.org
- **Type:** Credit union (member-owned)
- **Eligibility:** Military members, veterans, DoD civilians, family members
- **Branches:** 350+ worldwide
- **ATMs:** 30,000+ fee-free ATMs

#### **Referral Program:** ✅ YES - Senior Enlisted Reward Program
- **Bonus:** $100 per senior NCO referral
- **Limit:** 5 referrals per member ($500 maximum)
- **Payment:** Deposited within 14 business days
- **Eligibility:** Referring member must be Senior NCO

#### **Additional Promotions:**
- New member bonuses (varies by promotion)
- Example: $200 bonus for new 12-month Special EasyStart Share certificate

#### **Services:**
- Banking (checking, savings)
- Credit cards
- Mortgages and home loans
- Auto loans
- Personal loans
- Investment services

#### **Customer Rating:** 4.5/5 on Trustpilot (29,000+ reviews)

#### **Affiliate Strategy:**
- **Challenge:** Referral program limited to members and Senior NCOs
- **Opportunity:** Content partnerships, promotional bonuses
- **Recommendation:** MEDIUM PRIORITY - Limited but legitimate referral program
