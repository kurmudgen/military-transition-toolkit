## RISK MITIGATION & COMPLIANCE

### Legal/Regulatory Considerations:

**FTC Disclosure Requirements:**
- ‚úÖ Clear affiliate relationship disclosure on all affiliate content
- ‚úÖ Disclosure at beginning of content, not buried in footer
- ‚úÖ Clear language: "We may earn a commission if you purchase through our links"

**VA Claims Compliance:**
- ‚úÖ ONLY partner with VA-accredited attorneys/agents for claims assistance
- ‚úÖ Verify accreditation independently
- ‚úÖ Feature free VSO options prominently

**Mental Health Ethics:**
- ‚úÖ Feature free VA mental health resources first
- ‚úÖ Include crisis resources (988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
- ‚úÖ Present paid therapy as supplement, not replacement for VA care

**Military-Specific Considerations:**
- ‚úÖ Accurate information about GI Bill, VA benefits, etc.
- ‚úÖ Don't make guarantees about VA ratings, loan approvals, job placements, etc.
- ‚úÖ Maintain editorial independence (honest reviews, even of affiliate partners)

---

### Partnership Risks:

**Quality Control:**
- Vet all partners thoroughly
- Read reviews and check BBB ratings
- Test services when possible
- Monitor customer feedback

**Revenue Concentration:**
- Don't rely on single affiliate partner
- Diversify across categories
- Have backup partners in each category

- Have multiple partners per category
