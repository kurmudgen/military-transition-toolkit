## SBA Veteran Business Programs

### Boots to Business (B2B)
- **Amount**: FREE (No cost to participants)
- **Type**: Entrepreneurship education and training
- **Eligibility**:
  - Veterans of all eras
  - Active Duty Service Members (including National Guard and Reserve)
  - Military spouses
- **Details**: Two-day course offered as part of TAP (Transition Assistance Program)
- **Variants**:
  - Boots to Business: On military installations worldwide
  - Boots to Business Reboot: For veterans in communities
  - Revenue Readiness: Business model development
- **Impact**: 236,000+ graduates since 2013
- **URL**: https://www.sba.gov/business-guide/grow-your-business/veteran-owned-businesses

### SBIR/STTR Grants (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Amount**:
  - Phase I: Up to $200,000 (6 months - 1 year)
  - Phase II: Up to $750,000 (2 years)
- **Type**: Non-dilutive funding for tech innovation and research
- **Eligibility**: Small businesses focused on technological innovation
- **Deadline**: Various by agency (DoD, DOE, HHS, etc.)
- **Note**: Congressional authorization expired Sept. 30, 2025 - check current status
- **URL**: https://www.sbir.gov/

### Veterans Business Outreach Centers (VBOCs)
- **Amount**: FREE counseling and training
- **Type**: Business counseling and resource connections
- **Details**: SBA announced $1M+ in 2024 for new VBOCs in OH, PA, and PR
- **Services**: Training, counseling, mentorship
- **URL**: https://www.sba.gov/local-assistance/find/?type=Veterans%20Business%20Outreach%20Center&pageNumber=1

## Corporate Programs

### Amazon Web Services (AWS)

#### AWS Activate Program
- **Amount**: $1,000 - $100,000 in AWS credits
- **Type**: Cloud computing credits + technical support
- **Tiers**:
  - Founders Tier: For self-funded/bootstrapped startups
  - Portfolio Tier: For VC/accelerator-backed startups
- **Additional Benefits**: Technical support, training resources
- **Note**: No veteran-specific program identified, but open to all eligible startups
- **URL**: https://aws.amazon.com/startups

#### AWS Generative AI Accelerator
- **Amount**: Up to $1M in credits
- **Type**: 8-week global program
- **Eligibility**: Late-Seed to Series A startups building generative AI
- **Selection**: 40 top startups annually
- **Benefits**: Credits, mentorship, investor access
- **URL**: https://aws.amazon.com/startups/programs

#### AWS AI Credits (High-Tier)
- **Amount**: Up to $300,000 in credits
- **Type**: For foundation AI models
- **Eligibility**: Startups working on compute-heavy AI workloads
- **URL**: https://aws.amazon.com/startups

### Google for Startups

#### Veterans Programs
- **Amount**: Various (no specific grant amounts listed)
- **Type**: Training, mentorship, partnerships
- **Programs**:
  - Google Launchpad for Veterans (AI training starting Nov 13-14, 2025)
  - Partnership with Patriot Boot Camp, Bunker Labs, Atlas
  - Veterans Career Week (virtual, no-cost)
  - SkillBridge Program (12-week fellowship, 200+ fellows supported)
- **Recent Impact**:
  - Trained 4,000+ service members in 2024
  - Google.org: $50M+ to veteran nonprofits
  - 2025: $125k grants to Beneath the Service, SOTERIA, VetsinTech, VetSec
- **URL**: https://blog.google/outreach-initiatives/entrepreneurs/veterans-military-spouses/
- **Additional**: https://grow.google/veteranled/

### Microsoft for Startups (formerly Founders Hub)

#### Microsoft for Startups Program
- **Amount**: Up to $150,000 in Azure credits (over 4 years)
- **Type**: Cloud computing credits + tools
- **Additional Benefits**: Up to 20 seats of GitHub Enterprise (1 year)
- **Eligibility**:
  - Two paths: Investor-backed startups OR early-stage (no funding required)
  - Open to all founders regardless of background
- **Note**: Program renamed from "Founders Hub" as of July 2, 2025
- **Veteran-Specific**: No specific veteran programs identified in search
- **URL**: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/startups

## Veteran Startup Accelerators

### Bunker Labs CEOcircle
- **Amount**: No direct grant (high-value mentorship program)
- **Type**: 13-month accelerator program
- **Eligibility**:
  - Veteran and military spouse CEOs/executives
  - Driving $5M+ investment OR $1M+ revenue
  - Companies at similar stage/revenue level
- **Program Structure**:
  - 9 virtual huddles
  - 4 in-person fly-in forums
  - Runs November to November
- **2025 Status**: Cohort already selected from 170+ companies
- **Next Application**: June - August 2025 for 2026 cohort
- **Partners**: JPMorgan Chase Commercial Banking, IVMF Syracuse
- **Average Revenue**: $20M of selected participants
- **URL**: https://ivmf.syracuse.edu/programs/entrepreneurship/bunker-labs/ceocircle/

### VetsinTech

#### Startup Pitch Competition
- **Amount**: $25,000 (grand prize)
- **Type**: Annual startup competition (5th year in 2025)
- **Eligibility**: U.S. military veterans and spouses (early-stage startups)
- **2025 Winner**: Quivly AI (August 12, 2025)
- **Details**: Recognizes and accelerates veteran-founded startups
- **URL**: https://vetsintech.co

#### VetsinTech Programs
- **Type**: Training, mentorship, hackathons
- **Programs**:
  - Startup bootcamps
  - VIT SUN (Startup Network) with J.P. Morgan
  - VetVC and VetCap (Capital for Veterans)
  - Industry-aligned training
- **Network**: 120,000+ vets, 25 chapters nationwide
- **Focus**: Tech-based education, employment, entrepreneurship
- **URL**: https://vetsintech.co

### Warrior Rising

#### Grant Program
- **Amount**: Up to $20,000 in funding and training support
- **Type**: Business grants for veterans who complete training
- **Eligibility**:
  - U.S. military veterans and their families
  - Must complete Warrior Academy training first
  - Must provide proof of service (DD214 or Gov ID)
  - For-profit ventures at any stage
- **Cost**: FREE (no cost with proof of service)
- **Application**: Submit pitch deck and video
- **URL**: https://www.warriorrising.org/

#### Training Programs
- **Warrior Academy**: Entry-level business training (prerequisite)
- **Warrior University**: 8-week virtual accelerator (advanced)
- **Capital Readiness**: Enhanced capability for veteran entrepreneurs
- **URL**: https://www.warriorrising.org/programs-warrior-academy/

### V-WISE (Veteran Women Igniting the Spirit of Entrepreneurship)

#### Program Structure
- **Amount**: $75 registration fee (covers course + conference)
- **Type**: Training program for women veterans and military spouses
- **Format**:
  - 4-week online course (20+ modules)
  - 3-5 hours/week commitment
  - Weekly live facilitated office hours
  - 3-day in-person capstone conference
- **Included**: 2 nights lodging + most meals during conference
- **2025 Location**: Atlanta, GA (capstone conference)
- **Eligibility**:
  - Active-duty women
  - Honorably discharged women veterans
  - Military spouses/partners
  - New business idea or recently started business
- **Administrator**: Syracuse University IVMF
- **URL**: https://ivmf.syracuse.edu/programs/entrepreneurship/start-up/v-wise/

## Non-Dilutive Funding Programs

### Stephen L. Tadlock Veteran Grant
- **Amount**: $1,000 microgrants
- **Recipients**: 20-25 veteran-owned small businesses annually
- **Type**: Non-dilutive grant
- **Administrator**: Founders First CDC
- **Eligibility**: Veteran-owned small businesses
- **URL**: https://foundersfirstcdc.org/stephen-tadlock

### Farmer Veteran Fellowship Fund
- **Amount**: $1,000 - $5,000 (can apply for less than $1,000)
- **Type**: Equipment/resource grants via third-party vendors
- **Eligibility**:
  - Must be FVC (Farmer Veteran Coalition) member
  - Honorable discharge from U.S. Military
  - Beginning years of farming/ranching
  - For-profit agricultural businesses (nonprofits lower priority)
  - Previous recipients only eligible if awarded $1,000 or less
- **Application Period**: Opens January 2026 (closed Feb 14, 2025)
- **Award Notifications**: May (announced May 1, 2025)
- **Timeline**: 6 months to use award with approved vendor
- **Requirements**: Business plan, proof of service, essay responses
- **URL**: https://farmvetco.org/fvfellowship/

### NASE Growth Grants
- **Amount**: Up to $4,000
- **Type**: Business growth grants
- **Eligibility**: Self-employed veterans
- **URL**: (Search for National Association for the Self-Employed)
