[MAIN CONTENT]

**Section 1: Market Value (60 seconds)**

**Entry:** $60,000-$80,000/year
**Mid-level:** $80,000-$120,000/year
**Senior/Contractor:** $100,000-$173,000/year

Top contractor positions at CACI: $82,700-$173,900. Average EOD specialist: $90,664/year.

**Section 2: Federal Law Enforcement (90 seconds)**

**FBI Bomb Technician:** $73,000-$110,000/year. Investigate bombing incidents, disarm devices, train agents. Requires EOD certification.

**ATF Explosive Specialist:** $73,000-$105,000/year. Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms & Explosives. Investigate bombings, illegal explosives.

**Secret Service:** Explosive detection, presidential protection. $70,000-$110,000/year.

**TSA/DHS:** Aviation security, bomb detection. $55,000-$85,000/year.

**Section 3: Defense Contractors (120 seconds)**

**CACI:** EOD contractor positions $82K-$173K. Overseas and CONUS. TS/SCI clearance = premium pay.

**Other top contractors:** Booz Allen Hamilton, Leidos, SAIC, Amentum. Range: $90K-$150K+ for experienced EOD.

**Overseas EOD contracting:** $120,000-$180,000/year tax-free in combat zones. Away from family 6-12 months. High risk = high pay.

**Training roles:** EOD instructor for DoD: $85,000-$130,000/year. Teach military EOD students.

**Section 4: Public Safety EOD (90 seconds)**

**Police/Sheriff Bomb Squad:** $70,000-$110,000/year. Major city departments. NYPD, LAPD, Chicago need bomb techs. Government pension + benefits.

**Fire Department Hazmat/Bomb:** $75,000-$100,000/year. Combined firefighter/bomb tech. Excellent benefits.

**Airport/Port Authority:** $65,000-$95,000/year. Security for transportation hubs.

**Private Sector Security:** Corporate/event security with bomb detection. $60,000-$90,000/year.

**Section 5: Required Certifications (60 seconds)**

**Maintain:** DOD EOD certification, FBI Hazardous Devices School (some positions), State bomb technician license (varies).

**Additional value:** Hazmat certifications, Post-Blast Investigation, Render Safe courses.

Most certifications free through DoD COOL or employer-provided training.

**Section 6: Alternative Paths (60 seconds)**

**Industrial Explosives:** Mining, construction, demolition. Blasting supervisor: $70,000-$100,000/year. Requires blaster's license.

**Ordnance & Explosives Safety:** Military bases, ammunition plants. $75,000-$110,000/year. Inspect, certify safe handling.

**K9 Handler (Explosive Detection):** TSA, police, private security. $50,000-$75,000/year. Work with bomb-sniffing dogs.

**Training/Consulting:** Private EOD training companies, consulting for events/productions. Variable income, $75,000-$150,000+.
