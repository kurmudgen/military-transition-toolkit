**Section 4: Certifications (Career Multipliers) (90 seconds)**

[Visual suggestion: Cert path]

**Essential certifications:**

**CompTIA Network+:** $300
- Entry networking cert
- Salary impact: $8K-$12K
- Free through DoD COOL

**Cisco CCNA:** $300
- Industry standard
- Salary impact: $15K-$25K
- Free through Onward to Opportunity

**CompTIA Security+:** $425
- Required for DoD contractors
- Salary impact: $10K-$18K
- Free through DoD COOL

**Advanced certs:**
- CCNP (Cisco advanced): $1,200, but $25K+ impact
- JNCIA (Juniper): For enterprise networks
- Wireless certifications (CWNA): $395

**Get these FREE through:**
- DoD COOL (while in or 180 days after)
- GI Bill reimbursement
- Onward to Opportunity program
