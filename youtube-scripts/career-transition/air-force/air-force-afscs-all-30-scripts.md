## 3D0X2 - Cyber Systems Operations

**Civilian careers:** Cybersecurity analyst ($80K-$130K), SOC analyst ($75K-$120K), Systems administrator ($70K-$100K), Network security engineer ($85K-$135K)

**Best path:** Cybersecurity with Security+/CISSP certs, defense contractors with clearance pay premium

**Top employers:** Defense contractors (cleared $95K-$150K), NSA, Northrop Grumman, Booz Allen, tech companies

**Certifications:** Security+, CISSP, CEH through DoD COOL
