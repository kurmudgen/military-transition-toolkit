# Navy Ratings - All 30 Career Transition Scripts

Complete career guidance for top 30 Navy ratings transitioning to civilian careers.

---

## IT - Information Systems Technician

**Civilian careers:** Network administrator ($65K-$95K), Cybersecurity analyst ($80K-$130K), Systems administrator ($70K-$100K), IT support ($55K-$80K)

**Best path:** Cybersecurity or network admin with Security+/CCNA certs (free DoD COOL)

**Top employers:** Defense contractors (cleared positions $90K-$140K), Microsoft, Amazon AWS, tech companies

**Certifications:** Security+, CCNA, CISSP through DoD COOL

---

**Best path:** Pursue merchant marine license or port/maritime operations

**Top employers:** Shipping companies, ports, Coast Guard (civilian), maritime unions

## HM - Hospital Corpsman

**Civilian careers:** Paramedic ($48K-$65K), Registered Nurse via accelerated programs ($70K-$98K), Physician Assistant ($110K-$135K with additional school), Medical assistant ($35K-$50K)
