# Comprehensive Competitive Analysis: Military Job Boards & Placement Services

**Date:** November 16, 2025
**Prepared For:** Military Transition Toolkit (MTT)
**Total Competitors Analyzed:** 8

---

## Executive Summary

This report provides an in-depth competitive analysis of 8 major military job board and placement service providers. The military transition market serves 200,000+ annual separations with a veteran workforce of 18+ million. Key findings reveal significant gaps in user experience, skills translation, and authentic employer vetting that MTT can exploit for differentiation.

**Market Context:**
- 70% of veterans describe transition as "stressful"
- 50% find transition "confusing" or "painful"
- 33% cite finding employment as their biggest challenge
- Average time to first civilian job: 4 months
- 60-80% of veterans leave first civilian job before 2 years
- Only 23% of potential recruits meet service requirements

## 1. CLEAREDJOBS.NET

### Platform Overview

**Founded:** 2001
**Headquarters:** Falls Church, VA
**Employees:** 11-50
**Revenue:** $1M - $5M
**Ownership:** Privately held, veteran-owned
**Status:** Active

**Mission:** Connect security-cleared professionals with defense, intelligence, and government employers while supporting military transition programs.

**Target Audience:**
- Security-cleared professionals (active clearance holders)
- Direct-hire employers only (no placement firms/headhunters allowed)
- Veterans transitioning from military service

**Scale:**
- ~25,000 active job postings (vs. ClearanceJobs' 65,000)
- 2nd largest cleared job board
- Active job fair presence at military bases
- 4,500+ Facebook followers

### Feature Breakdown

**For Job Seekers (FREE):**
- Resume upload with clearance level showcase
- Job search by clearance level, location, skills
- Profile visibility controls (Active, Private, Anonymous)
- Job Agent email alerts (up to 25 results/day)
- Map view for location-based search
- No applicant tracking system integration

**For Employers (PAID):**
- Unlimited resume database access during service period
- Boolean search capabilities
- Advanced search tools and filters (6 total)
- "Parking space" job posting model (swap jobs freely)
- Resume agents (automated 24-hour searches)
- Social media job promotion
- Professional account managers
- No pay-per-view or search limits
- Basic recordkeeping tools

**Training & Support:**
- Monthly Boolean search training for employers
- Educational programs at military bases
- Lunches for transition programs at NCR bases

**Notable Limitations:**
- No mobile app (iOS/Android)
- No applicant tracking system
- No free job posting option
- 90% of job agents ineffective (per company blog)
- Limited geo-targeting (vs. ClearanceJobs)
- Dated UI/UX

### Pricing Model & Monetization

**Business Model:** B2B subscription services

**Revenue Streams:**
- Resume database subscriptions (no published pricing)
- Job posting packages (parking space model)
- Job fair participation
- Banner advertising
- Direct email campaigns
- Featured Employer program
- Social media marketing

**Pricing:** Not publicly available. Must contact sales (703-871-0037 Option 5)

**Policy:** Strict "no return, no refund" policy

**For Job Seekers:** 100% free (all services)

### User Experience & Onboarding

**Job Seeker Onboarding:**
1. Create account with email (username = email)
2. Upload resume
3. Add clearance level, skills, job title
4. Set profile visibility preference
5. Configure job agents for automated alerts

**Recent UX Updates:**
- Fresh redesign launched (user-friendly interface)
- New profile fields: Recent/Current Job Title (headline)
- Enhanced map view for location search
- "Complete Your Profile Now!" dashboard prompt

**Friction Points:**
- Job agents limited to 25 results/day
- No mobile app experience
- Dated UI compared to modern job boards
- Limited filters (6 vs. ClearanceJobs' 14)
- No experience level filtering

### Strengths & Competitive Advantages

1. **Veteran-Owned Status** - Authentic military credibility
2. **Direct-Hire Only Model** - No staffing agencies; quality control
3. **Professional Account Managers** - Dedicated employer support
4. **Unlimited Resume Access** - No pay-per-view fees
5. **Flexible Job Postings** - Swap jobs freely (parking space model)
6. **Military Base Presence** - On-base educational programs and job fairs
7. **Clearance Focus** - Specialized niche in high-demand market
8. **Community Involvement** - Wreaths Across America, Still Serving Veterans
9. **Boolean Search** - Advanced search for recruiters

### Weaknesses & Vulnerabilities

1. **10x Smaller Traffic** - ClearanceJobs has 988.8K visits vs. ClearedJobs.Net's much smaller reach
2. **Limited Job Volume** - 25,000 jobs vs. 65,000 on ClearanceJobs
3. **7x Fewer Resumes** - Significantly smaller candidate pool
4. **Outdated Technology** - "Dated UI," no mobile app, limited ATS integration
5. **Less User-Friendly** - Fewer filters, no live chat, limited payment options
6. **No Pricing Transparency** - Hidden costs concern
7. **Limited Reviews** - No Trustpilot/Google reviews; 4 Glassdoor reviews only
8. **Ineffective Job Agents** - 90% ineffective per company admission
9. **No BBB Rating** - Limited third-party validation
10. **Employment Scam Concerns** - Company acknowledges bad actors in space

### User Sentiment & Reviews

**Glassdoor (Employer Reviews):**
- 4 reviews total (very limited data)

**ScamAdviser:**
- Positive trust score based on 40 data sources
- "Appears legit and safe"

**BetterTeam Analysis:**
- No employer reviews found
- No BBB rating
- 4,500+ Facebook followers/likes

**Reddit/Forums:** No discussions found

**Overall Sentiment:** Limited public feedback; appears legitimate but lacks social proof

### Opportunity Analysis for MTT

**What MTT Should Learn:**
1. Direct-hire only model creates quality perception
2. Professional account managers add value for employers
3. Clearance-specific focus serves underserved niche
4. Military base presence builds credibility and pipeline
5. Unlimited access models preferred over pay-per-view

**Differentiation Angles for MTT:**
1. **Modern UX:** Build mobile-first with contemporary design
2. **Transparency:** Public pricing, no hidden fees
3. **Effective Alerts:** Fix the 90% ineffective job agent problem
4. **Skills Translation:** Built-in military-to-civilian skill translator
5. **Employer Vetting:** Verify "military-friendly" claims (address 22% unmet expectations)
6. **Broader Audience:** Serve all transitioning military, not just cleared professionals
7. **Career Coaching:** Integrate transition support beyond job listings
8. **Social Proof:** Build review systems and testimonials from day one
9. **ATS Integration:** Connect with employer systems for easier applications
10. **Quality Over Volume:** Position as curated, vetted opportunities vs. mass listings

4. **Broader Inclusion:** Serve all veterans, not just "top candidates" for conferences
5. **Employer Accountability:** Track and publish retention rates beyond 1 year
6. **Skills-First Matching:** AI-powered but candidate-controlled matching
7. **Faster Timelines:** Immediate job access vs. waiting for conference invitations
8. **Integrated Resources:** Career planning + education benefits + healthcare in one platform
9. **Community Building:** Peer support beyond transactional job placement
10. **Outcome Transparency:** Public success metrics vs. private RPO arrangements
