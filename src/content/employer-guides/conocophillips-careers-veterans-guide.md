# ConocoPhillips Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 10,000+ employees globally
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: ConocoPhillips Military Veterans Network
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Operations Tech, Engineer, Maintenance, HSE, Project Management
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $65,000 - $180,000+ depending on role
- **E&P Leader**: Largest independent exploration and production company
- **Technical Focus**: Engineering-driven culture matches military precision

ConocoPhillips is the world's largest independent exploration and production (E&P) company, focused purely on finding and producing oil and natural gas. Veterans with technical, mechanical, and engineering backgrounds find excellent opportunities and a culture valuing safety, operational excellence, and innovation.

## Company Overview

ConocoPhillips explores for, produces, transports, and markets crude oil, bitumen, natural gas, and natural gas liquids worldwide. Unlike integrated majors, they focus exclusively on exploration and production (upstream).

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 10,000+ globally
- **Annual Production**: 1.8+ million barrels oil equivalent/day
- **Reserves**: 6.8+ billion barrels oil equivalent
- **Countries**: Operations on 6 continents
- **Annual Revenue**: $60+ billion

### Culture for Veterans

ConocoPhillips emphasizes the SPIRIT values: Safety, People, Integrity, Responsibility, Innovation, and Teamwork. This aligns with military values. The company's focus on operational excellence, safety, and technical precision mirrors military operations. Veterans find their training valued in field operations and engineering roles.

## What Veterans Should Know

### Pros

1. **Excellent Compensation**: Industry-leading pay
2. **Benefits**: Outstanding 401(k) and benefits
3. **Technical Challenge**: Sophisticated E&P operations
4. **SPIRIT Values**: Align with military values

3. **Industry Cycles**: Oil prices affect operations
4. **Physical Demands**: Field work is demanding
5. **Geographic Requirements**: Must relocate for many roles
