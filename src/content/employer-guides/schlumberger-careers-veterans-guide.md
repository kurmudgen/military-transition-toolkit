# Schlumberger (SLB) Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 100,000+ employees globally
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: SLB Military Recruiting, VETS ERG
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Field Engineer, Operator, Technician, Project Manager, IT
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $60,000 - $180,000+ depending on role
- **Industry Leader**: World's largest oilfield services company
- **Technical Excellence**: Engineering-driven culture with global opportunities

Schlumberger (now SLB) is the world's largest oilfield services company, providing technology, information solutions, and integrated project management to the oil and gas industry. Veterans with technical, mechanical, and engineering backgrounds find excellent opportunities and extensive global travel.

## Company Overview

SLB provides technology and services to oil and gas exploration and production worldwide. Their services include drilling, reservoir characterization, production, and processing solutions across every phase of hydrocarbon discovery and recovery.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 100,000+ globally
- **Annual Revenue**: $30+ billion
- **Countries**: Operations in 100+ countries
- **Technology Centers**: Major R&D facilities worldwide
- **Service Lines**: Multiple specialized divisions

### Culture for Veterans

SLB's culture emphasizes technical excellence, safety, and global collaboration. The field-intensive nature of oilfield services mirrors military deployment operations. ## Application Process

### Step 1: Find Open Positions

**Where to Look:**

- Engineering job boards

**Keywords to Search:**
- "Field Engineer"

- "Operator"
- "Technician"
- Specific service line names

**Best Times to Apply:**
- Year-round hiring
- Major project awards
- Exploration activity increases

### Step 2: Resume & Application

**What SLB Looks For:**
- Technical/mechanical aptitude
- Problem-solving ability
- Adaptability to field conditions
- Global mobility
- Safety consciousness

**Resume Format:**
- Technical skills prominent
- Equipment experience highlighted
- Deployment/travel history noted
- Certifications listed

**Resume Keywords That Work:**
- "Field operations"
- "Technical troubleshooting"
- "Equipment maintenance"
- "Remote/deployed operations"
- "Safety compliance"

**Important:**
- Emphasize technical training
- Highlight experience in challenging environments
- Note willingness for global mobility

### Step 3: Interview Process

**Typical Interview Stages:**
1. Online Application
2. Phone Screen with Recruiter
3. Technical Assessment
4. Interview with Hiring Manager
5. Panel Interview
6. Offer

**Common Questions:**
- "Why SLB / oilfield services?"
- "Describe your technical experience"
- "How do you handle working in remote locations?"
- "Tell me about solving a complex technical problem"
- "Are you prepared for extensive travel/rotation?"

**What They Value:**
- Technical excellence
- Adaptability
- Safety commitment
- Global mobility
- Problem-solving

### Step 4: Offer & Negotiation

**Typical Offer Timeline:**
- Field roles: 2-4 weeks
- Engineering: 4-8 weeks
- Senior: 6-10 weeks

**Compensation Package:**
- Competitive base salary
- Field premiums
- International assignments (tax advantages)
- 401(k) or equivalent
- Healthcare
- Training/development investment

**Negotiation Tips:**
- Field premiums are standard
- International pay varies by location
- Training is extensive (value it)
- Ask about rotation schedules
