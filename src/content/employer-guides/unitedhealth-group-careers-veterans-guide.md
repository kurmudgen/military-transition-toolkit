# UnitedHealth Group Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 400,000+ employees globally
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: UnitedHealth Group Military Program, Optum Serve
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Clinical, IT, Operations, Analytics, Management
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $45,000 - $150,000+ depending on role
- **Healthcare Focus**: Largest healthcare company—diverse opportunities
- **Military Healthcare**: Optum Serve provides healthcare services to military/VA

UnitedHealth Group is the largest healthcare company in the world, operating UnitedHealthcare (insurance) and Optum (healthcare services, pharmacy, technology). Veterans find opportunities across clinical, technology, and operations roles, with Optum Serve specifically focused on military healthcare.

## Company Overview

UnitedHealth Group operates two main businesses: UnitedHealthcare provides health insurance products, while Optum offers healthcare services, pharmacy benefits, and technology solutions. Optum Serve specifically serves military members, veterans, and their families.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 400,000+ globally
- **Annual Revenue**: $370+ billion
- **Members**: 150+ million people served
- **Optum Serve**: Serves millions of military/VA beneficiaries
- **Fortune Ranking**: Top 5 company

### Culture for Veterans

UnitedHealth Group's culture emphasizes improving health outcomes and caring for people. Veterans—especially those with medical backgrounds—find purpose in this healthcare mission. Optum Serve's focus on military healthcare creates natural pathways for veterans wanting to continue serving military communities.

## Application Process

### Step 1: Find Open Positions

**Where to Look:**
- Primary: careers.unitedhealthgroup.com
- Optum Serve: optum.com/optum-serve
- LinkedIn: UnitedHealth Group, Optum
- Military job fairs

**Keywords to Search:**
- "Clinical"
- "Analyst"
- "Optum Serve"
- "Operations"
- "Technology"
- "Military"

**Best Times to Apply:**
- Year-round hiring
- Open enrollment support: August-December
- Government contract awards

### Step 2: Resume & Application

**What UnitedHealth Group Looks For:**
- Healthcare interest/experience
- Analytical skills
- Customer service orientation
- Technology aptitude
- Leadership experience

**Resume Format:**
- Healthcare experience prominent
- Analytical skills highlighted
- Technology proficiencies listed
- Leadership examples

**Resume Keywords That Work:**
- "Healthcare/medical"
- "Patient care"
- "Data analysis"
- "Claims/benefits"
- "Technology systems"

**Important:**
- Highlight medical MOS background
- Note TRICARE/military healthcare experience
- Show analytical capabilities

### Step 3: Interview Process

**Typical Interview Stages:**
1. Online Application
2. Phone Screen with Recruiter
3. Hiring Manager Interview
4. Panel Interview (some roles)
5. Offer

**Common Questions:**
- "Why healthcare / UnitedHealth Group?"
- "Tell me about your healthcare experience"
- "How do you handle complex data analysis?"
- "Describe improving a process"
- "How do you prioritize competing demands?"

**What They Value:**
- Patient/member focus
- Analytical thinking
- Collaboration
- Continuous improvement
- Integrity

### Step 4: Offer & Negotiation

**Typical Offer Timeline:**
- Entry level: 1-2 weeks
- Professional: 2-4 weeks
- Senior: 3-6 weeks

**Compensation Package:**
- Competitive salary
- Annual bonus (varies by role)
- 401(k) with match
- Healthcare benefits
- Stock purchase plan
- Tuition reimbursement

**Negotiation Tips:**
- Some flexibility on base salary
- Bonus varies by role and level
- Ask about sign-on bonus

## What Veterans Should Know
