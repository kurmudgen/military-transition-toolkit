# CVS Health Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 300,000+ employees
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: CVS Health Military & Veteran Program
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Pharmacy Technician, Store Manager, MinuteClinic, Distribution, Corporate
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $30,000 - $150,000+ depending on role
- **Healthcare Focus**: Largest pharmacy chain + MinuteClinic + Aetna insurance
- **Medic Path**: Direct pathway for military medics to healthcare roles

CVS Health is transforming from a pharmacy chain into a diversified healthcare company. For veterans, especially those with medical backgrounds (corpsmen, medics), CVS offers pathways into civilian healthcare. The company hired its 25,000th veteran in recent years and continues expanding veteran programs.

## Company Overview

CVS Health operates retail pharmacies, MinuteClinic walk-in clinics, specialty pharmacy services, pharmacy benefit management (Caremark), and health insurance (Aetna). This diversification creates healthcare career opportunities beyond traditional retail.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 300,000+
- **Annual Revenue**: $350+ billion
- **Retail Stores**: 9,000+ CVS Pharmacy locations
- **MinuteClinics**: 1,100+ in-store clinics
- **Aetna**: 30+ million members

### Culture for Veterans

CVS Health's culture emphasizes helping people on their path to better health. For veterans—especially former medics and corpsmen—this aligns with their service experience of caring for others. The company values military discipline, healthcare training, and leadership skills.

## What Veterans Should Know

### Pros

1. **Healthcare Career Path**: Medics can transition to civilian healthcare
2. **Pharmacy Tech Training**: Support for certification
3. **Career Diversity**: Retail, clinical, corporate options
4. **Benefits**: Healthcare, retirement, education
5. **Growing Company**: Healthcare services expanding

### Cons

1. **Retail Hours**: Stores open extended hours
2. **Pharmacy Pressure**: High-volume, accuracy-critical
3. **Customer Demands**: Retail pharmacy can be stressful
4. **Certification Required**: Pharmacy tech needs state license
5. **Physical Work**: Standing, stock management

## Requirements

### Education
- Store Associate: No degree required

### Certifications
- Pharmacy Technician: State certification required
- NP/PA: State license for MinuteClinic

- Lifting moderate weight
- Accuracy-focused work

### Experience
- Store Associate: 0 years
- Pharmacy Tech: Training provided
- Store Manager: 2-4 years retail
- MinuteClinic NP/PA: Clinical experience
