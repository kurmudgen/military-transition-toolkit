# ExxonMobil Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 62,000+ employees globally
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: ExxonMobil Veterans Network, Military Recruiting
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Operations Tech, Engineer, Maintenance, HSE, IT
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $65,000 - $180,000+ depending on role
- **Energy Giant**: One of world's largest publicly traded oil and gas companies
- **Technical Excellence**: Engineering-driven culture aligns with military precision

ExxonMobil is among the world's largest energy companies, with operations spanning exploration, production, refining, and chemicals. Veterans with technical backgrounds find excellent opportunities and a culture emphasizing safety, reliability, and operational excellence.

## Company Overview

ExxonMobil explores for and produces crude oil and natural gas, manufactures petroleum products and chemicals, and markets fuels and chemicals worldwide.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 62,000+ globally
- **Annual Revenue**: $350+ billion
- **Daily Production**: 3.7+ million barrels oil equivalent
- **Refineries**: Major facilities worldwide
- **Chemical Plants**: Integrated chemical operations
