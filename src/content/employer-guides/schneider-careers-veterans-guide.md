# Schneider National Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 15,000+ employees
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: Schneider Military Apprenticeship Program, CDL Training
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Company Driver, Owner-Operator, Fleet Manager, Diesel Technician, Operations
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $50,000 - $90,000+ depending on role
- **Military Apprenticeship**: Registered DoL apprenticeship for veterans
- **Orange Fleet**: Iconic orange trucks across America

Schneider is one of America's most recognized trucking companies with extensive veteran support programs. Their registered Military Apprenticeship Program provides GI Bill benefits while earning a CDL and starting a driving career. Veterans are highly valued at Schneider.

## Company Overview

Schneider National provides transportation, intermodal, and logistics services across North America. Known for their iconic orange trucks, Schneider operates one of the largest private fleets and has been in business since 1935.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 15,000+
- **Annual Revenue**: $6+ billion
- **Power Units**: 10,000+
- **Trailers**: 45,000+
- **Intermodal Containers**: 25,000+

### Culture for Veterans

Schneider's culture emphasizes safety, integrity, respect, and excellence. The company's long history and family-founded values create a stable environment. Veterans appreciate Schneider's structured operations, clear expectations, and genuine respect for military service.

## Veteran Hiring Programs

### Schneider Military Apprenticeship Program (SMAP)

Registered Department of Labor apprenticeship:
- GI Bill eligible—earn stipend while training
- Free CDL training
- Guaranteed job upon completion
- Mentorship from veteran drivers

### Military Skills Translation

Schneider recognizes:
- Military driving experience
- Leadership and discipline
- Safety consciousness
- Logistics background

### CDL Training Program

For veterans without CDL:
- Company-sponsored training
- GI Bill accepted
- Quick path to employment
- Paid training period

### Veteran Employee Network

Community support:
- Mentorship programs
- Career development
- Networking events
- Veteran recognition

## Application Process

### Step 1: Find Open Positions

**Where to Look:**
- Primary: schneiderjobs.com
- Military specific: schneiderjobs.com/military
- LinkedIn: Schneider National
- Indeed for local positions

**Keywords to Search:**
- "Company Driver"
- "Regional Driver"
- "Intermodal"
- "Fleet Manager"
- "Apprenticeship"

**Best Times to Apply:**
- Year-round driver hiring
- Spring/summer freight increases
- Continuous corporate recruiting

### Step 2: Resume & Application

**What Schneider Looks For:**
- Clean driving record
- Reliability
- Safety focus
- Professional attitude
- Military experience valued

**Resume Format:**
- Driving experience prominent
- Military vehicle certifications
- Safety achievements
- Leadership roles

**Resume Keywords That Work:**
- "CDL" or military driving equivalent
- "Safe driving record"
- "Convoy operations"
- "Vehicle maintenance"
- "Team leadership"

**Important:**
- Clean MVR required
- Military driving experience counts
- GI Bill eligibility for apprenticeship

### Step 3: Interview Process

**Typical Interview Stages (Drivers):**
1. Online Application
2. Recruiter Phone Screen
3. Driving Record Verification
4. Interview
5. DOT Physical/Drug Test
6. Offer/Orientation

**Typical Interview Stages (Corporate):**
1. Online Application
2. Phone Screen
3. Hiring Manager Interview
4. Final Interview
5. Offer

**Common Questions:**
- "Why Schneider?"
- "Tell me about your driving background"
- "How do you prioritize safety?"
- "What does professionalism mean to you?"
- "How do you handle time on the road?"

**What They Value:**
- Safety commitment
- Reliability
- Professionalism
- Integrity
- Team orientation

### Step 4: Offer & Negotiation

**Typical Offer Timeline:**
- Drivers: 1-2 weeks
- Corporate: 2-4 weeks

**Compensation Package:**
- Per-mile or salary
- Sign-on bonus opportunities
- Healthcare benefits
- 401(k) with match
- Performance bonuses

**Negotiation Tips:**
- Experienced drivers can negotiate rate
- Ask about home time preferences
- Sign-on bonus may be available

## What Veterans Should Know

### Pros

1. **GI Bill Apprenticeship**: Earn while training
2. **Military Recognition**: Experience genuinely valued
3. **Multiple Driving Options**: OTR, regional, intermodal
4. **Established Company**: 85+ year history
5. **Training Programs**: CDL training available
6. **Career Advancement**: Non-driving paths available
7. **Benefits**: Comprehensive package

### Cons

1. **Time Away**: OTR requires road time
2. **Variable Income**: Mileage-based for drivers
3. **Physical Demands**: Loading may be required
4. **Regulatory**: Hours of service limits
5. **Weather**: Drive in all conditions
