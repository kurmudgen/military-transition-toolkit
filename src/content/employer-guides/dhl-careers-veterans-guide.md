# DHL Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 600,000+ employees globally (40,000+ in US)
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: DHL Veterans Program, Certified™ Military-Friendly Employer
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Courier, Operations Supervisor, Warehouse Associate, Driver, Manager
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $40,000 - $100,000+ depending on role
- **Global Company**: World's largest logistics company—international opportunities
- **Express Focus**: DHL Express (international shipping) is major US operation

DHL is the world's largest logistics company, offering diverse career opportunities in express shipping, freight forwarding, supply chain management, and e-commerce. Veterans find opportunities across operations, driving, and management with potential for global assignments.

## Company Overview

DHL operates through multiple divisions: DHL Express (international express), DHL Global Forwarding (freight), DHL Supply Chain (contract logistics), and DHL eCommerce. The company has the world's most international express network.

### By the Numbers
- **Global Employees**: 600,000+
- **US Employees**: 40,000+
- **Annual Revenue**: $95+ billion (global)
- **Countries**: 220+ countries and territories
- **Aircraft**: 300+ (largest international cargo airline)

### Culture for Veterans

DHL's culture emphasizes teamwork, respect, and delivering results. As a German-based company with global operations, they value precision and reliability—qualities veterans possess. Their "Certified Great Place to Work" status reflects commitment to employee well-being.

## Application Process

### Step 1: Find Open Positions

**Where to Look:**
- Primary: careers.dhl.com (global)
- dhl.com/us-en/careers (US)
- LinkedIn: DHL
- Indeed for local positions

**Keywords to Search:**
- "Courier"
- "Operations"
- "Warehouse"
- "Supply Chain"
- "Express"

**Best Times to Apply:**
- Year-round hiring
- Peak season prep: Q3
- Continuous operations recruiting

### Step 2: Resume & Application

**What DHL Looks For:**
- Reliability and punctuality
- Customer service orientation
- Safety consciousness
- International perspective helpful
- Language skills valued

**Resume Format:**
- Achievement-focused
- Customer service examples
- Safety record
- Any international experience

**Resume Keywords That Work:**
- "International logistics"
- "Customer service"
- "Safety compliance"
- "Operations management"
- "Import/export" (if relevant)

**Important:**
- Note any international experience
- Language skills are assets
- Clean driving record for courier roles

### Step 3: Interview Process

**Typical Interview Stages:**
1. Online Application
2. Phone Screen
3. In-Person Interview
4. Background Check
5. DOT requirements (drivers)
6. Offer

**Common Questions:**
- "Why DHL / logistics?"
- "Tell me about customer service experience"
- "How do you handle time-sensitive situations?"
- "Describe working in a fast-paced environment"
- "How do you prioritize multiple deliveries?"

**What They Value:**
- Customer focus
- Reliability
- Safety
- Teamwork
- Speed and accuracy

### Step 4: Offer & Negotiation

**Typical Offer Timeline:**
- Operations: 1-2 weeks
- Management: 2-4 weeks

**Compensation Package:**
- Competitive wages/salary
- Benefits package
- 401(k)
- Employee discounts
- International opportunities

**Negotiation Tips:**
- Some flexibility on rate/salary
- Ask about advancement timeline
- Consider international opportunities
