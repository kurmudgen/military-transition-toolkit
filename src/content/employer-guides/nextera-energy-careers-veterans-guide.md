NextEra Energy is the world's largest producer of renewable energy from the wind and sun, operating Florida Power & Light and NextEra Energy Resources. Veterans with technical, mechanical, and electrical backgrounds find excellent opportunities in this rapidly growing clean energy sector.

## Company Overview

NextEra Energy operates two main businesses: Florida Power & Light (regulated utility serving 12 million Floridians) and NextEra Energy Resources (nation's largest generator of renewable energy from wind, solar, and battery storage).

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 15,000+
- **Annual Revenue**: $20+ billion
- **Wind Turbines**: 19,000+ turbines operated
- **Solar Capacity**: Leading solar generator
- **States**: Operations in 40+ states
- **Customers**: 12+ million (FPL)

### Culture for Veterans

NextEra's culture combines utility reliability with renewable energy innovation. The company values safety, operational excellence, and environmental stewardship. Veterans find the mission-oriented focus on powering communities and clean energy transition meaningful. Technical training from military service translates directly to wind, solar, and grid operations.

## Application Process

### Step 1: Find Open Positions

**Where to Look:**
- Primary: careers.nexteraenergy.com
- LinkedIn: NextEra Energy
- Indeed for specific locations
- Clean energy job boards

**Keywords to Search:**
- "Wind Technician"
- "Solar Technician"
- "Power Plant Operator"
- "FPL" (Florida Power & Light)
- "Renewable"

**Best Times to Apply:**
- Year-round hiring (rapid growth)
- New project announcements
- Before storm season (FPL)

### Step 2: Resume & Application

**What NextEra Energy Looks For:**
- Technical/mechanical aptitude
- Safety consciousness
- Adaptability
- Physical capability
- Problem-solving skills

**Resume Format:**
- Technical experience prominent
- Safety focus highlighted
- Equipment experience listed
- Certifications noted

**Resume Keywords That Work:**
- "Turbine/generator"
- "Electrical systems"
- "Mechanical maintenance"
- "Safety compliance"
- "Troubleshooting"

**Important:**
- Highlight mechanical/electrical experience
- Note any turbine or generator experience
- Emphasize ability to work at heights (wind roles)

### Step 3: Interview Process

**Typical Interview Stages:**
1. Online Application
2. Phone Screen
3. Technical Assessment
4. Interview with Manager
5. Physical Assessment (field roles)
6. Offer

**Common Questions:**
- "Why NextEra / renewable energy?"
- "Describe your technical/mechanical experience"
- "How do you handle working at heights?"
- "Tell me about troubleshooting complex equipment"
- "How do you stay safe in hazardous conditions?"

**What They Value:**
- Safety commitment
- Technical skills
- Reliability
- Physical fitness
- Clean energy passion

### Step 4: Offer & Negotiation

**Typical Offer Timeline:**
- Technician: 2-4 weeks
- Engineering: 3-6 weeks
- Management: 4-8 weeks

**Compensation Package:**
- Competitive base salary
- Annual bonus
- 401(k) with match
- Pension (FPL)
- Healthcare
- Stock purchase plan

**Negotiation Tips:**
- Some positions have set scales
- Engineering has flexibility
- Travel positions often pay premium
- Sign-on bonuses possible
