# L3Harris Technologies Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 50,000+ employees globally
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: L3Harris Military Veteran Initiative, Veteran ERG
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Communications Engineer, Technician, Program Manager, ISR Analyst, Cybersecurity
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $60,000 - $160,000+ depending on role
- **Military Communications**: World leader in tactical radios—natural fit for Signal soldiers
- **Clearance Value**: High percentage of positions require Secret or TS/SCI

L3Harris is the result of the 2019 merger between L3 Technologies and Harris Corporation, creating the sixth-largest defense contractor. Known for tactical communications, ISR systems, and space technology, L3Harris is a natural fit for Signal, Intel, and Space veterans.

## Company Overview

L3Harris Technologies provides advanced defense and commercial technologies. Core products include tactical radios (used by every US military branch), ISR systems, space systems, maritime electronics, and cybersecurity solutions. If you used a military radio, it was likely made by Harris/L3Harris.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 50,000+ globally
- **Annual Revenue**: $18+ billion
- **Tactical Radios**: #1 supplier to US military
- **Space Systems**: Major satellite and launch provider
- **ISR**: Leading provider of intelligence systems

### Culture for Veterans

L3Harris' culture is deeply connected to military communications and intelligence. Many products are designed with direct military input, and veteran employees contribute invaluable operational perspective. The company understands military terminology, culture, and mission—making veteran integration seamless.

## Application Process

### Step 1: Find Open Positions

**Where to Look:**
- Primary: l3harris.com/careers
- LinkedIn: L3Harris + filter
- ClearanceJobs.com
- Indeed for technician roles

**Keywords to Search:**
- "Radio" or "Communications"
- "ISR" or "Intelligence"
- "Space"
- "Cleared" or "Clearance"
- "Signal" (your MOS may appear)

**Best Times to Apply:**
- After major radio contract awards
- Government fiscal year (October)
- Continuous for cleared positions

### Step 2: Resume & Application

**What L3Harris Looks For:**
- Military communications experience
- Security clearance
- Technical aptitude
- Problem-solving skills
- Operational understanding

**Resume Format:**
- Clearance prominently displayed
- Specific radio systems experience
- MOS/Rating clearly explained
- Certifications listed

**Resume Keywords That Work:**
- "Tactical communications"
- "Radio systems"
- "SATCOM"
- "ISR"
- "Signals intelligence"
- Specific radio types (Harris, SINCGARS, etc.)

**Important:**
- If you used Harris radios, mention it specifically
- Translate military radio knowledge to civilian terms
- Note any formal communications training

### Step 3: Interview Process

**Typical Interview Stages:**
1. Application Review
2. Recruiter Screen
3. Technical Interview with Hiring Manager
4. Panel Interview (senior roles)
5. Security Verification
6. Offer

**Common Questions:**
- "Why L3Harris?"
- "What military radio systems have you worked with?"
- "Describe troubleshooting communications problems"
- "How do you handle classified information?"
- "Tell me about working in a tactical environment"

**What They Value:**
- Communications expertise
- Security consciousness
- Technical problem-solving
- Operational experience
- Team collaboration

### Step 4: Offer & Negotiation

**Typical Offer Timeline:**
- Cleared positions: 2-3 weeks
- Technical roles: 2-4 weeks

**Compensation Package:**
- Competitive base salary
- Annual bonus
- 401(k) with match
- ESPP (Employee Stock Purchase)
- Comprehensive benefits
