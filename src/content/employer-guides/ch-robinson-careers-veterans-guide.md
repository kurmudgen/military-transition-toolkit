# C.H. Robinson Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 15,000+ employees globally
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: C.H. Robinson Military Veterans Program
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Carrier Sales Rep, Account Executive, Operations Specialist, Analyst, Manager
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $45,000 - $120,000+ depending on role
- **Business Model**: Freight brokerage—matching shippers with carriers
- **Office Environment**: Sales-driven, fast-paced, commission potential

C.H. Robinson is one of the world's largest freight brokers, connecting shippers who need to move goods with carriers who have capacity. This non-asset model means veterans work in sales, operations, and analytics rather than driving. Military leadership and communication skills translate well.

## Company Overview

C.H. Robinson provides freight transportation services and logistics technology. As a broker, they don't own trucks—they match shipper needs with carrier capacity, taking a margin on each shipment. They arrange over 18 million shipments annually.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 15,000+ globally
- **Annual Revenue**: $17+ billion
- **Shipments**: 18+ million annually
- **Carriers**: Access to 85,000+ carriers
- **Offices**: 270+ worldwide

### Culture for Veterans

C.H. Robinson's culture is fast-paced, competitive, and performance-driven. The sales environment rewards hustle, communication skills, and relationship building—strengths many veterans possess. Military experience in logistics and transportation is directly applicable to understanding freight movement.

## Application Process

### Step 1: Find Open Positions

**Where to Look:**
- Primary: chrobinson.com/careers
- LinkedIn: C.H. Robinson
- College recruiting events
- Veteran job fairs

**Keywords to Search:**
- "Carrier Sales"
- "Account Executive"
- "Operations"
- "Logistics"
- "Associate" (entry level)

**Best Times to Apply:**
- Year-round hiring
- January (new year budget)
- Summer (recent grad hiring)

### Step 2: Resume & Application

**What C.H. Robinson Looks For:**
- Communication skills
- Competitive drive
- Problem-solving ability
- Customer service orientation
- Logistics interest

**Resume Format:**
- Achievement-focused
- Sales/negotiation examples
- Leadership highlighted
- Customer interaction experience

**Resume Keywords That Work:**
- "Sales" or "customer relationship"
- "Logistics/supply chain"
- "Communication"
- "Problem resolution"
- "Team leadership"

**Important:**
- Emphasize communication and negotiation
- Note any logistics/transportation background
- Show competitive nature/drive

### Step 3: Interview Process

**Typical Interview Stages:**
1. Online Application
2. Phone Screen with HR
3. Hiring Manager Interview
4. Panel Interview (sometimes)
5. Offer

**Interview Format:**
- Behavioral questions
- Sales aptitude assessment
- Role-play scenarios (sometimes)
- Culture fit evaluation

**Common Questions:**
- "Why C.H. Robinson / logistics?"
- "Tell me about a time you persuaded someone"
- "How do you handle rejection?"
- "Describe a sales or negotiation experience"
- "How do you prioritize competing demands?"

**What They Value:**
- Communication skills
- Persistence and resilience
- Competitive nature
- Customer focus
- Team collaboration

### Step 4: Offer & Negotiation

**Typical Offer Timeline:**
- Entry level: 1-2 weeks
- Experienced: 2-4 weeks

**Compensation Package:**
- Base salary
- Commission/bonus structure
- Benefits package
- 401(k) with match
- Education assistance

**Negotiation Tips:**
- Base salary has some flexibility
- Commission structure typically standard
- Ask about territory or book of business
