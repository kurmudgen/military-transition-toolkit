# J.B. Hunt Transport Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 35,000+ employees
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: J.B. Hunt Military Transition Program, CDL Training
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Company Driver, Intermodal Driver, Fleet Manager, Diesel Mechanic, Operations
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $50,000 - $100,000+ depending on role
- **CDL Training**: Free CDL training for qualified candidates
- **Home Time**: Intermodal positions often offer better home time than traditional OTR

J.B. Hunt is one of the largest transportation companies in North America, offering diverse driving and non-driving career opportunities. Their military transition program helps veterans obtain CDLs and start trucking careers with excellent pay and home time options.

## Company Overview

J.B. Hunt Transport Services provides transportation and delivery services across North America. They operate multiple business segments including Intermodal (rail/truck), Dedicated Contract Services, Integrated Capacity Solutions, Final Mile Services, and Truckload.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 35,000+
- **Annual Revenue**: $15+ billion
- **Trucks**: 18,000+
- **Trailers**: 100,000+
- **Intermodal Containers**: 100,000+

## Application Process

### Step 1: Find Open Positions

**Where to Look:**
- Primary: jbhunt.com/careers
- jbhunt.com/drivers (driver-specific)
- LinkedIn: J.B. Hunt
- Indeed for local positions

**Keywords to Search:**
- "Company Driver"
- "Intermodal" (best home time)
- "Dedicated" (regional)
- "Fleet Manager"
- "CDL Training"

**Best Times to Apply:**
- Year-round driver hiring
- Freight demand peaks: Q4, spring
- Continuous corporate hiring

### Step 2: Resume & Application

**What J.B. Hunt Looks For:**
- Clean driving record
- Reliability and professionalism
- Customer service orientation
- Safety consciousness
- Military driving experience

**Resume Format:**
- Driving experience highlighted
- Military vehicle certifications
- Safety record
- Leadership experience

**Resume Keywords That Work:**
- "CDL" or military driving license
- "Million mile safe driver"
- "Convoy operations"
- "Fleet management"
- "Logistics coordination"

**Important:**
- Clean MVR (Motor Vehicle Record) essential
- Mention any military convoy experience
- Note large vehicle operation experience

### Step 3: Interview Process

**Typical Interview Stages (Drivers):**
1. Online Application
2. Phone Screen
3. Driving Record Review
4. DOT Physical
5. Drug Screen
6. Orientation

**Typical Interview Stages (Corporate):**
1. Online Application
2. Phone Screen with HR
3. Hiring Manager Interview
4. Final Interview
5. Offer

**Common Questions:**
- "Why trucking / J.B. Hunt?"
- "Tell me about your driving experience"
- "How do you handle time away from home?"
- "Describe your safety practices"
- "What does customer service mean to you?"

**What They Value:**
- Safety record
- Reliability
- Professionalism
- Customer focus
- Integrity

### Step 4: Offer & Negotiation

**Typical Offer Timeline:**
- Drivers: 1-2 weeks after application
- Corporate: 2-4 weeks

**Compensation Package:**
- Per-mile or salary (drivers)
- Sign-on bonus often available
- Benefits after probationary period
- 401(k) with match
- Performance bonuses

**Negotiation Tips:**
- Experienced drivers can negotiate per-mile rate
- Corporate positions have some flexibility
- Sign-on bonus often negotiable

## What Veterans Should Know

### Pros

1. **Free CDL Training**: Company-sponsored for qualified candidates
2. **Multiple Driving Options**: Local, regional, OTR, intermodal
3. **Home Time**: Intermodal offers more home time
4. **Earning Potential**: Top drivers exceed $100K
5. **Fortune 500**: Large, stable company
6. **Career Paths**: Non-driving opportunities available
7. **Benefits**: Healthcare, retirement, tuition assistance

### Cons

1. **Time Away**: OTR means weeks away from home
2. **Physical Demands**: Loading/unloading at some jobs
3. **Weather**: Drive in all conditions
4. **Regulatory Requirements**: Hours of service limits
5. **CDL Maintenance**: Ongoing requirements
