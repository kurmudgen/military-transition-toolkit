# Oracle Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 150,000+ employees globally
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: Oracle Veterans Program, Military Recruiting Team
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Cloud Solutions Engineer, Technical Support, Sales, Consultant, Database Administrator
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $65,000 - $170,000+ depending on role
- **Cloud Focus**: Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) is rapidly growing and hiring
- **Government Business**: Oracle has significant federal contracts valuing cleared candidates

Oracle is one of the world's largest enterprise software companies, with a growing cloud business competing with AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. Veterans find opportunities across cloud engineering, sales, support, and consulting, with federal work requiring security clearances.

## Company Overview

Oracle provides enterprise software, cloud computing services, and hardware products. Known historically for database technology, Oracle has transformed into a major cloud provider with Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) and SaaS applications including NetSuite, Cerner (healthcare), and Oracle Fusion.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 150,000+ globally
- **Annual Revenue**: $50+ billion
- **Market Cap**: $320+ billion
- **Cloud Revenue**: $17+ billion and growing rapidly
- **Data Centers**: 40+ cloud regions globally

### Culture for Veterans

Oracle's culture emphasizes technical excellence, sales performance, and customer success. The environment is competitive and results-oriented. Veterans often thrive in Oracle's structured sales and consulting environments where clear goals and metrics provide familiar guidance. The company's strong federal presence also creates natural pathways for cleared veterans.

## Application Process

### Step 1: Find Open Positions

**Where to Look:**
- Primary: oracle.com/careers
- LinkedIn: Oracle + military/veteran filter
- Oracle Cloud careers specifically
- Oracle government careers (federal)

**Keywords to Search:**
- "Cloud" or "OCI"
- "Solutions Engineer"
- "Technical Support"
- "Consultant"
- "Sales"
- "Principal" (senior roles)

**Best Times to Apply:**
- Q1: New fiscal year (June start)
- Post-earnings: Often announce hiring initiatives
- Continuous for cloud roles (growth area)

### Step 2: Resume & Application

**What Oracle Looks For:**
- Technical aptitude
- Customer engagement skills
- Problem-solving ability
- Sales/results orientation
- Continuous learning mindset

**Resume Format:**
- Clean, professional format
- Achievement-oriented bullets
- Quantified results
- Technical skills section

**Resume Keywords That Work:**
- "Cloud implementation"
- "Customer success"
- "Technical solution"
- "Revenue growth" (for sales)
- "Problem resolution"
- "Database" (if applicable)

**Important:**
- Highlight any cloud experience or certifications
- Note security clearance (critical for federal roles)
- Show customer-facing experience

### Step 3: Interview Process

**Typical Interview Stages:**
1. Application Review
2. Recruiter Phone Screen (30 minutes)
3. Technical Interview/Assessment
4. Hiring Manager Interview (45-60 minutes)
5. Final Panel Interview
6. Offer

**Interview Format:**
- Technical questions (for technical roles)
- Behavioral questions (STAR method)
- Sales scenarios (for sales roles)
- Case studies (for consulting)

**Common Questions:**
- "Why Oracle?"
- "What do you know about Oracle Cloud?"
- "Tell me about a time you solved a complex technical problem"
- "How do you handle difficult customer situations?"
- "Describe a time you exceeded expectations"

**What They Value:**
- Technical excellence
- Customer focus
- Competitive drive
- Results orientation
- Continuous learning

### Step 4: Offer & Negotiation

**Typical Offer Timeline:**
- Technical roles: 2-3 weeks
- Sales roles: 2-4 weeks
- Federal roles: May be longer (clearance)

**Compensation Package:**
- Base salary
- Variable compensation (especially sales)
- RSUs (vesting over 4 years)
- Annual bonus (for non-sales)
- Comprehensive benefits

**Negotiation Tips:**
- Oracle has reasonable flexibility
- Sales roles: Negotiate base vs. variable split
- Ask about RSU grants
- Level matters for long-term comp

## Requirements

### Education
- Technical Support: No degree required (experience valued)
- Sales: Bachelor's preferred, not required
- Engineering: Bachelor's in CS/related or equivalent experience
- Consulting: Bachelor's typically required

### Certifications
- Oracle Cloud certifications (strongly valued)
- AWS/Azure (transferable knowledge)
- Security certifications (for security roles)
- PMP (for project management)

### Clearance
- Required for many federal positions
- Active clearance = significant advantage
- Oracle Secure Global Cloud requires cleared personnel

### Experience
- Support: 0-2 years
- Solutions Engineer: 2-5 years
- Senior Engineer: 5-10 years
- Principal: 10+ years
