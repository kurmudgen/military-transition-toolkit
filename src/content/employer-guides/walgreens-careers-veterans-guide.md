Walgreens Boots Alliance is a global pharmacy and healthcare company. Veterans find opportunities in retail pharmacy, healthcare services, distribution, and corporate roles. The company's healthcare focus creates pathways for military medical personnel.

## Company Overview

Walgreens operates retail pharmacies across the United States, complemented by VillageMD clinics, specialty pharmacy, and healthcare services. Part of Walgreens Boots Alliance, the company has global operations.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 320,000+ globally
- **Annual Revenue**: $140+ billion
- **US Stores**: 9,000+ Walgreens locations
- **VillageMD Clinics**: 600+ primary care clinics
- **Daily Customers**: 8+ million

### Culture for Veterans

Walgreens culture emphasizes serving communities and supporting health. The company values military experience, particularly healthcare backgrounds. Veterans find the structured pharmacy environment and customer service focus familiar.

- Detail-oriented work

- Store Associate: 0 years
