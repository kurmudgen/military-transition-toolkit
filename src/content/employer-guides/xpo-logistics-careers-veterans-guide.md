# XPO Logistics Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 38,000+ employees
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: XPO Veterans Initiative, Career Development Programs
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: LTL Driver, Dock Worker, Operations Supervisor, Fleet Technician, Sales
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $45,000 - $100,000+ depending on role
- **LTL Focus**: North America's largest less-than-truckload (LTL) carrier
- **Local Routes**: Many positions offer daily home time

XPO is the largest LTL (less-than-truckload) freight carrier in North America. Veterans find excellent opportunities in driving, operations, and management roles. LTL operations often provide better home time than traditional trucking, with many drivers home daily.

## Company Overview

XPO provides freight transportation services with a focus on less-than-truckload (LTL) shipping. LTL combines shipments from multiple customers on single trucks, making efficient use of capacity. XPO operates an extensive North American network of service centers.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 38,000+
- **Annual Revenue**: $8+ billion
- **Service Centers**: 380+ across North America
- **Power Units**: 13,000+
- **Trailers**: 36,000+

### Culture for Veterans

XPO's culture emphasizes safety, service excellence, and teamwork. The operational environment—with structured processes, clear metrics, and 24/7 operations—feels familiar to veterans. XPO values the discipline, reliability, and leadership veterans bring.

## What Veterans Should Know

### Pros

1. **Home Time**: Many LTL routes are local/regional
2. **Day Cab Work**: Home most nights
3. **Benefits**: Comprehensive package
4. **Career Growth**: Operations management paths
5. **Stability**: Essential freight industry
6. **Training**: Development opportunities
7. **Multiple Roles**: Not just driving

### Cons

1. **Physical Work**: Dock work is demanding
2. **Early Hours**: Many routes start pre-dawn
3. **Variable Schedule**: Freight volume varies
4. **Dock Requirements**: Some drivers touch freight
5. **Split Operations**: Pickup and delivery model

## Requirements

### Education
- Dock/Driver: High school diploma
- Operations: Associates helpful
- Management: Bachelor's preferred

### Certifications
- CDL Class A or B (driving)
- Forklift certification (dock)
- DOT Medical Card
- HAZMAT (some positions)

### Physical Requirements
- Lift 50+ lbs
- Extended standing (dock)
- Sit for extended periods (drivers)
- Work in varying conditions

### Experience
- Dock: 0 years (training provided)
- Driver: CDL + clean record
- Management: 3+ years relevant
