# Meta Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 70,000+ employees globally
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: Meta Military, Veterans@ ERG
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Technical Program Manager, Data Center Technician, Operations, Recruiting, Policy
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $80,000 - $250,000+ depending on role
- **Compensation**: Among the highest in tech with significant RSU components
- **Culture**: Fast-paced, impact-focused, "move fast" mentality suits veterans

Meta (formerly Facebook) offers top-tier compensation and a fast-paced environment where veterans can make immediate impact. Their "move fast" culture aligns well with military decisiveness, and their data centers and operations roles are particularly veteran-friendly.

## Company Overview

Meta Platforms develops products connecting billions of people through Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger. They're also investing heavily in the metaverse through Reality Labs, building VR/AR hardware and experiences.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 70,000+ globally
- **Annual Revenue**: $135+ billion
- **Market Cap**: $1.2+ trillion
- **Monthly Active Users**: 3+ billion across family of apps
- **Data Centers**: 20+ data center locations globally

### Culture for Veterans

Meta's culture emphasizes moving fast, being bold, and focusing on impact. The flat organizational structure gives individuals significant autonomy and responsibility—something veterans transitioning from hierarchical structures may need to adapt to, but often find liberating. The mission-driven aspect ("give people the power to build community") resonates with service-oriented veterans.

## Veteran Hiring Programs

### Meta Military

Meta's dedicated military program includes:
- Veteran recruiting specialists
- Military skills translation
- Resume review and interview prep
- Connections to veteran employees

### Veterans@ Employee Resource Group

Meta's veteran ERG provides:
- Mentorship programs
- Career development resources
- Community and networking
- Advocacy for veteran hiring
- Executive sponsorship

### Data Center Veterans Program

Special recruitment focus for data center roles:
- No degree required
- Paid training provided
- Veterans prioritized for operations roles
- Clear career progression

### Hiring Our Heroes Partnership

Meta participates in the Corporate Fellowship program, offering 12-week fellowships to transitioning service members with potential conversion to full-time roles.

## Application Process

### Step 1: Find Open Positions

**Where to Look:**
- Primary: metacareers.com
- LinkedIn: Meta + military/veteran filter
- Data center specific: metacareers.com/areas-of-work/infrastructure
- Military recruiting team on LinkedIn

**Keywords to Search:**
- "Technical Program Manager"
- "Data Center"
- "Operations"
- "Trust & Safety"
- "Policy"
- "Recruiting"

**Best Times to Apply:**
- Q1 (January-March): Post-holiday hiring push
- After earnings reports: Often announces hiring plans
- Continuous for data centers

### Step 2: Resume & Application

**What Meta Looks For:**
- Impact and scale (numbers, metrics)
- Ability to move fast and iterate
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Problem-solving and resourcefulness
- "Boldness"—willingness to take smart risks

**Resume Format:**
- One page strongly preferred
- Impact-focused bullets
- Quantifiable achievements
- Show scale when possible

**Resume Keywords That Work:**
- "Scaled from X to Y"
- "Impact: X users/dollars/percentage"
- "Built/launched/shipped"
- "Led cross-functional team"
- "Moved fast to deliver"

**Important:**
- Demonstrate impact at scale
- Show bias toward action
- Highlight collaboration

### Step 3: Interview Process

**Typical Interview Stages:**
1. Application Review
2. Recruiter Screen (30-45 minutes)
3. Technical Screen (for technical roles)
4. Onsite/Virtual Loop (4-5 interviews)
5. Hiring Committee Review
6. Offer

**Interview Format:**
- **Behavioral**: STAR method focusing on impact
- **Technical**: Role-specific assessments
- **Values/Culture**: Meta's cultural values alignment
- **Case Studies**: For PM and operational roles

**Common Questions:**
- "Tell me about your biggest impact in your previous role"
- "How do you prioritize when everything is important?"
- "Describe a time you had to move fast without complete information"
- "Tell me about a time you failed and how you handled it"
- "How would you explain [Meta product] to someone who's never used it?"

**What They Value:**
- Bias toward action
- Scale thinking
- Open communication
- Building social value
- Moving fast

### Step 4: Offer & Negotiation

**Typical Offer Timeline:**
- Hiring Committee: 1-2 weeks after interviews
- Verbal offer: Days after HC approval
- Written offer: 1-2 days after verbal

**Compensation Package:**
- Base salary (high)
- Annual bonus (10-25%)
- RSUs (significant component, vest quarterly)
- Sign-on bonus (often large)
- 401(k) match

**Negotiation Tips:**
- Meta pays competitively and there's room to negotiate
- Research levels.fyi carefully
- Stock is significant—negotiate RSU grants
- Level matters more than base salary
- Sign-on bonus is often negotiable

## Requirements

### Education
- Data Center: No degree required
- Technical roles: CS degree or equivalent experience
- Non-technical: Bachelor's preferred but not always required
- Military experience often accepted in lieu of traditional education

### Certifications
- Networking (CCNA) helpful for data center
- Security certifications for security roles
- Project management (PMP) for PM roles

### Clearance
- Most roles: No clearance needed
- Some government-facing roles may prefer cleared candidates
- Note clearance status on resume

### Experience
- IC3/IC4 (Entry): 0-3 years
- IC5 (Mid): 3-7 years
- IC6 (Senior): 7-12 years
- IC7+ (Staff): 12+ years
