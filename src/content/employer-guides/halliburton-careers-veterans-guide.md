# Halliburton Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Company Size**: 45,000+ employees globally
- **Veteran Hiring Program**: Halliburton Veterans Initiative, Military Skills Translation
- **Top 5 Roles for Veterans**: Field Operator, Technician, Engineer, Supervisor, HSE
- **Average Starting Salary Range**: $55,000 - $170,000+ depending on role
- **Oilfield Leader**: One of world's largest oilfield services companies
- **Field Operations**: Technical field work mirrors military deployment tempo

Halliburton is one of the world's largest oilfield services companies, providing products and services for the entire lifecycle of oil and gas reservoirs. Veterans with mechanical, technical, and leadership backgrounds find excellent opportunities in field operations that value military discipline and adaptability.

## Company Overview

Halliburton provides a broad range of oilfield services including drilling, evaluation, completion, production, and intervention services. They operate across two divisions: Completion and Production, and Drilling and Evaluation.

### By the Numbers
- **Employees**: 45,000+ globally
- **Annual Revenue**: $23+ billion
- **Countries**: Operations in 70+ countries
- **Product Service Lines**: 14 major service lines
- **Technology Centers**: Global R&D facilities

### Culture for Veterans

Halliburton's culture emphasizes safety, operational excellence, and getting the job done. Field operations in remote locations under demanding conditions align with military operations. Veterans find their technical skills, adaptability, and leadership valued. The company has a long history of hiring veterans.

## Application Process

### Step 1: Find Open Positions

**Where to Look:**
- Primary: jobs.halliburton.com
- LinkedIn: Halliburton
- Indeed for specific locations
- Oil & gas job boards

**Keywords to Search:**
- "Operator"
- "Field Engineer"
- "Technician"
- "Frac" or "Cementing"
- Specific service line names

**Best Times to Apply:**
- Year-round hiring
- Activity increases with oil prices
- New contract awards

### Step 2: Resume & Application

**What Halliburton Looks For:**
- Technical/mechanical aptitude
- Willingness for field work
- CDL or ability to obtain
- Safety consciousness
- Physical fitness

**Resume Format:**
- Technical experience prominent
- Equipment operation highlighted
- CDL/driving record noted
- Physical work history

**Resume Keywords That Work:**
- "Equipment operation"
- "Mechanical/technical"
- "CDL/driving"
- "Field operations"
- "Safety compliance"

**Important:**
- Emphasize equipment experience
- Highlight willingness for travel
- Note CDL status or willingness to obtain

### Step 3: Interview Process

**Typical Interview Stages:**
1. Online Application
2. Phone Screen
3. Interview with Hiring Manager
4. Physical/Drug Testing
5. Offer

**Common Questions:**
- "Why Halliburton / oilfield services?"
- "Tell me about your mechanical experience"
- "Are you comfortable with extensive travel?"
- "Describe working in challenging conditions"
- "Do you have CDL or can you obtain one?"

**What They Value:**
- Reliability
- Technical aptitude
- Physical fitness
- Safety commitment
- Adaptability

### Step 4: Offer & Negotiation

**Typical Offer Timeline:**
- Field roles: 1-3 weeks
- Engineering: 3-6 weeks
- Management: 4-8 weeks

**Compensation Package:**
- Competitive base salary
- Overtime (significant in field)
- Per diem for travel
- 401(k) with match
- Healthcare
- Training/certifications

**Negotiation Tips:**
- Field premiums are standard
- Overtime significantly increases earnings
- CDL holders may command premium
- Ask about schedule/location preferences

## Requirements

### Education
- Operator: High school diploma
- Technician: Technical training preferred
- Engineer: Bachelor's in engineering
- Management: Bachelor's typically required

### Certifications
- CDL Class A (many field roles)
- H2S safety certification
- OSHA safety training
- Technical certifications by role

### Physical Requirements
- Lift 50-100 lbs regularly
- Work long hours (12+ hour shifts)
- Work in all weather conditions
- Pass drug screening
- Pass physical exam

### Experience
- Operator: Entry level accepted
- Technician: Technical/mechanical background
- Engineer: Engineering degree
- Supervisor: 3-5 years field experience
