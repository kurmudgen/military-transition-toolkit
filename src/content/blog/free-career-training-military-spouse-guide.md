# Free Career Training for Military Spouses: All Programs Guide

## Bottom Line Up Front

Multiple organizations offer **completely free career training** to military spouses—no loans, no repayment, no cost to you. Programs range from tech bootcamps to professional certifications to full fellowships with corporate partners. Combined, these resources represent **millions of dollars** in training available exclusively to military families. This guide lists every major free program, eligibility requirements, and how to apply.

## The Military Spouse Career Challenge

Free training removes the biggest barrier to career advancement. Why pay for education when these options exist?

**What's Available for Free:**
- Tech training and bootcamps
- Professional certifications
- Corporate fellowships with job placement
- Skill-building courses
- Career coaching and support

**The Catch:**
Most programs have limited spots, competitive admission, or specific eligibility. Apply early and to multiple programs.

## Major Free Training Programs

### Hiring Our Heroes Programs

**Hiring Our Heroes Corporate Fellowship Program**

**What It Is:** 12-week fellowship at major corporations with salary and job offer potential

**Cost:** Free (you receive a stipend/salary)

**What You Get:**
- 12 weeks at host company
- Real work experience
- Mentorship
- High conversion to job offers (80%+)
- Full salary during fellowship

**Companies Include:** Amazon, Microsoft, Humana, USAA, and 100+ others

**Eligibility:**
- Active duty spouses
- Must be able to complete full fellowship
- Competitive selection process

**How to Apply:**
- hiringourheroes.org
- Applications typically open quarterly
- Multiple tracks (tech, business, skilled trades)

---

## How to Apply Strategically

### Application Strategy

1. **Apply to Multiple Programs**
   - Most are competitive
   - Different timelines and deadlines
   - More applications = more opportunities

2. **Start with Federal Benefits**
   - MyCAA if eligible
   - GI Bill if transferred
   - These are guaranteed for eligible spouses

3. **Add Corporate Programs**
   - Hiring Our Heroes fellowships
   - Corporate scholarship programs
   - Often include job placement

4. **Supplement with Free Resources**
   - Online courses (Coursera, edX)
   - LinkedIn Learning
   - YouTube tutorials

### Timeline

- **3-6 months before training:** Research and apply to competitive programs
- **1-3 months before:** Apply to less competitive programs
- **Ongoing:** Use free online resources while waiting
