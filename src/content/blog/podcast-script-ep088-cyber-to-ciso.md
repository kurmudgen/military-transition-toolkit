[INTRO]
Major Alex Thompson, Air Force Cyber Warfare Officer with 12 years experience in offensive/defensive cyber operations, became Chief Information Security Officer for mid-size financial services company earning $180K+ equity within 6 years of military service.

[MAIN CONTENT]
**Military:** 17D Cyber Operations Officer, managed cyber defense/offense teams, held multiple cyber certifications (CISSP, CEH, GIAC), led incident response operations, TS/SCI.
**Transition:** Hired as Security Engineer at defense contractor ($120K), promoted to Security Manager ($145K), recruited as CISO ($180K+ equity) 6 years post-military.
**Career Path:** Defense contractor → Fortune 1000 Security Manager → Mid-size company CISO. Strategy: build diverse experience before targeting executive roles.
**Keys:** Military cyber experience + clearance opened defense sector, technical + leadership skills enabled rapid progression, cyber shortage accelerated career advancement.

[CONCLUSION]
Alex went from Cyber Warfare Officer to $180K+ CISO in 6 years through strategic career progression. Military cyber experience is golden ticket in corporate cybersecurity leadership.
