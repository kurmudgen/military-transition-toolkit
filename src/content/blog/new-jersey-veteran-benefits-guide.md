# New Jersey Veteran Benefits: Complete State Programs Guide (2025)

New Jersey honors its 350,000+ veterans with substantial state benefits including complete property tax exemptions for disabled veterans, full military retirement income exclusion, and education assistance. Despite being known for high taxes, New Jersey provides meaningful tax relief for veterans.

This guide covers all New Jersey veteran benefits with eligibility requirements and application procedures.

## Property Tax Exemptions

### 100% Disabled Veteran Property Tax Exemption

**Complete Property Tax Exemption:**
- **Exemption:** 100% of property taxes
- **Savings:** Often $8,000-$15,000+ annually (NJ has high property taxes)
- **Coverage:** Primary residence

**Eligibility:**
- 100% permanent and total service-connected disability
- OR Active duty wartime veteran rated 100% P&T
- Own and occupy home as primary residence
- New Jersey resident

**This is one of the most valuable disabled veteran benefits in the country due to NJ's high property tax rates.**

### Surviving Spouse Exemption

**Surviving Spouse of Qualifying Veteran:**
- Complete property tax exemption continues
- Must have been married at time of veteran's death
- Must remain unremarried
- Must continue occupying the home

### $250 Veteran Property Tax Deduction

**All Wartime Veterans:**
- $250 annual property tax deduction
- Applies to all qualifying veterans
- In addition to other exemptions

**Eligibility:**
- Wartime service
- Honorable discharge
- New Jersey resident
- Own property in NJ

### How to Apply

**For 100% Exemption:**
1. Obtain VA letter certifying 100% permanent and total disability
2. Contact your municipal Tax Assessor
3. Complete Form V.S.S. (Veteran Status Sheet)
4. Submit DD-214 and VA letter
5. Apply by October 1 for following tax year

**For $250 Deduction:**
1. File with municipal Tax Assessor
2. Provide DD-214
3. One-time application (unless you move)

## Income Tax Benefits

### Military Retirement Pay Exclusion

**New Jersey Tax Benefit:**
- Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from NJ income tax
- Complete exclusion for all military retirees
- No age or income restrictions

**Active Duty Pay:**
- Active duty military pay is also exempt from NJ income tax

### Combat Pay

**Tax-Free:**
- Combat zone pay exempt (federal and state)

## Education Benefits

### NJ Veterans Tuition Credit Program

**Benefit:**
- Up to $400 per year at NJ public colleges
- Undergraduate study only

**Eligibility:**
- Veteran of US Armed Forces
- New Jersey resident
- Enrolled at NJ public college

### POW/MIA Tuition Benefit

**Children of POW/MIA:**
- Free tuition at NJ public colleges
- Full tuition waiver

### National Guard Tuition Waiver

**NJ National Guard:**
- Tuition waiver at NJ public colleges
- For eligible Guard members

### In-State Tuition for Veterans

**All Veterans:**
- Qualify for in-state tuition regardless of prior residency
- Effective immediately upon enrollment
- Covers all NJ public colleges and universities

**GI Bill Users:**
- In-state tuition guaranteed under federal law

## Vehicle Benefits

### Disabled Veteran License Plates

**Free Plates for:**
- Disabled Veterans (various levels)
- Purple Heart recipients
- Medal of Honor recipients
- Former POWs
- Gold Star families

**Additional Benefits:**
- Free registration (100% disabled)
- Free plate
- Toll exemptions on NJ Turnpike and Garden State Parkway (for some plates)

### E-ZPass Toll Exemptions

**100% Disabled Veterans:**
- May qualify for toll exemptions

1. Visit NJ MVC
2. Complete Form BA-49
3. Provide DD-214 and VA documentation

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does New Jersey tax military retirement pay?

No. New Jersey fully exempts military retirement pay from state income tax.

### How much can I save with the property tax exemption?

100% disabled veterans can save $8,000-$15,000+ annually due to NJ's high property tax rates. This is one of the most valuable state benefits in the country.

### What's the $250 deduction?

All wartime veterans receive a $250 annual property tax deduction in addition to any other exemptions.

### How do I get free hunting/fishing licenses?

Contact NJ Division of Fish and Wildlife with proof of 100% service-connected disability.

### Are toll exemptions available?

100% disabled veterans may qualify for toll exemptions on NJ Turnpike and Garden State Parkway with proper plates.

New Jersey offers exceptional benefits for disabled veterans. Combined with full military retirement income exclusion, NJ provides significant tax relief.
