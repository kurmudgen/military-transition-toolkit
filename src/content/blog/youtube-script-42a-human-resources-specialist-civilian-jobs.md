**Section 6: HR Certifications (Career Multipliers) (90 seconds)**

[Visual suggestion: Certification ROI comparison]

Certifications dramatically increase your salary.

**Entry-level certification:**

**SHRM-CP (Society for HR Management - Certified Professional):**
- Cost: $300-$400 exam + $200 study materials
- Salary impact: $8,000-$15,000 increase
- Industry standard
- Shows you know HR beyond military

**PHR (Professional in Human Resources):**
- Cost: $395 exam
- Salary impact: $8,000-$12,000 increase
- Alternative to SHRM-CP
- HRCI certification

**Senior certifications:**

**SHRM-SCP (Senior Certified Professional):**
- Cost: $425 exam
- Requires more experience
- Salary impact: $15,000-$25,000 increase
- For HR managers and directors

**SPHR (Senior Professional in Human Resources):**
- Cost: $495 exam
- Strategic HR focus
- Salary impact: $15,000-$25,000 increase

**Specialized certifications:**
- CCP (Certified Compensation Professional): $600, focuses on pay
- PHRca (California-specific): For CA jobs
- GPHR (Global PHR): For international companies

**How to get them:**
- Study 2-3 months while working
- GI Bill may cover prep courses
- Many employers reimburse exam costs
- DoD COOL might cover (check)

**Timeline:** Get SHRM-CP within first year of civilian HR work. Huge ROI.
