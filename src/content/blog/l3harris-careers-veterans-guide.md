# L3Harris Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

L3Harris Technologies was formed in 2019 from the merger of L3 Technologies and Harris Corporation, creating one of the world's largest defense and aerospace companies. Specializing in communications, electronic warfare, and intelligence/surveillance/reconnaissance (ISR) systems, L3Harris is where military communications expertise translates directly to civilian careers.

With approximately 50,000 employees and $18+ billion in annual revenue, L3Harris offers veterans exceptional opportunities—especially those with signal, communications, or electronic systems backgrounds.
