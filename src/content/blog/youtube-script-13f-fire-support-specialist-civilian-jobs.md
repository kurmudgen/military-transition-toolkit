[MAIN CONTENT]

**Section 1: Market Value (45 seconds)**

Entry: $45,000-$60,000/year | Mid-level: $60,000-$85,000/year | Senior: $75,000-$100,000/year

Your skills: Call for fire procedures, target acquisition, communications coordination, map reading, digital systems = valuable civilian applications.

**Section 2: Emergency Services Dispatch (75 seconds)**

**911 Dispatcher/Emergency Communications:** $40,000-$65,000/year. Coordinate police/fire/EMS response. Your CFF experience = direct parallel. Manage multiple radio frequencies, prioritize emergencies, maintain calm under pressure.

**Fire/EMS Dispatcher:** $45,000-$70,000/year. Government benefits + pension. Major cities pay more (NYC, LA, Chicago $60K-$80K).

**Requirements:** Usually certification course (3-6 months), often paid. Your military comms = advantage.

**Section 3: Air Traffic Control (75 seconds)**

**FAA Air Traffic Controller:** $60,000-$140,000/year. Control aircraft movement. Your target tracking and coordination skills translate. Extremely stressful but well-paid.

**Requirements:** FAA Academy (Oklahoma City, 3-5 months, government pays), under age 31 to start, pass security clearance and medical. Military experience gives veterans' preference.

**Tower Controller:** $70,000-$100,000/year. Control takeoffs/landings at airports.

**En Route Controller:** $85,000-$140,000/year. Control aircraft between airports. Higher pay, more complex.

**Section 4: Defense Contractors (75 seconds)**

**Fire Support Coordinator:** Support artillery/fires training. $65,000-$95,000/year. Work at military installations, training ranges.

**Targeting Analyst:** Defense intelligence contractors. $70,000-$105,000/year. Analyze enemy targets for military planning. Clearance required.

**Training Instructor:** Teach call for fire procedures. $60,000-$90,000/year. Companies supporting military training.

**Simulation Specialist:** Fire support simulators/training systems. $65,000-$95,000/year.

**Top companies:** CACI, Booz Allen, Cubic, Sabre, defense training contractors.

**Section 5: Law Enforcement Roles (60 seconds)**

**Police Dispatcher:** $40,000-$60,000/year. Similar to 911 but police-specific.

**Sheriff Communications:** $42,000-$65,000/year. Often better benefits than city dispatch.

**Tactical Dispatcher:** SWAT/tactical teams. $50,000-$70,000/year. Your understanding of tactical operations valuable.

**Section 6: Alternative Paths (60 seconds)**

**Drone/UAV Operator:** Commercial drone operations. $50,000-$85,000/year. Your targeting experience helps. Requires FAA Part 107 license ($175).

**Logistics Coordinator:** Coordinate shipments, resources. $45,000-$70,000/year. Your coordination skills transfer.

**GIS Specialist:** Geographic information systems. $55,000-$85,000/year. Map analysis skills valuable. May need certifications/degree.

**Project Coordinator:** Corporate project management. $50,000-$75,000/year. Coordination and communication skills.
