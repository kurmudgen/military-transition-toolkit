# Peraton Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

Peraton is a national security company formed through the merger of Perspecta, Northrop Grumman's federal IT and mission support business, and other acquisitions. This relatively new entity (formed 2021) has quickly become a major player in space, intelligence, cyber, and mission support.

With approximately 14,000 employees and $7+ billion in annual revenue, Peraton offers veterans—especially those with space, intelligence, or cyber backgrounds—career opportunities in some of the most critical national security missions.

## Peraton Hiring Process

### Step 1: Apply

**Where to apply:**
- peraton.com/careers
- ClearanceJobs
- LinkedIn
- Indeed

### Step 2: Screening

Review for:
- Clearance status (critical)
- Technical qualifications
- Experience match
- Security requirements

### Step 3: Interviews

Multiple rounds:

**Phone Screen:**
- Clearance verification
- Background discussion
- Interest confirmation

**Technical Interview:**
- Skills assessment
- Domain expertise
- Problem-solving

**Manager/Panel Interview:**
- Team fit
- Behavioral assessment
- Career discussion

### Step 4: Offer

If selected:
- Compensation negotiation
- Benefits review
- Start date
- Security processing

### Timeline

Typical process:
- Application to response: 1-3 weeks
- Interview process: 2-4 weeks
- Offer to start: Depends on clearance status

Active TS/SCI enables faster starts.
