## Contingency Plans (If Behind Schedule)

### If You Fall Behind on Content Publishing

**Option 1: Prioritize Quality Over Quantity**
- Focus on top 500 pages instead of 1,600
- Ensure those 500 are perfectly optimized
- Better to rank well with 500 than rank poorly with 1,600

**Option 2: Simplify Content**
- Reduce word count target (1,000 words instead of 1,500)
- Focus on core information only
- Add depth later (after initial publish)

**Option 3: Hire Help**
- Hire freelance writers (Upwork, Fiverr)
- You handle optimization, they handle first drafts
- Cost: $20-50 per 1,000-word article

### If Link Building Isn't Working

**Focus on:**
- Tool creation (linkable assets get links passively)
- Original research (journalists will link)
- Quality over quantity (1 .gov link > 10 low-quality links)

### If Traffic Is Lower Than Expected

**Diagnose:**
- Are pages indexed? (Check GSC Coverage report)
- Are you ranking? (Check GSC Performance report)
- Is content quality sufficient? (Compare to competitors)
- Are you targeting right keywords? (Check search volume)

**Fix:**
- Improve on-page SEO (titles, headers, keyword optimization)
- Build more backlinks (increases authority)
- Optimize for featured snippets (quick CTR boost)
- Share on social media (drive initial traffic)
