# Lockheed Martin Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

Lockheed Martin is the world's largest defense contractor, and they need you. With over $67 billion in annual revenue and 116,000+ employees, Lockheed builds the F-35, missile defense systems, space vehicles, and countless other systems critical to national security.

For veterans—especially those with aviation, weapons systems, or technical backgrounds—Lockheed Martin represents one of the premier career destinations in the defense industry.
