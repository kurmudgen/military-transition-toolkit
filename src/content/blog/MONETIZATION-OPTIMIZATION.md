### Pricing Experiments

#### Experiment 1: Test Higher Price Point (Month 3)

**Hypothesis**: Some users would pay more for "premium" features

**Test**: Introduce "Pro" tier at $15/month
- All standard features
- Plus: 1-on-1 coaching call/month
- Plus: Priority support
- Plus: Early access to new features

**Success Criteria**: 10%+ of new users choose Pro tier

**Impact**: If 100 users/month choose Pro instead of Standard:
- Additional revenue: $800/month ($15-$7 = $8 × 100)

#### Experiment 2: Increase Lifetime Price (Month 4)

**Hypothesis**: Lifetime is underpriced at $249

**Current Math**:
- $249 lifetime
- Average user stays 36 months
- Effective monthly: $249 / 36 = $6.92/mo (less than monthly plan!)

**Test**: Increase to $349 or $449
- Position as "Founding Member" pricing for current users
- New users pay higher price
- Grandfather existing lifetime buyers

**Impact**:
- $349: +$100 per lifetime sale
- $449: +$200 per lifetime sale
- If 10 lifetime sales/month: +$1,000-$2,000 additional revenue

#### Experiment 3: Remove Free Trial, Add Money-Back Guarantee (Month 6)

**Hypothesis**: Free trial creates "tire-kickers" who never intended to pay

**Current**: 7-day free trial, no credit card required
- 500 trials/month
- 40% convert = 200 paid
- 60% never pay = 300 "free riders"

**Test**: No free trial, 30-day money-back guarantee, credit card required
- Friction increases, trial starts drop to 300/month
- But conversion increases to 70% = 210 paid
- Net: +10 paid users, fewer support costs

**Risk**: Could backfire if conversion drops more than expected

**Mitigation**: Test on 20% of traffic, measure impact before rolling out

## Lifetime Value (LTV) Optimization

### Current LTV Calculations

**Monthly Subscriber**:
- Price: $7/month
- Average retention: 12 months
- LTV: $7 × 12 = $84

**Annual Subscriber**:
- Price: $49/year
- Average retention: 24 months (2 renewals)
- LTV: $49 × 2 = $98

**Lifetime Subscriber**:
- Price: $249 one-time
- LTV: $249 (plus potential upsells)

**Blended LTV** (60% monthly, 25% annual, 15% lifetime):
- (0.60 × $84) + (0.25 × $98) + (0.15 × $249)
- $50.40 + $24.50 + $37.35
- **= $112.25**

---

### LTV Optimization Strategies

#### 1. Increase Retention (Biggest Impact)

**Current**: 12-month average retention
**Target**: 18-month average retention (+50%)

**Tactics**:
- Better onboarding (drive to aha moment faster)
- Feature adoption campaigns (sticky features = retention)
- Community building (hard to leave community)
- Regular value delivery (new content monthly)

**Impact on LTV**:
- Monthly: $7 × 18 = $126 (vs $84) = +$42 LTV (+50%)
- Annual: $49 × 3 = $147 (vs $98) = +$49 LTV (+50%)
- **New Blended LTV**: $168 (+50%)

**Revenue Impact**:
- 1,000 customers × $168 LTV = $168,000 lifetime revenue
- vs 1,000 × $112 = $112,000
- **+$56,000 additional revenue (+50%!)**

---

#### 2. Increase ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)

**Tactic**: Upsell and cross-sell

**Upsells**:
- Monthly → Annual (convert 30% of monthly to annual)
- Standard → Premium tier (convert 20% to $15/mo tier)
- Add-ons: 1-on-1 coaching session ($100 each)

**Cross-Sells**:
- MTT → SpouseForce (20% of MTT users have spouses)
- MTT → Certification courses (10% buy course)
- MTT → Premium content ($49 one-time guides)

**Impact**:
- Base: $7/month
- Upsells: +$3/month average (weighted by conversion)
- Cross-sells: +$2/month average (weighted by conversion)
- **New ARPU**: $12/month (vs $7) = +71%

**New LTV**: $12 × 18 months = $216 (vs $126)

---

#### 3. Reduce Churn

**Current Churn**: 8% monthly = 12-month average retention
**Target Churn**: 5% monthly = 20-month average retention

**Tactics**:
- Early warning system (re-engage dormant users)
- Win-back campaigns (save canceling users)
- Improved payment recovery (failed payments)
- Annual plan push (annual churn is lower)

**Impact on LTV**:
- Monthly: $7 × 20 = $140 (vs $84) = +$56 LTV (+67%)
