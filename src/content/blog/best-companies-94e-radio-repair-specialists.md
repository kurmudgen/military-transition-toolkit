## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which company pays highest for 94E specialists?**
A: Cisco and Qualcomm lead in tech ($95,000-$140,000 year 5). Year 10+ earning potential exceeds $150,000-$200,000. L3Harris competitive ($130,000 year 5+).

**Q: Should I choose tech (Cisco) or defense contractor?**
A: **Tech**: Higher long-term compensation ($200,000+), faster advancement, stock options, better work-life. **Defense**: Military background valued, clearance advantage, stable growth. Both excellent options.

**Q: What certifications should I pursue?**
A: FCC Professional Engineer License provides 15-20% salary boost. CompTIA certifications helpful for network roles. RF-specific certifications vary by employer.

**Q: How quickly can I advance to senior engineer/supervisor?**
A: Cisco/tech: 4-6 years to senior roles. Defense: 5-7 years. Fast performers advance faster.

**Q: What's the job outlook?**
A: Excellent. 5G/6G deployment creating sustained demand. Network infrastructure upgrading nationwide. Skilled RF technicians in high demand.

**Q: Are there remote opportunities?**
A: Limited for field technicians (on-site required). Engineering and planning roles increasingly remote. Network operations roles can be remote.
