# KBR Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

KBR, Inc. is a global engineering, construction, and services company with deep roots in defense and government services. Known for providing base operations support, logistics, and engineering services to the U.S. military worldwide, KBR has been a major employer of veterans for decades.

With approximately 30,000 employees and $7+ billion in annual revenue, KBR offers veterans—especially those with logistics, maintenance, engineering, or operations backgrounds—career opportunities both domestically and internationally.

### OCONUS/Deployed Positions

Significant uplift for deployed work:
- **Base salary:** Starting point
- **Danger pay:** Where applicable
- **Hardship differential:** Challenging locations
- **Foreign Earned Income Exclusion:** Tax benefit potential
- **Housing/meals:** Often provided
- **R&R travel:** Typically included

**Total compensation can be 50-100%+ higher than equivalent CONUS positions**
