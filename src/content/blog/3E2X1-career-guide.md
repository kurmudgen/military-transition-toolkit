---# 3E2X1 - Pavements & Construction Equipment Career Transition Guide

## Overview

Pavements & Construction Equipment specialists operate heavy equipment, perform earthmoving, paving, grading, and maintain construction equipment for Air Force infrastructure projects.

**Daily:** Operate dozers, graders, excavators, pavers, compactors, perform earthwork, grading, paving, equipment maintenance, blueprints, surveying.

## Civilian Paths

**Heavy Equipment Operator:** Construction sites. $45K-$85K. Union scale higher. Strong demand. Recession follows construction but rebounds.
**Grading Contractor:** Site preparation specialist. $50K-$90K. Can start own grading business.
**Paving Operator:** Asphalt/concrete paving. $48K-$88K. Seasonal in some areas but high earning potential.
**Equipment Maintenance:** Construction equipment mechanic. $45K-$80K. Keep heavy equipment running.
**Survey Technician:** Construction surveying/grading. $40K-$70K. Less physical alternative.

## Certifications

**NCCCO (National Commission for Certification of Crane Operators):** Crane operator certification. Opens specialized high-paying roles.
**NCCER (National Center for Construction Education & Research):** Heavy equipment operator certifications.
**CDL (Commercial Driver's License):** Often required to transport equipment. Class A or B.

## Salary

| Experience | Non-Union | Union | Equipment Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| Entry Operator | $35K-$50K | $45K-$60K | General |
| Journeyman | $50K-$75K | $65K-$90K | Specialized |
| Master Operator | $65K-$90K | $80K-$105K | Crane/Tower |
| Foreman/Contractor | $75K-$120K+ | $90K-$140K+ | Leadership |

**Union Premium:** Operating Engineers union (IUOE) pays significantly better with excellent benefits.
**Overtime:** Construction often 50-60 hour weeks during season. OT boosts earnings 30-40%.

## Top Companies

Bechtel, Kiewit, Fluor, Granite Construction, heavy civil contractors, DOT contractors, mining companies, utilities, equipment rental companies (United Rentals, Sunbelt).

## Action Plan

Join Operating Engineers union (IUOE) for best pay/benefits. Get CDL. Consider specialized equipment (cranes pay premium). Apply to heavy civil contractors.
