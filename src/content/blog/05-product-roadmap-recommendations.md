---# Product Roadmap Recommendations

**Based on Competitive Analysis - Prioritized by Impact & Feasibility**

---

## Framework: Effort vs. Impact Matrix

```
HIGH IMPACT, LOW EFFORT = Quick Wins (Do First)
HIGH IMPACT, HIGH EFFORT = Strategic Bets (Plan & Execute)
LOW IMPACT, LOW EFFORT = Fill-Ins (When capacity available)
LOW IMPACT, HIGH EFFORT = Avoid
```

---

## TIER 1: QUICK WINS (1-2 Days Dev Time Each)

**Execute Immediately - High Impact, Low Effort**

### 1. Community Q&A Forum ‚ö° **TOP PRIORITY**
**Why:** Veterans desperately need peer support. Nobody offers comprehensive forum.
**Impact:** Massive differentiation + network effects
**Effort:** Use existing forum software (Discourse, Flarum) - 1-2 days setup
**Competitive Advantage:** Only comprehensive platform with active community
**Metrics:** DAU, questions asked/answered, time on site

### 2. State Benefits Comparison Landing Pages üåü **UNIQUE FEATURE**
**Why:** Nobody else has this. High SEO value. Critical decision factor for relocating veterans.
**Impact:** Drive organic traffic, unique value prop
**Effort:** State-by-state pages with structured data (can start with top 10 states)
**Example:** "/state-benefits/texas-vs-florida-veterans" = high-value content
**Competitive Advantage:** MTT is ONLY source for this comparison
**Metrics:** Organic search traffic, conversions to signup

### 3. Improve VA Claims Builder UX üåü **KILLER FEATURE**
**Why:** This is MTT's #1 differentiator. Must be perfect.
**Current State:** Functional but could be more intuitive
**Improvements:**
- Add condition templates (e.g., "PTSD," "Tinnitus," "Back pain")
- Wizard-style step-by-step flow
- Auto-save progress (local storage)
- Print/export to PDF
**Effort:** 1-2 days UX improvements
**Impact:** Core feature optimization
**Metrics:** Completion rate, time to complete, user satisfaction

### 4. Job Board Integration (Aggregate External Feeds)
**Why:** Don't build job board from scratch. Pull from Military.com, VetJobs, Indeed.
**Impact:** Comprehensive job listings without building marketplace
**Effort:** RSS/API integration - 1-2 days
**Competitive Advantage:** Ad-free aggregation vs Military.com's ad-heavy experience
**Metrics:** Jobs viewed, applications clicked through

### 5. LinkedIn Profile Optimization Wizard
**Why:** Only Hire Heroes and LinkedIn itself offer this. Huge gap.
**Impact:** High-value feature, differentiates from most competitors
**Effort:** Step-by-step wizard with best practices - 1-2 days
**Competitive Advantage:** Free DIY version vs LinkedIn Premium's paywalled insights
**Metrics:** Wizard completions, LinkedIn profile strength score

### 6. Testimonials & Social Proof
**Why:** Build trust, show real results
**Impact:** Conversion rate optimization
**Effort:** Add testimonials section to homepage - 1 day
**Source:** Early users, Reddit, social media
**Metrics:** Conversion rate, trust indicators

### 7. Referral Program
**Why:** Word-of-mouth is #1 acquisition channel for veteran services
**Impact:** Viral growth loop
**Effort:** Simple referral tracking - 1-2 days
**Mechanics:** "Invite a battle buddy, both get premium free for 1 month"
**Metrics:** Referral rate, viral coefficient (K-factor)

## TIER 2: MEDIUM LIFTS (1-2 Weeks Dev Time Each)

**High Impact, Medium Effort - Execute Next Quarter**

### 8. Mobile App (React Native)
**Why:** Only Military.com, LinkedIn, Veterans United have apps. Huge gap.
**Impact:** Accessibility, push notifications, offline access
**Effort:** 1-2 weeks for MVP (React Native shares code with web)
**Features:**
- Transition checklist with push reminders
- VA claims builder (offline-first)
- Community forum access
- Job search
**Competitive Advantage:** Comprehensive transition app (others are single-purpose)
**Metrics:** App downloads, DAU, retention

### 9. Resume Builder v2 (ATS-Optimized Templates)
**Why:** CareerProPlus charges $500. Hire Heroes provides but with waitlist. Gap for DIY users.
**Current:** Basic templates
**v2 Features:**
- 10+ professional templates (ATS-optimized)
- Military-to-civilian skills translation (integrated MOS translator)
- Real-time feedback (keyword optimization, formatting)
- Export to PDF/Word
**Effort:** 1-2 weeks (design templates, build editor)
**Competitive Advantage:** Free, comprehensive, AI-powered vs $500 paid services
**Metrics:** Resumes created, downloads, job application success

### 10. Interview Prep AI Coach
**Why:** FourBlock, ACP offer human coaching. Expensive/limited. AI scales infinitely.
**Impact:** High-value feature, proven demand
**Effort:** 1-2 weeks (integrate AI API, build interface)
**Features:**
- Common interview questions by industry/role
- AI evaluates answers, provides feedback
- Practice sessions tracked
- Behavioral interview techniques (STAR method)
**Competitive Advantage:** Free unlimited practice vs limited human coaching
**Metrics:** Practice sessions completed, user ratings, job offer correlation

### 11. Salary & Benefits Comparison Calculator
**Why:** LinkedIn has salary data but doesn't factor in VA benefits, state taxes, etc.
**Impact:** High-value decision tool
**Effort:** 1-2 weeks (build calculator, data integration)
**Features:**
- Input job offers (salary, benefits, location)
- Factor in VA disability, state taxes, cost of living
- Total compensation comparison
- Retirement/savings projections
**Competitive Advantage:** ONLY tool factoring VA benefits into salary decisions
**Metrics:** Calculations run, offers compared, conversions

### 12. Partner Integrations (O2O, VetsinTech, Bunker Labs)
**Why:** Don't compete with specialized training. Integrate and refer.
**Impact:** Comprehensive ecosystem, partnership revenue
**Effort:** 1-2 weeks per integration
**Examples:**
- "Interested in tech? Check out VetsinTech's free training" (affiliate)
- "Want to start a business? Bunker Labs can help" (integration)
- "Need coaching? Book session with Hire Heroes" (marketplace)
**Competitive Advantage:** MTT becomes hub connecting all veteran resources
**Metrics:** Referrals sent, partnership revenue, user satisfaction

### 13. Application Tracker (Job Search Dashboard)
**Why:** Nobody offers this. Veterans apply to dozens of jobs, lose track.
**Impact:** Massive value-add, retention driver
**Effort:** 1 week (build tracking interface, integrations)
**Features:**
- Log applications (company, role, date, status)
- Set reminders (follow up after 1 week)
- Track outcomes (interview, offer, rejection)
- Analytics (response rate, time to offer)
**Competitive Advantage:** ONLY platform with comprehensive application tracking
**Metrics:** Applications tracked, follow-ups completed, offers received

## TIER 3: STRATEGIC BETS (1-3 Months)

**High Impact, High Effort - Long-term Moat Builders**

### 14. AI-Powered MOS Translator & Job Matching
**Why:** Military.com and VetJobs have basic translators. AI can do it 10x better.
**Impact:** Best-in-class career matching
**Effort:** 2-3 months (train AI model, build matching algorithm)
**Features:**
- Enter MOS, get civilian job matches
- Skills extraction from DD-214/resume
- Personalized career path recommendations
- Salary projections by path
**Competitive Advantage:** Most accurate military-to-civilian translation
**Metrics:** Match quality (user ratings), job applications from matches, placements

### 15. VA Claims Expert Marketplace (Premium Service)
**Why:** Veterans will pay for expert claim review ($149 vs $3K VSO scams)
**Impact:** Revenue driver, high-value service
**Effort:** 2-3 months (vet experts, build marketplace, payment integration)
**Model:**
- MTT vets and onboards VA claims experts (VSOs, attorneys)
- Veterans pay $149 for expert review
- MTT takes 20-30% commission
- Expert reviews claim, provides feedback, helps file
**Competitive Advantage:** Affordable expert help (vs $3K+ scams or overworked free VSOs)
**Metrics:** Expert reviews purchased, claim approval rates, revenue

### 16. Local Veteran Chapters & Events
**Why:** VetsinTech (27 cities) and Bunker Labs have local presence. Community craves in-person.
**Impact:** Deep engagement, local network effects
**Effort:** 3+ months (recruit chapter leaders, organize events)
**Model:**
- Volunteer chapter leaders in major metros
- Monthly meetups (coffee, workshops, networking)
- MTT provides structure/materials, chapters execute
**Start:** 5-10 pilot cities (San Diego, Norfolk, San Antonio, DC, Colorado Springs)
**Competitive Advantage:** Online + offline hybrid community
**Metrics:** Chapter growth, event attendance, local DAU

### 17. Employer Partnerships & Job Placement
**Why:** FourBlock places at $100K+ avg. Hire Heroes has employer network. Revenue opportunity.
**Impact:** Revenue, placements, reputation
**Effort:** 3+ months (sales, onboarding, integration)
**Model:**
- Employers pay to access MTT's vetted veteran talent
- Veterans opt-in to be contacted by employers
- MTT curates matches (quality over quantity)
- Commission on placements (15-25% of first-year salary)
**Competitive Advantage:** Comprehensive veteran profiles (resume + skills + VA status + preferences)
**Metrics:** Employer partnerships, placements, revenue per placement

### 18. Premium VA Claims Analytics & Insights
**Why:** Data-driven approach to maximize disability rating
**Impact:** Premium revenue, proven ROI
**Effort:** 2-3 months (analyze claim data, build insights engine)
**Features:**
- Show approval rates by condition/reviewer/location
- Suggest additional conditions to claim (based on your profile)
- Predict disability rating (AI model)
- Optimize claim strategy (what evidence to include, when to file)
**Competitive Advantage:** Data nobody else has (claims outcomes)
**Metrics:** Premium conversions, rating increases, claim approvals

## Key Insight

**Build on MTT's unique strengths:** VA Claims, State Benefits, Privacy, Comprehensiveness

**Partner for commodity features:** Job boards, training courses, specialized coaching

**Prioritize features NO competitor has:** Claims builder, state comparison, application tracker, comprehensive dashboard

**Avoid competing head-on:** Don't try to out-job-board Military.com or out-mentor ACP. Do what they CAN'T.

**Result:** Defensible moat, differentiated product, sustainable competitive advantage
