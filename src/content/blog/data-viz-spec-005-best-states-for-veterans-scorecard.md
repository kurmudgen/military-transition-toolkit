## 1. Executive Overview

### Purpose
This comprehensive scorecard visualization evaluates and ranks all 50 U.S. states across 8 critical categories affecting veterans: tax benefits, healthcare access, employment opportunities, education benefits, cost of living, veteran community, quality of life, and state-specific programs. The visualization synthesizes complex multi-dimensional data into an intuitive, actionable scorecard that helps veterans identify the best states for their specific priorities.

### Target Audience
- Veterans planning post-service relocation
- Military families researching future locations
- Transitioning service members evaluating job markets
- Veteran advocates comparing state policies
- State legislators benchmarking veteran services
- Media and researchers analyzing veteran support

### Key Insights to Communicate
1. No single "best" state - optimal choice depends on individual priorities
2. South Carolina leads overall (2025 WalletHub ranking)
3. Texas and Florida dominate for tax benefits
4. Rural states (Wyoming, Montana, South Dakota) excel in healthcare access per capita
5. Top 10 states offer 3-4x better veteran support than bottom 10
6. States can score high in some categories while low in others
7. Weighting categories by personal priorities changes optimal state recommendation

9. **U.S. News & World Report** - Best States overall rankings (quality of life)

### Data Collection Period
September 2024 - November 2025

### Update Frequency
- **Annual comprehensive update** (January): All 8 categories refreshed
- **Quarterly spot updates** (April, July, October): Tax and employment data

6. **Veteran Community**: 10% - Veteran population %, veteran organizations, support services

## 6. Accessibility Guidelines

### WCAG 2.1 Level AA Compliance

#### Color Contrast
- All text meets 4.5:1 minimum
- Score colors supplemented with icons/patterns
- High contrast mode available
- Color never sole indicator of information

#### Keyboard Navigation
- Tab through all cards and controls
- Arrow keys navigate state cards in grid
- Enter/Space opens state details
- Escape closes modals
- Keyboard shortcuts: S (search), C (compare), F (filter)

#### Screen Reader Support
- Cards announce: "South Carolina, rank 1, overall score 87.4 out of 100. Tax benefits 92, excellent. Healthcare access 78, good."
- Radar charts described textually
- Table mode with full semantic HTML
- ARIA labels on all interactive elements
- Live regions announce filter/sort changes

#### Focus Indicators
- 3px solid #1565C0 outline
- Never removed
- Visible on all interactive elements

### Alternative Formats
- Full data table accessible to screen readers
- CSV export of all data
- Printable PDF report generator
- Text-only summary view
