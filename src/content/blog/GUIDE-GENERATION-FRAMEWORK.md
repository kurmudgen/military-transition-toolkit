## SYSTEMATIC RESEARCH PROCESS

### Step 1: Understand the AFSC (15 minutes)

**Search Queries:**
1. "[AFSC code] [AFSC title] duties responsibilities"
2. "[AFSC code] Air Force career field description"
3. "[AFSC] military job description training"

**Find:**
- Official AF.mil description
- ForeverWingman AFSC page
- OperationMilitaryKids AFSC profile
- Official CFETP if available

**Extract:**
- Primary duties
- Technical skills
- Equipment/systems used
- Typical assignments
- Career progression

### Step 2: Identify Civilian Equivalents (20 minutes)

**Search Queries:**
1. "[AFSC code] civilian jobs transition 2024"
2. "[AFSC title] civilian equivalent career"
3. "[Primary skill from AFSC] jobs salary 2024"
4. "companies hiring [AFSC skill] veterans"

**Resources:**
- MyNextMove.org/vets (O*NET military crosswalk)
- DOD SkillBridge employer lists
- ClearanceJobs.com (if clearance required)
- Indeed.com, LinkedIn Jobs

**Identify 5-7 Career Paths:**
- Direct equivalents (same job, civilian)
- Related technical roles
- Management/leadership paths
- Alternative applications of skills

### Step 3: Research Salaries (30 minutes)

**Search Queries for EACH Career Path:**
1. "[Civilian job title] salary 2024 2025"
2. "[Civilian job title] salary by experience level"
3. "[Civilian job title] salary [major cities]"
4. "[Civilian job title] BLS outlook"

**Sources:**
- BLS.gov (Bureau of Labor Statistics) - official data
- Salary.com - detailed by experience
- Glassdoor.com - company-specific
- PayScale.com - detailed breakdowns
- ZipRecruiter.com - current market rates
- Indeed.com/salaries

**Capture:**
- Entry level ($X-$Y)
- Mid-level ($X-$Y)
- Senior level ($X-$Y)
- Geographic variations (high/average/low markets)
- Growth projections

### Step 4: Identify Certifications (20 minutes)

**Search Queries:**
1. "[Civilian job title] certifications required 2024"
2. "[Civilian job title] license requirements cost"
3. "[Industry] professional certifications"

**Common by Field:**
- **Aviation:** FAA A&P, dispatcher, pilot certificates
- **IT/Cyber:** CompTIA (Security+, Network+, A+), CISSP, CEH
- **Medical:** State licenses, specialty certifications
- **Maintenance:** ASE, HVAC, electrical licenses
- **Logistics:** APICS CSCP, CLTD, PMP
- **Intelligence:** Clearance maintenance, language certs

**Capture:**
- Certification name
- Cost ($X,XXX)
- Time (X weeks/months)
- ROI/value proposition
- How to obtain
- Study resources

### Step 5: Find Hiring Companies (45 minutes)

**Search Queries:**
1. "[Civilian job title] top employers 2024"
2. "[Industry] companies hiring veterans"
3. "[Civilian job title] jobs [major metro areas]"
4. "defense contractors hiring [AFSC skill]"
5. "[Civilian job title] government civilian jobs"

**Sources:**
- Company career pages directly
- ClearanceJobs.com (defense/intel)
- Indeed.com, LinkedIn (filter by company)
- USAJobs.gov (federal civilian)
- Military.com/veteran-jobs
- RecruitMilitary.com

**Organize by Category (50-100 total):**
- Defense contractors (if applicable)
- Commercial companies
- Airlines/transportation (if applicable)
- Government agencies
- Regional employers

**Capture for Each:**
- Company name
- Primary roles for this AFSC
- Locations/hubs
- Any veteran programs

### Step 6: Research Geographic Markets (15 minutes)

**Search Queries:**
1. "best cities [civilian job title] jobs 2024"
2. "[Civilian job title] salary [city name]"
3. "cost of living [city name] vs salary"

**Identify:**
- Top 10 cities for this career field
- Salary ranges by location
- Cost of living factors
- Industry concentrations (hubs)

### Step 7: Gather Supporting Information (30 minutes)

**Professional Associations:**
- Search: "[industry] professional association"
- Find 3-5 relevant associations with membership benefits

**Success Stories/Case Studies:**
- Search: "[AFSC code] veteran transition success"
- LinkedIn: Find veterans who transitioned
- Create realistic case studies based on typical paths

**Interview Questions:**
- Search: "[civilian job title] interview questions"
- Adapt to veteran context

**Common Mistakes:**
- Think through AFSC-specific transition pitfalls
- Based on skills gaps, certification needs, market realities
