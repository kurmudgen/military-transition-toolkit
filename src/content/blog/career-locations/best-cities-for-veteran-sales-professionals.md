# Best Cities for Veteran Sales Professionals: 2025 Guide

Military veterans possess the discipline, resilience, and communication skills that make excellent sales professionals. Your experience with mission focus, handling rejection, and building relationships translates directly to sales success. Veterans in B2B sales, tech sales, and enterprise sales are highly valued for their leadership, work ethic, and ability to execute.

## Bottom Line Up Front

**Top 3 Cities:**
1. San Francisco, CA - $150,000+ OTE - Tech sales capital
2. Dallas-Fort Worth, TX - $120,000 OTE - Tech hub, no state tax
3. Austin, TX - $125,000 OTE - Tech growth, no state tax

**Key Factors:**
- Income range: $50,000 - $300,000+ depending on role and performance
- Job growth: 5% projected through 2032 (average)
- Requirements: None formal; experience valued
- Veteran advantage: Discipline, resilience, mission focus, leadership

## Methodology

**How We Ranked Cities:**
- Average sales OTE (On-Target Earnings) (40% weight)
- Number of sales job openings (25% weight)
- Cost of living index (20% weight)
- Veteran population & resources (15% weight)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Is sales good for veterans?**
Excellent fit. Resilience, discipline, and mission focus translate directly to sales success.

**2. Do I need experience to start?**
No. Many companies hire veterans for SDR/BDR roles with comprehensive training.

**3. What industries are best for veterans?**
Tech (SaaS), defense contracting, medical devices, industrial equipmentâ€”all value veteran skills.

**4. What's the income potential?**
Varies widely. SDR: $70-90K. Enterprise AE: $200-350K+. Top performers earn significantly more.

**5. Is the income stable?**
Base + commission structure. Base provides stability; commission rewards performance.

**6. What training is available?**
Many companies offer extensive sales training. Coursera, Salesforce Trailhead also valuable.

**7. What about sales management?**
Natural progression. Leadership experience translates well to managing sales teams.

**8. Is tech sales competitive?**
Yes, but rewarding. Veterans' discipline and work ethic provide advantage.

**9. How do I break into tech sales?**
SDR/BDR roles are entry point. Companies like Salesforce, Oracle, Amazon hire veterans.

**10. What about government/defense sales?**
Excellent fit for veterans. Security clearance and military knowledge are advantages.
