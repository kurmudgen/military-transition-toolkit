## How to Maximize Your Chances

### 1. Emphasize Maintenance Expertise

**Don't write:** "91B Vehicle Mechanic, repaired military vehicles"

**Do write:** "Fleet maintenance professional with expertise in vehicle diagnostics, complex systems maintenance, preventive care, and equipment reliability management"

### 2. Highlight Key Skills

- Vehicle diagnosis and repair
- Preventive maintenance procedures
- System knowledge (diesel, electrical, hydraulic)
- Fleet maintenance management
- Troubleshooting and problem-solving
- Documentation and compliance
- Numbers: "Maintained 100+ vehicle fleet," "Achieved 95% uptime," "Reduced maintenance costs 20%"

### 3. Pursue Certifications

- **ASE Certifications:** Automotive Service Excellence (A1-A8)
- **Diesel/Heavy Equipment:** Specialized certifications
- **Manufacturer certifications:** Company provides specific training

### 4. Target Large Fleet Operations

- Higher pay than small shops
- Union representation common
- Better benefits and job security
- Clear advancement to management
- Steady work year-round

### 5. Strategic Application

- Apply to 20-30+ companies
- Target both mechanic and manager positions
- Consider government positions (pension, security)
- Look for advancement paths
- Build toward supervisor/manager roles
