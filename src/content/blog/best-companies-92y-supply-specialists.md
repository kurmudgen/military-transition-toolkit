## How to Maximize Your Chances

### 1. Emphasize Supply Management Experience

**Don't write:** "92Y Supply Specialist, managed inventory"

**Do write:** "Supply chain professional with expertise in inventory management, procurement coordination, warehouse operations, and logistics optimization"

### 2. Highlight Key Skills

- Inventory management and control
- Procurement and purchasing
- Warehouse operations and organization
- Supply chain coordination
- Systems and software (SAP, Oracle, etc.)
- Cost management and budgeting
- Numbers: "Managed $10M+ inventory," "Optimized warehouse efficiency 20%," "Coordinated 500+ suppliers"

### 3. Consider Certifications

- **APICS CSCP:** Supply chain professional certification
- **Lean Six Sigma:** Process improvement (Green Belt: $3K-$5K)
- **Procurement certification:** NCMA or similar

### 4. Target Growth Companies

- Amazon, Walmart, Target - massive hiring and growth
- Faster advancement than mature companies
- Better pay at growth companies
- Stock options at public companies

### 5. Strategic Application

- Apply to 25-30+ companies
- Target both coordinator and manager roles
- Don't just pursue entry jobs
- Emphasize growth potential
- Look for advancement paths
