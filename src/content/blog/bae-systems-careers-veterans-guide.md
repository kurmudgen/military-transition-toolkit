# BAE Systems Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

BAE Systems is a global defense, aerospace, and security company headquartered in the UK but with a significant US presence. The US subsidiary, BAE Systems, Inc., is one of the top 10 defense contractors in America, specializing in electronic warfare, combat vehicles, ship repair, and munitions.

For veterans—especially those with electronics, vehicle, or naval backgrounds—BAE Systems offers career opportunities where your operational experience matters.
