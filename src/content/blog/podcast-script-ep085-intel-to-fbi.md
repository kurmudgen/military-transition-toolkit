[INTRO]
Captain Jennifer Lee, Army Military Intelligence Officer with Top Secret/SCI clearance and 8 years counterintelligence experience, became FBI Special Agent earning $130K+ within 2 years of military service.

[MAIN CONTENT]
**Military:** 35L Counterintelligence Agent, managed source operations, conducted investigations, deployed to Middle East.
**Transition:** Applied to FBI during final year of service. 9-month application process. Accepted into FBI Academy at Quantico immediately after separation.
**Process:** FBI Academy (21 weeks), first field assignment (counterterrorism division), GS-13 Special Agent pay scale ($130K in high-cost areas).
**Keys:** TS/SCI clearance expedited process, military CI experience directly applicable, leadership record strengthened application.

[CONCLUSION]
Jennifer transitioned from Army CI to FBI Special Agent ($130K+). Military intelligence experience and clearances highly valued by federal law enforcement.
