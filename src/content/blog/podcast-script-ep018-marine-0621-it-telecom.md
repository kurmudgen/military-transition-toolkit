[INTRO]
**Cold Open:** 0621 - field radio operator. Communications systems, network troubleshooting, radio maintenance, signal operations. IT and telecom need you - network tech at fifty-five thousand, telecom tech at sixty-five thousand, network engineer at eighty-five thousand plus.

[MAIN CONTENT]

**Your 0621 Skills**

Radio communications systems (HF, VHF, UHF, SATCOM), network configuration, troubleshooting, equipment maintenance, signal planning, tactical communications. Technical and operational experience.

**Career Paths**

**Telecommunications Technician:** Install/maintain telecom systems - phone systems, fiber optics, wireless. $55K-$75K. Field service or facility-based.

**Network Technician:** Install/configure network equipment, troubleshoot connectivity. $50K-$70K. Entry IT role with growth potential.

**Field Service Technician:** On-site equipment installation/repair for telecom/network companies. $55K-$80K. Travel required but good pay.

**Network Engineer:** Design/implement networks, manage infrastructure. $75K-$105K. Requires certifications (CCNA) plus experience.

**Wireless Technician:** Cell tower work, wireless network installation. $60K-$85K. Physical work but high demand.

**IT Support Specialist:** General IT support, help desk, systems administration. $50K-$70K. Broader IT career entry.

**RF Engineer:** Radio frequency engineering, antenna design. $80K-$120K. Advanced role, may require degree plus experience.

**Companies:** AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile (wireless carriers), Crown Castle, American Tower (tower companies), Cisco, Juniper (equipment manufacturers), MSPs (managed service providers), DoD contractors (CACI, L3Harris for military comms).

**Certifications**

**CompTIA Network+:** Networking fundamentals. $350 exam. Good baseline.

**Cisco CCNA:** Industry standard networking cert. $300 exam. Your communications background helps. Opens network engineer path.

**CompTIA Security+:** If going DoD contractor route. $392 exam. Required by DoD 8570.

**Wireless Certifications:** CWNA (Certified Wireless Network Administrator) for wireless specialization. $250 exam.

**Manufacturer Certifications:** If working with specific vendors (Cisco, Juniper, etc.), their certifications valuable.

**Timeline:** 12mo study Network+/CCNA, 6-9mo get certified and apply jobs, 3-6mo interview, separation start tech role.

**Path Progression:** Start tech $55K-$65K → add certs and experience → engineer $75K-$105K (3-5 years) → senior/architect $100K-$130K+ (5-10 years).

Resources at militarytransitiontoolkit.com. Next: Marine 3531 motor operator to logistics.
