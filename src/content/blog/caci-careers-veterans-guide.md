# CACI Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

CACI International Inc has been providing services to the federal government since 1962. With deep roots in intelligence, IT services, and defense support, CACI has built a culture that values military experience and the mission-focused mindset veterans bring.

With approximately 23,000 employees and over $6 billion in annual revenue, CACI offers veterans—especially those with intelligence, IT, or operations backgrounds—excellent career opportunities in the government services space.

## CACI Hiring Process

### Step 1: Apply

**Where to apply:**
- caci.com/careers
- ClearanceJobs
- LinkedIn
- Indeed

### Step 2: Screening

Review for:
- Clearance status
- Basic qualifications
- Experience match
- Education verification

### Step 3: Interviews

Typical process:

**Phone Screen:**
- Initial qualification
- Clearance verification
- Interest confirmation

**Technical/Hiring Manager Interview:**
- Skills assessment
- Role fit discussion
- Team introduction

**Some positions may require customer interview**

### Step 4: Offer

If selected:
- Compensation discussion
- Benefits overview
- Start date
- Security processing

### Timeline

Typical timeline:
- Application to response: 1-3 weeks
- Interview process: 2-4 weeks
- Offer to start: 2-4 weeks

Active clearance speeds process significantly.
