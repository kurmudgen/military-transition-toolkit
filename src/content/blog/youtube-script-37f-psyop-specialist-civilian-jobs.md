[MAIN CONTENT]

**Section 1: Market Value (60 seconds)**

**Entry cleared positions:** $75,000-$95,000/year
**Mid-level MISO planner:** $104,000-$140,000/year
**Senior contractor:** $140,000-$179,000/year

Peraton MISO planner range: $104,000-$166,000. Amentum PSYOP planner: $112,000-$179,000.

**Your advantage:** TS/SCI clearance + PSYOP experience + planning skills = extremely valuable combination.

**Section 2: Defense Contractors (MISO/IO) (120 seconds)**

**Peraton:** MISO/PSYOP planners $104K-$166K. Multiple locations CONUS/OCONUS. Requires TS/SCI + 8 years experience.

**Amentum:** PSYOP/MISO planners $112K-$179K. Support SOCOM operations. Tampa, NC, overseas rotations.

**CACI, Booz Allen, Leidos:** Information operations, influence campaigns. $95K-$150K depending on role/location.

**Positions:** MISO Planner, Information Operations Analyst, Strategic Communications Planner, Regional Expert, Media Production Specialist.

**Requirements:** TS/SCI clearance (critical), 37F experience, planning/targeting experience, regional expertise valuable (language skills premium).

**Section 3: Strategic Communications (Corporate Path) (90 seconds)**

**Corporate Strategic Communications:** Fortune 500 companies need influence campaign expertise. Title: Strategic Communications Manager, Public Affairs Specialist.

**Salary:** $75,000-$130,000/year. Companies: Defense primes, tech companies, consulting firms.

**Political Consulting:** Campaign strategy, messaging, polling analysis. $70,000-$150,000/year + bonuses. Election cycles = high demand.

**Crisis Communications:** Corporate crisis management, reputation defense. $85,000-$140,000/year. Your influence ops experience = valuable.

**Digital Marketing/Social Media Strategy:** Online influence campaigns (legal/ethical). $65,000-$110,000/year. Your targeting knowledge translates.

**Section 4: Federal Government (Civilian) (90 seconds)**

**Department of State:** Public diplomacy, strategic communications. GS-12 to GS-14: $87,000-$130,000/year. Overseas postings common.

**Broadcasting Board of Governors (USAGM):** Voice of America, Radio Free Europe. $75,000-$110,000/year. Counter-propaganda missions.

**DoD Civilian:** Strategic communications, information operations support. $80,000-$120,000/year. Support active duty PSYOP/MISO.

**DHS/CIA:** Information operations, influence analysis. $85,000-$130,000/year. TS/SCI required.

**Intelligence Community:** All-source analysis with PSYOP lens. $90,000-$140,000/year.

**Section 5: Media Production & Content Creation (60 seconds)**

**Media Production Specialist:** Create influence content for DoD contractors. $70,000-$105,000/year. Video, audio, print, digital.

**Content Strategist:** Plan multimedia campaigns. $75,000-$120,000/year. Your target audience analysis = core skill.

**Documentary/Film Production:** Military/defense documentaries. Freelance $60,000-$100,000+/year. Leverages your story-telling skills.

**Podcast Producer/Host:** Defense/geopolitics podcasts growing. Variable income, some make $75,000-$150,000+.

**Section 6: Think Tanks & Research (60 seconds)**

**Policy Analyst:** RAND, CSIS, Brookings, ISW, CNA. $70,000-$120,000/year. Research influence operations, write policy papers.

**Regional Expert:** Focus on area you deployed to. $75,000-$110,000/year. Language skills + cultural knowledge = premium.

**Disinformation Researcher:** Study foreign influence campaigns (Russia, China). $65,000-$105,000/year. Growing field.

**Academic Path:** Teaching PSYOP/influence ops at military schools. $70,000-$100,000/year. Master's/PhD often required.
