## Proposed Solution

### Platform as Employee Benefit

**What It Is:**
Comprehensive digital career development platform provided to all veteran hires for their first 2 years (or duration of employment).

**How It Works:**

**Phase 1: Pre-Hire** (Optional)
- Candidates access platform during interview process
- Understand corporate career expectations
- Prepare for interviews with career planning context

**Phase 2: Onboarding** (Critical)
- Access provided in welcome packet
- Veteran-specific onboarding content
- Career path planning within your organization
- Skills development roadmap

**Phase 3: Ongoing Development** (Retention Driver)
- Continuous career guidance
- Performance review preparation
- Internal mobility planning
- Compensation negotiation support

**Key Features:**

1. **Career Development Planning:** Understanding corporate advancement, performance reviews, professional networking
2. **Skills Translation:** Articulating military experience in performance reviews, translating to corporate KPIs
3. **Professional Development:** Industry certifications, GI Bill utilization, technical skills
4. **Compensation Guidance:** Understanding civilian comp structures, negotiation strategies
5. **Cultural Integration:** Workplace norms, communication styles, relationship building
6. **State Benefits:** For relocating veterans, state-specific resources
