# SAIC Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

Science Applications International Corporation (SAIC) has been serving government customers for over 50 years. As a pure-play government contractor focused on IT modernization, engineering, and mission support, SAIC offers veterans career opportunities where your government experience directly applies.

With approximately 25,000 employees and $7+ billion in annual revenue, SAIC is one of the largest government-focused contractors in the industry.

## Why SAIC for Veterans?

SAIC works almost exclusively for government:
- Defense and intelligence customers
- Federal civilian agencies
- No commercial distraction
- Mission understanding valued

## SAIC Hiring Process

### Step 1: Apply

**Where to apply:**
- saic.com/careers
- ClearanceJobs
- LinkedIn
- Indeed

### Step 2: Screening

HR review for:
- Basic qualifications
- Clearance eligibility
- Experience match
- Education requirements

### Step 3: Interviews

Typical process:

**Phone Screen:**
- Initial discussion
- Qualification verification
- Interest confirmation

**Technical Interview:**
- Skills assessment
- Role-specific questions
- Problem-solving

**Manager Interview:**
- Team fit
- Behavioral questions
- Career discussion

### Step 4: Offer

If selected:
- Compensation discussion
- Benefits overview
- Start date
- Clearance processing

### Timeline

Typical timeline:
- Application to response: 1-3 weeks
- Interview process: 2-4 weeks
- Offer to start: 2-4 weeks
- Total: 5-11 weeks

Clearance processing adds time if needed.
