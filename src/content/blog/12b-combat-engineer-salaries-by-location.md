# 12B Combat Engineer: Location-Based Salary Analysis

## BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)

12B Combat Engineers earn highest salaries in major construction markets and federal contracting hubs. Construction Project Manager salaries: $95,000-$160,000+ in major metros (New York, San Francisco, Chicago, Dallas). Federal contracting (DC metro): $100,000-$150,000+. Union construction offers $70,000-$120,000+ depending on region and market. New York/California highest nominal salaries but extreme cost-of-living offsets. Texas construction combines strong market with no state income tax. Strategic location selection impacts 20-year earnings by $800,000-$1,500,000+ accounting for cost-of-living and tax differences.

---

## Top Paying Markets for 12B

### New York Metro (Union Construction Premium)
- **Project Manager:** $110,000-$160,000
- **Union Laborer/Operating Engineer:** $80,000-$130,000+
- **Cost of Living:** +45-50%
- **5-Year PM Earning:** $575,000-$850,000
- **Net After Cost:** $375,000-$550,000

### San Francisco / California
- **Project Manager:** $115,000-$170,000
- **Construction Manager:** $100,000-$150,000
- **Cost of Living:** +55-65%
- **5-Year Earning:** $600,000-$900,000
- **Net After Cost:** $310,000-$450,000

### Texas (Dallas / Houston / Austin)
- **Project Manager:** $100,000-$145,000
- **Construction Manager:** $95,000-$135,000
- **No State Income Tax:** +$15,000-$20,000/year advantage
- **Cost of Living:** +10-15%
- **5-Year Earning:** $500,000-$725,000
- **Net After Cost/Tax:** $520,000-$780,000 (excellent)

### Chicago
- **Project Manager:** $100,000-$140,000
- **Union Construction Strong:** +$5,000-$15,000 premium
- **Cost of Living:** +5-10%
- **5-Year Earning:** $500,000-$700,000
- **Net After Cost:** $490,000-$700,000

## Career Pathways by Location

### Federal Contracting Path (DC Metro)
- **Site Manager:** $90,000-$130,000
- **Program Manager:** $110,000-$160,000
- **Benefits:** Federal pension, health insurance
- **5-Year Potential:** $525,000-$750,000
- **Total Comp:** +$200,000-$300,000 (benefits value)

### Union Construction (New York / Chicago)
- **Strong Benefits:** Healthcare, pension, apprenticeship advancement
- **Union Premium:** $20,000-$40,000+ above non-union
- **Advancement:** Strong to superintendent ($140,000-$200,000+)
- **5-Year Earning:** $400,000-$600,000+ (including benefits)

### Residential/Commercial Construction (South/Southwest)
- **Texas/Florida/Arizona:** $85,000-$130,000 project manager range
- **Rapid Growth Markets:** Strong advancement opportunity
- **No State Income Tax (TX/FL):** Tax advantage
- **5-Year Earning:** $425,000-$650,000
- **Net After Cost/Tax:** $450,000-$700,000
