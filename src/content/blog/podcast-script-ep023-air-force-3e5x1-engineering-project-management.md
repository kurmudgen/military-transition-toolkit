[INTRO]
**Cold Open:** 3E5 - engineering. Construction management, project coordination, facility operations. Project management and facilities careers need you - coordinator at sixty thousand, PM at eighty-five thousand, facilities manager at ninety thousand plus.

[MAIN CONTENT]

**Your 3E Skills:** Construction project oversight, facility maintenance management, contractor coordination, budget management, engineering technical knowledge, quality assurance.

**Career Paths:**

**Project Coordinator:** Entry project management. $55K-$75K. Supporting PMs, tracking schedules/budgets.

**Project Manager:** Leading projects start-finish. $75K-$110K. PMP certification valuable. Your AF project experience translates.

**Facilities Manager:** Managing building operations, maintenance, contracts. $70K-$100K. Commercial buildings, campuses, industrial facilities.

**Construction Manager:** Overseeing construction projects. $80K-$120K. Similar to 12B path (Episode 12) but more office/coordination focused.

**Real Estate/Property Manager:** Managing property portfolios. $60K-$95K. Your facility management experience applies.

**Engineering Technician:** Supporting engineers with technical work. $55K-$80K. Entry if pursuing engineering path.

**Companies:** CBRE, JLL, Cushman & Wakefield (commercial real estate/facilities), construction companies (see Episode 12), facility management companies, hospitals (large facility ops), universities, government facilities.

**PMP Certification:** Project Management Professional. Requires 35hr education + experience. Exam $555. Adds $15K-$25K salary. Use GI Bill for PMP boot camp or self-study. Critical for PM career advancement.

**Degree:** Not always required entry-level but helps advancement. Construction management, facilities management, or general business degrees. Your AF technical training counts as experience.

**Timeline:** 12mo study PMP materials and apply for exam (need experience hours documented), 6-9mo get PMP and apply jobs, 3-6mo interview emphasizing project/facility management experience.

**Career Growth:** Coordinator $60K → PM $85K (3-5yr with PMP) → Senior PM/Program Manager $110K-$140K+ (5-10yr).

Resources at militarytransitiontoolkit.com. Next: Air Force 4A0X1 to healthcare administration.
