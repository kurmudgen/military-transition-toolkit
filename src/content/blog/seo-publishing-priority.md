## Priority Scoring Formula

Each piece scored on:
1. **Search Volume** (40% weight)
   - 10,000+ = 10 points
   - 5,000-10,000 = 8 points
   - 1,000-5,000 = 6 points
   - 500-1,000 = 4 points
   - <500 = 2 points

2. **Keyword Difficulty** (30% weight)
   - KD 0-20 = 10 points (easy)
   - KD 21-40 = 7 points (medium)
   - KD 41-60 = 4 points (hard)
   - KD 61+ = 2 points (very hard)

3. **Conversion Potential** (20% weight)
   - High commercial intent = 10 points
   - Medium intent = 6 points
   - Informational only = 3 points

4. **Timeliness** (10% weight)
   - Time-sensitive (2025 guides) = 10 points
   - Evergreen = 7 points
   - Low urgency = 4 points

**Example:**
- "VA disability rates 2025" = (10 × 0.4) + (7 × 0.3) + (10 × 0.2) + (10 × 0.1) = **9.1/10** ⭐

## Red Flags to Watch

❌ **Don't publish if:**
- Content is duplicate/thin (add 500+ unique words minimum)
- Missing keyword research
- No internal linking plan
- Images lack alt text
- URL structure doesn't match strategy

✅ **Always check before publishing:**
- Title tag optimized (60 chars)
- Meta description written (160 chars)
- H1 includes target keyword
- 3-5 internal links added
- Featured snippet section included
- Schema markup ready
