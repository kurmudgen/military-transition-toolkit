---# SEO Content Clusters Strategy

## Overview
Organization of 1,600+ pages into 10 main content clusters using topic modeling and pillar-cluster architecture to boost topical authority and search rankings.

---

## Content Cluster Model

### What is a Content Cluster?

**Hub-and-Spoke Model:**
- **Pillar Page (Hub):** Comprehensive overview of broad topic
- **Cluster Content (Spokes):** Detailed articles on subtopics
- **Internal Links:** All spokes link to pillar, pillar links to all spokes

**SEO Benefits:**
- Establishes topical authority
- Improves internal link structure
- Helps Google understand site architecture
- Boosts rankings for entire topic cluster
