# Raytheon (RTX) Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

When America's military needs a missile to find its target, odds are it's a Raytheon missile. As part of RTX Corporation (formed from the merger of Raytheon and United Technologies), this defense giant builds the weapons systems that protect the nation.

For veterans—especially those with weapons systems, radar, or technical backgrounds—Raytheon offers career opportunities where your operational experience directly applies.

## Understanding RTX Corporation

### The Merger

In 2020, Raytheon merged with United Technologies to form RTX:

**RTX comprises:**
- **Raytheon:** Missiles, radar, sensors, cybersecurity
- **Pratt & Whitney:** Jet engines
- **Collins Aerospace:** Avionics and aircraft systems

### Company Scale

**RTX by the numbers:**
- $68+ billion annual revenue
- 180,000+ employees worldwide
- Major defense and commercial presence
- Global operations

### Why This Matters to You

Each segment offers opportunities:
- **Raytheon segment:** Defense and space
- **Pratt & Whitney:** Military and commercial engines
- **Collins Aerospace:** Aviation systems

### Step 3: Interviews

Multiple rounds:

**Phone Screen:**

**Technical Interview:**

- Role-specific questions
- Problem-solving
- Technical depth

**Panel/Hiring Manager:**
- Team fit
- Behavioral questions

### Step 4: Assessments

- Drug test

### Step 5: Offer

Typical timeline:
