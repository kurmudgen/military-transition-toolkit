## TIME-SAVING TIPS

1. **Batch similar AFSCs** - Research all pilot AFSCs together, all maintenance together, etc.

2. **Reuse company lists** - Defense contractors repeat across intelligence, cyber, maintenance AFSCs

3. **Template responses** - Interview answers for "why leaving military" similar across all AFSCs

4. **Salary ranges by industry** - Once you know aerospace technician ranges, applies to multiple maintenance AFSCs

5. **Geographic data** - Best cities for aviation jobs applies to 30+ aviation AFSCs

6. **Certification deep dives** - Once you research A&P thoroughly, applies to 15+ maintenance AFSCs
