## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the highest-paying path for 46Q specialists?**
A: Tech companies (Google, Apple) followed by PR agencies to senior roles ($200,000+). Startup equity can exceed all at successful exits.

**Q: Should I join a PR agency or go corporate?**
A: **Agency**: Fast advancement, diverse experiences, faster salary growth initially. **Corporate**: Better work-life balance, deeper company knowledge, higher long-term compensation.

**Q: Do I need an MBA?**
A: Not required for entry. Helpful for advancement to C-suite levels. PR and tech emphasize MBA less than consulting.

**Q: How quickly can I advance in PR agencies?**
A: Fastest track. Promotion every 1-2 years possible. From account manager to VP in 8-10 years. Agency experience accelerates corporate advancement.

**Q: What's the typical first role for 46Q specialists?**
A: Communications Manager at corporate ($75,000-$90,000) or Account Manager at agency ($62,000-$75,000). Both lead to $100,000+ within 3 years.

**Q: Are there remote opportunities?**
A: Growing. PR agencies hybrid (40-60% on-site). Corporate increasingly flexible (40-50% remote). Government on-site. Startups typically hybrid or remote.
