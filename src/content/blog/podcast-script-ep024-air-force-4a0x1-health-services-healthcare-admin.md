[INTRO]
**Cold Open:** 4A0 - health services management. Patient administration, medical records, healthcare operations. Healthcare administration needs you - coordinator at fifty-five thousand, manager at seventy-five thousand, director at one hundred thousand plus.

[MAIN CONTENT]

**Your 4A Skills:** Healthcare operations, medical records management, patient services, insurance/billing knowledge, regulatory compliance, medical administration.

**Career Paths:**

**Medical Records Coordinator:** Managing health information. $45K-$60K. Entry healthcare admin.

**Patient Services Coordinator:** Managing patient flow, scheduling, front-end operations. $50K-$65K.

**Healthcare Administrator:** Managing clinic/department operations. $65K-$90K. Your AF healthcare management experience.

**Practice Manager:** Running physician practices. $70K-$100K. Private practice or hospital-owned clinics.

**Healthcare Compliance Officer:** Ensuring HIPAA, regulatory compliance. $65K-$95K. Your knowledge of healthcare regulations valued.

**Health Information Manager:** Overseeing medical records, EHR systems. $70K-$100K. RHIA/RHIT certification helps.

**Hospital Administrator:** Senior hospital management. $90K-$140K+. Requires experience, often bachelor's/master's degree.

**Certifications:** RHIA (Registered Health Information Administrator) or RHIT (Registered Health Information Technician) for health information careers. CPC (Certified Professional Coder) if going medical coding route.

**Education:** Bachelor's in Healthcare Administration or related field opens management paths. Master's (MHA) for director/executive levels. Use GI Bill strategically.

**Companies:** All hospital systems (HCA, Ascension, Mayo), physician practice groups, VA hospitals (veteran preference), insurance companies (UnitedHealth, Cigna, Anthem), healthcare software companies.

**Timeline:** 12mo decide path, research certs/education needed, 6-9mo pursue certification or apply based on experience, 3-6mo interview healthcare organizations.

**Growth Path:** Coordinator $55K → Manager $75K (3-5yr) → Director $100K+ (5-10yr, usually requires degree).

Resources at militarytransitiontoolkit.com. Next: Coast Guard BM to maritime/port security.
