## How to Maximize Your Chances

### 1. Highlight Construction Management Experience

**Don't write:** "12B Combat Engineer, built fortifications"

**Do write:** "Construction and project management professional with expertise in construction operations, crew leadership, equipment operation, scheduling, and safety compliance for complex construction projects"

### 2. Emphasize Key Skills

- Project management and coordination
- Crew leadership and supervision
- Equipment operation and management
- Safety compliance and OSHA
- Schedule management and coordination
- Cost awareness and budgeting
- Quality assurance
- Numbers: "Managed 50+ person crews," "Led $5M+ projects," "Achieved zero safety incidents"

### 3. Relevant Certifications

- **OSHA 30-hour** - Safety foundation
- **Project Management Professional (PMP)** - Advanced
- **Certified Construction Manager (CCM)** - Industry recognized
- GI Bill covers certification costs

### 4. Strategic Application

- Apply to 20-30+ construction firms
- Target superintendent and project manager roles
- Don't just pursue entry positions - target management
- Build toward director and executive roles
- Emphasize safety record prominently

### 5. Geographic Flexibility

- Major markets pay premium: New York, LA, San Francisco, Chicago
- Strong regional opportunities throughout US
- Consider cost of living vs. salary
- Project-based work may require relocation
