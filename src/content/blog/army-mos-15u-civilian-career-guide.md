## Certifications that actually matter

### High priority:

**FAA A&P Certificate** - Your golden ticket to commercial aviation. With 30+ months documented CH-47 maintenance, may qualify to test out without 18-month school. Cost: $10,000-$36,000 school (GI Bill covers); $800-$1,250 testing if testing out. Value: Unlocks $70K-$120K career potential.

**Security clearance maintenance** - If active, find clearance-requiring employment within 2 years. Value: Worth $10K-$20K salary premium for contractor positions.

**Bachelor's degree** - Not required but strengthens competitiveness. GI Bill covers. Value: Opens management/engineering positions.

### Medium priority:

**FAA Inspection Authorization** - Advanced credential. Requires A&P + 3 years. Cost: $200-$500. Value: Inspector positions at $90K-$120K.

**NDT certifications** - For component overhaul. Cost: $2,000-$5,000. Value: Specialized credentials.
