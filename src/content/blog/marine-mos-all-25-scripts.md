## 0311 - Rifleman

**Civilian careers:** Law enforcement ($55K-$87K federal), Security contracting ($80K-$180K overseas), Corrections ($45K-$65K), Executive protection ($75K-$130K), Police officer ($55K-$85K)

**Best path:** Federal law enforcement or overseas security contracting for highest pay

**Top employers:** CBP, FBI, US Marshals, police departments, Constellis, Triple Canopy

**Note:** Similar to Army 11B Infantry - law enforcement is primary civilian path

## 0621 - Field Radio Operator

**Civilian careers:** Telecommunications technician ($55K-$85K), Radio frequency engineer ($75K-$115K), Network technician ($55K-$85K), Defense contractor comms ($70K-$110K with clearance)

**Best path:** Telecom installer or defense contractor

**Top employers:** AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Crown Castle, defense contractors

**Certifications:** Network+ through DoD COOL

## 6046 - Aircraft Maintenance Technician

## 3531 - Motor Vehicle Operator
