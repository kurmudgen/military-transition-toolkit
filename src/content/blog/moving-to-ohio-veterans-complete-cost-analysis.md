# Moving to Ohio for Veterans: Complete Cost Analysis and Financial Guide 2025

Ohio has become increasingly attractive for military retirees seeking exceptional affordability, strong job markets in growing cities, and favorable tax treatment for military pensions. The Buckeye State offers one of the lowest costs of living in the nation combined with reasonable salaries and veteran-friendly communities.

## Ohio Veteran Financial Advantages

**Military Pension Tax Exemption:**
- All military retirement pensions exempt from Ohio income tax
- Survivor benefit plan (SBP) also fully exempt
- No limit on exemption amount
- Automatic exemption on tax filing

**Ohio Income Tax on Other Income:**
- State income tax rate: 0-5.75% (progressive)
- Sales tax: 5.75% state average (reasonable)
- No additional local income tax

**Tax Savings Example:**
Retiree with $45,000 pension + $25,000 other income:
- Tax on non-pension: approximately $1,438
- Pension tax: $0
- Annual advantage: all military pensions completely tax-free

Ohio offers possibly the most attractive financial package for military retirees seeking minimum cost of living combined with military pension tax exemption.
