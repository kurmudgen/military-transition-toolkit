## Onboarding Optimization

### The First Mile Problem

**Stat**: 40-60% of users who sign up never come back.

**Why**: Poor onboarding. Users don't understand value or how to get it.

**Solution**: Systematic onboarding that drives to "aha moment" quickly.

---

### The "Aha Moment" for Each App

**MTT**: "I just saw my personalized transition timeline and it's clear what I need to do."
- **Time to Aha**: < 5 minutes
- **Key Action**: Complete profile â†’ Generate timeline
- **Trigger**: Seeing personalized timeline with their MOS, ETS date, and milestones

**SpouseForce**: "There are remote jobs I can do, even with frequent moves."
- **Time to Aha**: < 3 minutes
- **Key Action**: Filter job database by "remote" + "portable certifications"
- **Trigger**: Seeing 50+ remote jobs they qualify for

**MMC**: "I can actually build wealth on military pay."
- **Time to Aha**: < 5 minutes

- **Trigger**: Seeing personalized budget with savings potential

### Community Growth Strategy

**Week 1-4**: Seed the community
- Invite first 100 users personally
- Post 3-5 times/day yourself (create activity)
- Answer every question within 2 hours
- Recruit 3-5 moderators from engaged users

**Week 5-8**: Grow to critical mass
- Promote in every email
- Link in every blog post
- Incentivize participation (badges, recognition)
- Host first live event (AMA, Q&A)

**Week 9-12**: Scale to self-sustaining
- 80% of questions answered by community (not you)
- Daily organic posts from members
- Identify and empower super-users

- Week 4: 100 members, 20 posts/week

### Community Moderation Guidelines

**Rules**:
1. **Be Respectful**: No personal attacks, hate speech, or harassment
