[INTRO]
**Hook:** 36B Financial specialists can earn $55K-$110K in accounting, finance, and federal positions.

**Context:** Your military finance experience translates to bookkeeper, accountant, financial analyst roles.

---

[MAIN CONTENT]

**Section 1: Corporate Accounting (90 sec)**
Entry bookkeeper: $40K-$55K | Staff accountant: $50K-$70K | Senior accountant: $70K-$95K | Accounting manager: $85K-$120K.

Every company needs accounting staff. Your financial management experience recognized. Focus: Accounts payable/receivable, payroll, general ledger, month-end close.

**Section 2: Public Accounting Firms (75 sec)**
Big 4 (Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG): $60K-$75K entry | Regional firms: $50K-$65K | Local CPAs: $45K-$60K.

Tax season busy (Jan-April) but good training. Path to CPA license. With bachelor's + CPA license: $80K-$110K mid-career.

**Section 3: Federal Government (75 sec)**
Accountant/auditor: GS-7 to GS-12, $50K-$100K | Defense Finance Accounting Service (DFAS): $55K-$90K | IRS: $50K-$85K.

Federal benefits + pension. Your military finance = direct fit. USAJobs.gov search "accountant" or "financial management specialist."

**Section 4: Banking/Finance (60 sec)**
Bank operations: $45K-$65K | Financial analyst: $60K-$85K | Credit analyst: $55K-$80K | Loan officer: $50K-$90K (commission-based).

Banks value your attention to detail and regulatory compliance experience.

**Section 5: Required Certifications (75 sec)**
**CPA (Certified Public Accountant):** Requires bachelor's in accounting + 150 credit hours + exam. Salary impact: $20K-$40K. Use GI Bill for degree.

**CMA (Certified Management Accountant):** $1,500 exam, salary boost $10K-$20K.

**QuickBooks/Excel certifications:** $200-$400, employer often pays.

Not required to start but boost career significantly.

**Section 6: Financial Planning (60 sec)**
Financial advisor: $55K-$100K+ (commission) | Financial planner: $60K-$95K | Wealth management: $75K-$130K.

Requires Series 7/65/66 licenses. Your military financial counseling experience helps. Build client base over time.

---

[CONCLUSION]
**Highest pay:** CPA at public accounting or corporate controller $85K-$120K
**Most stable:** Federal accountant $50K-$100K + pension
**Fastest hire:** Staff accountant at any company $50K-$70K

**Critical step:** Get bachelor's in accounting using GI Bill, then pursue CPA license.

**Next step:** Apply to corporate accounting roles, study for CPA exam, search USAJobs for GS-7/9 accountant positions.
