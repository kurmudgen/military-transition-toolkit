## UNIVERSAL RESUME RULES

**ATS-FRIENDLY FORMAT:**
✓ Simple fonts (Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman)
✓ NO tables, text boxes, images, headers/footers
✓ Standard section headings
✓ Bullet points with action verbs
✓ Keywords from job description

**LENGTH:**
- E1-E4: 1 page
- E5-E6: 1-2 pages
- E7+/Officers: 2 pages
- Federal resumes: 3-5 pages (different format)

**SECTIONS (in order):**
1. Contact Information
2. Professional Summary (3-4 lines)
3. Core Competencies (skills keywords)
4. Professional Experience
5. Education & Certifications
6. Awards (optional)

| Platoon Sergeant | Department Manager, Operations Director |
| Mission Planning | Project Planning, Strategic Planning |

## ACTION VERBS BY SKILL TYPE

**LEADERSHIP:**
Led, Supervised, Managed, Directed, Coordinated, Mentored, Trained, Developed

**PROJECT/OPERATIONS:**
Executed, Implemented, Streamlined, Optimized, Planned, Organized, Facilitated

**PROBLEM-SOLVING:**
Resolved, Troubleshot, Diagnosed, Identified, Analyzed, Assessed, Evaluated

**COMMUNICATION:**
Briefed, Presented, Communicated, Documented, Reported, Liaised, Collaborated

**ACHIEVEMENT:**
Achieved, Exceeded, Increased, Improved, Reduced, Generated, Saved, Eliminated

## QUANTIFYING ACHIEVEMENTS

**Make every bullet point measurable:**

❌ **Weak:** "Led team of soldiers"
✅ **Strong:** "Led team of 12 soldiers achieving 100% mission success rate"

❌ **Weak:** "Managed equipment"
✅ **Strong:** "Managed $2.5M in equipment with 100% accountability"

❌ **Weak:** "Trained soldiers"
✅ **Strong:** "Trained 40+ soldiers resulting in 98% qualification rate"

❌ **Weak:** "Provided IT support"
✅ **Strong:** "Resolved 500+ IT tickets monthly with 95% first-contact resolution"

## FEDERAL RESUME FORMAT (USAJOBS)

**Key Differences:**
- **Length:** 3-5 pages (detailed)
- **Dates:** Include month/day/year and hours per week
- **Salary:** Include salary for each position
- **Supervisor:** Include supervisor name and phone
- **Duties:** Extremely detailed (1-2 pages per job)

**Federal Resume Sections:**
1. Contact Information
2. Citizenship
3. Veterans' Preference
4. Security Clearance
5. Work Experience (DETAILED)
6. Education
7. Certifications
8. Additional Information
