## Presentation Delivery Tips

### Before the Meeting:
1. Research the VSO thoroughly (size, membership demographics, current services, recent initiatives)
2. Customize slides with VSO's specific data where possible
3. Prepare demo accounts in advance
4. Load presentation on backup device
5. Test screen sharing if virtual presentation
6. Prepare printed leave-behinds

### During the Presentation:
1. Start on time and respect their schedule
2. Read the room—adjust pace and depth based on engagement
3. Encourage questions throughout, not just at the end
4. Use specific examples relevant to their membership
5. Listen more than you talk—understand their needs
6. Take notes on concerns and priorities
7. Be prepared to skip appendix slides if time is limited

### After the Presentation:
1. Send demo accounts within 24 hours
2. Follow up with detailed proposal within 48 hours
3. Schedule follow-up meeting before leaving
4. Send thank you email with key discussion points
5. Provide additional information requested
6. Stay responsive to questions

### Handling Common Objections:

**"We already have career counselors"**
→ "Exactly, and this makes them more effective by handling routine questions digitally, so they can focus on complex cases requiring human expertise."

**"Our members aren't that tech-savvy"**
→ "The platform is designed for all skill levels, mobile-friendly, and has phone/email support. Plus, our data shows 85% of post-9/11 veterans expect digital tools—this serves multiple generations."

**"This seems expensive"**
→ "At $2-$15 per member per year with unlimited access, compared to $200-$500+ for traditional career counseling, the ROI is compelling. Plus, we can start with a discounted pilot program."

**"We need to see it work first"**
→ "That's exactly why we offer pilot programs. Six months, 500-1,000 members, full analytics, and no long-term commitment."

**"We're not sure our members will use it"**
→ "We provide all member communication materials, your staff receives training on how to promote it, and we track adoption metrics. Most importantly, we make it valuable enough that members want to use it."

**"What if the platform goes down or has issues?"**
→ "We guarantee 99.9% uptime, have 24-hour support response times, and technical issues are resolved quickly. We also have backup systems and regular security audits."
