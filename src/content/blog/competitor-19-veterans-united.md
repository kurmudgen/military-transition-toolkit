---# Competitor Analysis: Veterans United Home Loans

**Category:** Benefits & Resources (Home Loans + Resources)

**Last Updated:** November 2025

## 1. Platform Overview

**Core Mission:** #1 VA home loan lender providing no-down-payment VA mortgages plus transition resources including credit counseling, homebuyer education, and relocation support.

**Target Audience:** Veterans, active duty service members, surviving spouses buying homes
**Founded:** VA loan specialist (established lender)
**Type:** For-profit mortgage lender (nonbank)
**Scale:** #1 VA lender nationally, 442,000+ reviews, 55,000+ assisted with credit counseling

---

## 2. Features

### ✅ VA Home Loans
- **No down payment** - 0% down on home purchase
- **No PMI** - Private mortgage insurance not required
- **Low rates** - Often below national average
- **Purchase loans** - Buy primary residence
- **Refinancing** - Cash-out, rate reduction (IRRRL)
- **Jumbo loans** - High-value properties
- **34-day average close** - Faster than national average

### ✅ Digital Platform
- **MyVeteransUnited portal** - Track loan progress, upload docs, manage payments
- **Mobile app** - iOS and Android
- **24/7 support** - Phone line (1-800-884-5560)
- **Calculators** - Affordability, payment estimators

### ✅ Support Services (FREE)
- **Credit counseling** - 55,000+ helped improve credit
- **Homebuyer education** - Course-style learning
- **Veterans United Realty** - 6,700+ agent network
- **Homebuyer Concierge** - Utilities, movers, local resources
- **Education resources** - VA loan benefits library

### ❌ Gaps
- Home loans only (not comprehensive transition)
- No VA disability claims assistance
- No job search or career tools
- No state benefits comparison (beyond housing)
- No transition checklist
- No community or peer networking
- No retirement/financial planning beyond mortgage
- No healthcare navigation
- Higher origination fees than some competitors

## 3. Pricing

**For Veterans/Borrowers:**
- **No down payment required** - 0% down
- **Origination fee:** Higher than average per federal data
- **Interest rates:** Competitive, often below national average
- **Credit score minimum:** 620 recommended (higher than some competitors)
- **No PMI** - Saves $100-200+/month
- **VA funding fee** - 2.15-3.3% (can be financed, waived for disabled veterans)

**Monetization:** Mortgage origination fees, interest income, refinancing

### Underserved Segments:
- **Pre-purchase veterans** - Need to maximize VA disability rating BEFORE buying (rating affects funding fee)
- **Relocating veterans** - Need job search + state benefits in new location, not just mortgage
- **Renters** - Not ready to buy but need housing guidance

### Key Insight:
Veterans United customers are **making major financial decisions** (home purchase) and need **comprehensive planning**:
- **VA disability rating affects funding fee** - 10%+ saves 1.65%, 30%+ waives fee entirely
- **State benefits vary** - Property tax exemptions, veteran homestead protection differs by state
- **Relocation = transition** - New city means job search, schools, community

MTT should **partner with Veterans United** to provide comprehensive transition support:
- **Before purchase:** Maximize VA disability rating (save on funding fee)
- **During purchase:** State benefits comparison, relocation planning
- **After purchase:** Community connection, ongoing financial optimization
