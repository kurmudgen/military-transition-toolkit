# Certifications Under $4,000 Covered by MyCAA: Complete List

## Bottom Line Up Front

MyCAA's **$4,000 education benefit** can fully cover dozens of career certifications in high-demand fields. The best certifications combine **low cost + high job demand + portability**. Top choices include medical coding (CPC), IT certifications (CompTIA), project management (CAPM), and bookkeeping. Most can be completed in **3-6 months** and lead to jobs paying **$40,000-$70,000+** that work remotely or exist at every duty station.

## The Military Spouse Career Challenge

Certifications offer advantages degrees don't:
- Complete in months, not years
- Often fully covered by MyCAA
- Demonstrate specific, marketable skills
- Many lead to remote work
- Recognized nationally (no state licensure)

For military spouses, certifications provide the fastest path from "PCS unemployed" to "gainfully employed."

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I get multiple certifications with MyCAA?**
A: Yes, up to the $4,000 cap and $2,000/year limit. Plan strategically.

**Q: What if the exam is separate from training?**
A: Some programs include exam fees; others don't. Verify before enrolling.

**Q: Are online certification programs accepted by employers?**
A: Yes, most certifications are based on passing an exam, regardless of how you prepared.

**Q: How long do certifications last?**
A: Varies—some require renewal/CEUs, others are lifetime. Check specific certification.

**Q: Can I start before getting MyCAA approval?**
A: No, wait for approval. Starting early may void your funding eligibility.
