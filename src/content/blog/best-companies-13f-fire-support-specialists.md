**Do write:** "Operations and tactical analyst professional with expertise in tactical planning, operational analysis, decision support, and command coordination"

### 2. Highlight Key Skills

- Tactical analysis and planning
- Operations management and coordination
- Decision-making under pressure
- Command structure understanding
- Communication and briefing skills
- Targeting and prioritization
- Intelligence integration
- Numbers: "Coordinated 500+ operations," "Supported command decisions," "Analyzed tactical situations for 100+ targets"

- **Operations Research Analyst certification:** Career advancement

### 4. Target Federal Agencies

- Higher pay than contractors (often)
- Better job security
- Federal pension benefits

- Clearance continuation valued

### 5. Strategic Application

- Apply to 15-20+ defense contractors and agencies
- Target analyst positions emphasizing operations
- Look for advancement to manager/director roles
- Build toward executive leadership
- Emphasize decision-making and leadership
