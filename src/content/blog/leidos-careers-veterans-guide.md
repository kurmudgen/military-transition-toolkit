# Leidos Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

Leidos is one of the largest and most diversified government contractors in the United States. Originally spun off from SAIC in 2013, Leidos has grown through acquisitions (including Lockheed Martin's IT division) to become a powerhouse in defense, intelligence, health, and civil markets.

With approximately 45,000 employees and $15+ billion in annual revenue, Leidos offers veterans diverse career opportunities across government sectors.

## Leidos Hiring Process

### Step 1: Apply

**Where to apply:**
- leidos.com/careers
- ClearanceJobs
- LinkedIn
- Indeed

### Step 2: Screening

Review for:
- Basic qualifications
- Clearance eligibility
- Experience match
- Education verification

### Step 3: Interviews

Multiple rounds typical:

**Phone Screen:**
- Initial qualification
- Interest confirmation
- Background discussion

**Technical Interview:**
- Skills assessment
- Role-specific questions
- Problem-solving

**Panel/Manager Interview:**
- Team fit
- Behavioral questions
- Career discussion

### Step 4: Selection

Final steps:
- Reference checks
- Background verification
- Security processing
- Offer presentation

### Timeline

Typical timeline:
- Application to response: 1-3 weeks
- Interview process: 2-5 weeks
- Offer to start: 2-4 weeks
- Total: 5-12 weeks
