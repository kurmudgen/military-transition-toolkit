# ManTech Careers for Veterans: Complete Guide

ManTech International Corporation is a leading provider of mission-focused technology solutions for defense, intelligence, and federal civilian agencies. With deep roots in the intelligence community and a strong focus on cyber operations, ManTech offers veterans—especially those with cyber, intelligence, or IT backgrounds—excellent career opportunities.

With approximately 10,000 employees and over $2.5 billion in annual revenue, ManTech specializes in the high-end, cleared work that veterans are uniquely qualified to perform.

## ManTech Hiring Process

### Step 1: Apply

**Where to apply:**
- mantech.com/careers
- ClearanceJobs
- LinkedIn
- Indeed

### Step 2: Screening

Review for:
- Clearance status (critical)
- Technical qualifications
- Experience match
- Certification verification

### Step 3: Interviews

Multiple rounds:

**Phone Screen:**
- Clearance verification
- Background discussion
- Interest confirmation

**Technical Interview:**
- Skills assessment
- Domain expertise
- Problem-solving

**Customer Interview:**
- Some positions require
- Client approval
- Mission fit

### Step 4: Offer

If selected:
- Compensation discussion
- Benefits review
- Start date (depends on clearance processing)
- Security processing

### Timeline

Typical timeline:
- Application to response: 1-2 weeks
- Interview process: 2-4 weeks
- Offer to start: Varies by clearance status

Active TS/SCI can start very quickly; others depend on clearance timeline.
