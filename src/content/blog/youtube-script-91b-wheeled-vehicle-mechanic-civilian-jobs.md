**Section 5: ASE Certifications (Career Multiplier) (90 seconds)**

[Visual suggestion: ASE certification list]

ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certs are industry standard and increase pay significantly.

**Automotive ASE tests:**
- A1: Engine Repair
- A2: Automatic Transmission
- A3: Manual Drive Train & Axles
- A4: Suspension & Steering
- A5: Brakes
- A6: Electrical/Electronic Systems
- A7: Heating & Air Conditioning
- A8: Engine Performance
- A9: Light Vehicle Diesel Engines

**Truck ASE tests (higher pay):**
- T1-T8: Various heavy truck systems
- T1: Gasoline Engines
- T2: Diesel Engines
- T3: Drive Train
- T4: Brakes
- T5: Suspension & Steering
- T6: Electrical/Electronic Systems
- T7: Heating, Ventilation & A/C
- T8: Preventive Maintenance

**Master certification:**
- Pass all 8 automotive tests = ASE Master Tech
- Pass all 8 truck tests = ASE Master Truck Tech
- Salary boost: $10,000-$20,000/year

**Cost:** $44 per test
- 8 tests = $352 total
- Study materials: $100-$200

**DoD COOL covers this:** Get ASE tests free while in or within 180 days of separation.

**ROI:** $352 investment = $10,000+ annual salary increase

**Study time:** 1-2 months per test. You know this stuff from Army.
