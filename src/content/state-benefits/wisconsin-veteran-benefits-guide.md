# Wisconsin Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

Wisconsin—America's Dairyland—offers veterans full military retirement tax exemption, excellent state veteran benefits including the Wisconsin GI Bill, property tax credits, and strong VA healthcare. With affordable living, strong manufacturing economy, and Midwestern values, Wisconsin provides veterans solid opportunities and quality of life.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 320,000 veterans (7.0% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: Full military retirement tax exemption, Wisconsin GI Bill, property tax credits, health care aid
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state tax on military retirement pay
  2. Wisconsin GI Bill (excellent state education benefit)
  3. Affordable cost of living
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Cold winters
  2. Higher state taxes on other income
  3. Limited major military installations
- **Best cities for veterans**: Madison, Milwaukee, Green Bay, Appleton, La Crosse

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Credits**
- Veterans and Surviving Spouses: Property tax credit
- Disabled veterans: Additional credits

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Military retirement pay: 100% exempt from Wisconsin income tax
- VA disability compensation: Not taxed
- SBP/DIC payments: Not taxed
- WI income tax: 3.54% to 7.65%

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates

**Hunting and Fishing Licenses**
- Disabled veterans: Free licenses
- Resident veterans: Discounted licenses

### Education Benefits

**Wisconsin GI Bill**
- Full tuition remission at UW System schools
- 128 credits or 8 semesters
- One of best state education benefits
- For qualifying veterans and eligible family members

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Choice Act compliant

**Public University Programs**
- UW-Madison: Strong veteran services
- All UW System schools have veteran support

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- Veterans: Preference in state hiring
- Disabled veterans: Additional preference

### Healthcare Benefits

**Wisconsin Veterans Homes (3 Facilities)**
- King: 721 beds (one of largest in nation)
- Union Grove: 158 beds
- Chippewa Falls: 72 beds

**Veterans Health Care Aid Program**
- Financial assistance for healthcare costs

## Common Questions

**Q: Does Wisconsin tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from Wisconsin income tax.

**Q: What is the Wisconsin GI Bill?**
A: Excellent state benefit providing full tuition remission (128 credits or 8 semesters) at UW System schools.

**Q: How cold is Wisconsin?**
A: Cold. January averages around 15-20°F. Significant snow. Dress warm.

**Q: What about Oshkosh Corporation?**
A: Major defense contractor in Oshkosh. Builds military vehicles (JLTV, FMTV). Good employer for veterans.
