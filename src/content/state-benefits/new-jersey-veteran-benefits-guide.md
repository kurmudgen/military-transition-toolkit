# New Jersey Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

New Jersey—the Garden State—offers veterans excellent benefits including full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, strong veteran preference, and access to major job markets in the NYC-Philadelphia corridor. With Joint Base McGuire-Dix-Lakehurst, multiple VA facilities, and high-paying job opportunities, New Jersey provides veterans strong benefits despite higher cost of living.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 350,000 veterans (5.0% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: Full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, veteran preference, NJ VET Program, free transit for disabled veterans
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state tax on military retirement pay
  2. Access to NYC and Philadelphia job markets
  3. Joint Base McGuire-Dix-Lakehurst
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. High cost of living and property taxes
  2. High overall state taxes
  3. Traffic congestion
- **Best cities for veterans**: Cherry Hill, Trenton, Edison, Jersey City, Newark

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Complete property tax exemption
- Surviving spouses: May continue exemption
- Must be NJ resident

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Military retirement pay: 100% exempt from NJ income tax
- VA disability compensation: Not taxed
- SBP/DIC payments: Not taxed
- NJ income tax: 1.4% to 10.75% (high rates, but military retirement exempt)

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates
- Medal of Honor: Free

**Transit Benefits**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Free NJ Transit system-wide
- Significant savings in commuter corridor

### Education Benefits

**NJ VET (Veterans Education and Transition) Program**
- Tuition-free for qualifying veterans at community colleges
- Covers tuition and fees

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Active duty and families: In-state rates
- Choice Act compliant

**Public University Programs**
- Rutgers University: Veteran services, Yellow Ribbon
- NJIT: Military-friendly programs
- All state colleges have veteran services

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- Veterans: Preference in state hiring
- Disabled veterans: Additional preference
- Applies to state and local positions

**State Hiring Programs**
- NJ Department of Labor veteran services
- One-Stop Career Centers: Priority of Service

**Professional Licensing**
- Military experience credited toward licensing
- Expedited licensing for veterans and spouses

### Healthcare Benefits

**NJ Veterans Memorial Homes (3 Facilities)**
- Paramus: 336 beds
- Menlo Park: 312 beds
- Vineland: 300 beds

## Common Questions

**Q: Does New Jersey tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from New Jersey income tax.

**Q: What about property taxes?**
A: NJ has the highest property taxes in the nation. 100% disabled veterans get complete exemption.

**Q: Is free NJ Transit really available?**
A: Yes. 100% disabled veterans ride NJ Transit free—buses, light rail, and commuter rail.

**Q: What military installations are in New Jersey?**
A: Joint Base McGuire-Dix-Lakehurst (Air Force, Army, Navy joint base)—one of the largest in the Northeast.

**Q: How is the NYC job market access?**
A: Excellent via NJ Transit and PATH. Many veterans commute to NYC for higher salaries.
