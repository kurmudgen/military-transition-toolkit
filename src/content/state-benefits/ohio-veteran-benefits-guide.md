# Ohio Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

Ohio—the Buckeye State—offers veterans strong benefits including full military retirement tax exemption, substantial property tax exemptions, excellent VA healthcare, and diverse job markets. With Wright-Patterson AFB, multiple VA medical centers, and affordable cost of living, Ohio provides veterans solid opportunities in America's heartland.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 680,000 veterans (7.5% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: Full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, Ohio War Orphans Scholarship, veterans preference
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state tax on military retirement pay
  2. Affordable cost of living
  3. Wright-Patterson AFB and defense jobs
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Cold, gray winters
  2. Some areas have struggling economies
  3. Variable job markets by region
- **Best cities for veterans**: Columbus, Cincinnati, Cleveland, Dayton, Toledo

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Full property tax exemption
- Disabled veterans: Partial exemptions based on disability
- Homestead exemption: Additional benefit for 65+

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Military retirement pay: 100% exempt from Ohio income tax
- VA disability compensation: Not taxed
- SBP/DIC payments: Not taxed
- Ohio income tax: 0% to 3.75%

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates
- Medal of Honor: Free

### Education Benefits

**Ohio War Orphans Scholarship**
- Children of deceased/disabled veterans: Full tuition at Ohio public schools
- Covers room and board

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Choice Act compliant

**Public University Programs**
- Ohio State: Veteran services, Yellow Ribbon
- All state universities have veteran services

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- 20% of points added for veterans
- Additional for disabled veterans
- Strong state preference

### Healthcare Benefits

**Ohio Veterans Homes (2 Facilities)**
- Sandusky: 436 beds
- Georgetown: 268 beds

## Common Questions

**Q: Does Ohio tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from Ohio income tax.

**Q: What is Wright-Patterson AFB?**
A: Major Air Force installation in Dayton. Hosts Air Force Materiel Command, research labs. Major veteran employer.

**Q: Is the Ohio War Orphans Scholarship good?**
A: Excellent. Covers full tuition and room/board at Ohio public universities.
