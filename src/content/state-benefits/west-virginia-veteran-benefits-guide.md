# West Virginia Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

West Virginia—the Mountain State—offers veterans full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, and one of the lowest costs of living in America. With strong community values, natural beauty, and affordable living, West Virginia attracts veterans seeking peaceful mountain living and strong veteran benefits.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 130,000 veterans (9.0% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: Full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, free college for veterans, hunting/fishing benefits
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state tax on military retirement pay
  2. Lowest cost of living in nation
  3. Strong outdoor recreation
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Limited job market
  2. Limited VA healthcare facilities
  3. Aging infrastructure in some areas
- **Best cities for veterans**: Charleston, Huntington, Morgantown, Martinsburg, Parkersburg

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Full property tax exemption
- Disabled veterans: Homestead exemption
- Surviving spouses: May continue exemptions

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Military retirement pay: 100% exempt from WV income tax
- VA disability compensation: Not taxed
- SBP/DIC payments: Not taxed
- WV income tax: 2.36% to 5.12%

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates

**Hunting and Fishing Licenses**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Free lifetime license
- Resident veterans: Discounted licenses

### Education Benefits

**West Virginia Veterans Upskilling and Retraining**
- Free community and technical college for veterans
- Excellent benefit

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Choice Act compliant

**Public University Programs**
- WVU: Veteran services
- Marshall University: Military-friendly

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- Veterans: Preference in state hiring
- Disabled veterans: Additional preference

### Healthcare Benefits

**West Virginia Veterans Nursing Facility (Clarksburg)**
- 120 beds
- Skilled nursing care

## Common Questions

**Q: Does West Virginia tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from WV income tax.

**Q: Is free college really available?**
A: Yes. Veterans can attend WV community and technical colleges tuition-free.

**Q: Can I commute to DC from Martinsburg?**
A: Yes. Many veterans in Eastern Panhandle commute to DC metro via MARC train or car.

**Q: Is the job market limited?**
A: Yes outside Charleston, Morgantown, and Martinsburg. Consider remote work opportunities.
