# South Dakota Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

South Dakota—Mount Rushmore State—offers veterans excellent tax benefits with no state income tax, no inheritance tax, property tax exemptions, and affordable living. With Ellsworth AFB and low cost of living, South Dakota attracts veterans seeking tax advantages and quality of life in America's heartland.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 65,000 veterans (9.2% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: No state income tax (military retirement not taxed), property tax exemptions, tuition waivers, hunting/fishing benefits
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state income tax on any income
  2. Very low cost of living
  3. Strong outdoor recreation
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Cold winters
  2. Limited job market
  3. Limited VA healthcare facilities
- **Best cities for veterans**: Sioux Falls, Rapid City, Aberdeen, Pierre, Brookings

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans: Property tax exemption based on disability
- Paraplegic veterans: Full exemption
- Surviving spouses: May continue exemptions

**Income Tax Benefits**
- South Dakota has NO state income tax
- Military retirement: Not taxed
- VA disability: Not taxed
- All income types: Not taxed

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates

**Hunting and Fishing Licenses**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Free licenses
- Resident veterans: Discounted combination licenses

### Education Benefits

**Free Tuition for Veterans' Dependents**
- Children of deceased/disabled veterans: Free tuition at state schools
- Guard members: Tuition assistance

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Choice Act compliant

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- Veterans: Preference in state hiring
- Disabled veterans: Additional preference

### Healthcare Benefits

**SD Veterans Home (Hot Springs)**
- 100 beds
- Michael J. Fitzmaurice State Veterans Home
