# Wyoming Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

Wyoming—the Cowboy State—offers veterans no state income tax, property tax exemptions, and outstanding outdoor recreation with the lowest population density in the nation. With affordable living, strong veteran appreciation, and wide-open spaces, Wyoming attracts veterans seeking tax advantages, outdoor lifestyle, and escape from crowded cities.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 45,000 veterans (10.0% of adult population—among highest)
- **Key state benefits**: No state income tax, property tax exemptions, free college for dependents, hunting/fishing benefits
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state income tax on any income
  2. Outstanding outdoor recreation
  3. Low cost of living and no crowds
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Limited VA healthcare facilities
  2. Limited job market
  3. Cold winters with extreme wind
- **Best cities for veterans**: Cheyenne, Casper, Laramie, Gillette, Rock Springs

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Property tax exemption
- Veterans: Various exemptions based on service
- Surviving spouses: May continue exemptions

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Wyoming has NO state income tax
- Military retirement: Not taxed
- VA disability: Not taxed
- All income: Not taxed

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates

**Hunting and Fishing Licenses**
- Disabled veterans: Reduced-fee licenses
- Resident veterans: Discounted Pioneer license (65+)

### Education Benefits

**Free Tuition for Veteran Dependents**
- Children of deceased/disabled veterans: Free tuition at state schools
- Excellent benefit

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Choice Act compliant

**Public University Programs**
- University of Wyoming: Veteran services
- Community colleges: Veteran support

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- Veterans: Preference in state hiring
- Disabled veterans: Additional preference

### Healthcare Benefits

**Wyoming Veterans Home (Buffalo)**
- 52 beds
- Skilled nursing and domiciliary

## Common Questions

**Q: Does Wyoming tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Wyoming has no state income tax on any income.

**Q: What military installation is in Wyoming?**
A: F.E. Warren AFB (Cheyenne) operates Minuteman III ICBMs. Major employer.

**Q: Is Wyoming really that empty?**
A: Yes. Lowest population density in nation. Entire state has about 580,000 people.

**Q: How's the weather?**
A: Cold winters with significant wind. High altitude, sunny, dry. Summer temps moderate.

**Q: Is the job market limited?**
A: Yes. Energy sector dominates. Consider remote work opportunities.
