# Virginia Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

Virginia—the Old Dominion—offers veterans full military retirement tax exemption, strong property tax benefits, and unmatched access to defense and federal employment. With the Pentagon, major military installations, and hundreds of defense contractors, Virginia is the premier destination for veterans seeking defense and government careers.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 700,000 veterans (10.0% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: Full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, Virginia Military Survivors and Dependents Program, veteran preference
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state tax on military retirement pay
  2. Pentagon and defense contractor jobs
  3. Major military installations throughout state
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. High cost of living (NoVA)
  2. Significant traffic (NoVA)
  3. Lower salaries outside NoVA
- **Best cities for veterans**: Northern Virginia, Virginia Beach, Richmond, Norfolk, Hampton Roads

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Complete real estate tax exemption
- Surviving spouses: May continue exemptions
- Localities may offer additional exemptions

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Military retirement pay: 100% exempt from Virginia income tax
- VA disability compensation: Not taxed
- SBP/DIC payments: Not taxed
- VA income tax: 2% to 5.75%

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates
- Medal of Honor: Free

### Education Benefits

**Virginia Military Survivors and Dependents Program (VMSDEP)**
- Children/spouses of deceased/disabled veterans: Free tuition at state schools
- Excellent education benefit

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Choice Act compliant

**Public University Programs**
- Virginia Tech: Veteran services
- UVA: Military-friendly
- George Mason: Strong veteran support (NoVA)
- All state schools have veteran services

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- Veterans: Preference in state hiring
- Disabled veterans: Additional preference
- Excellent federal/contractor hiring in NoVA

### Healthcare Benefits

**Virginia Veterans Care Centers (2 Facilities)**
- Roanoke: 160 beds
- Richmond: 200 beds

## Common Questions

**Q: Does Virginia tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from Virginia income tax.

**Q: Is NoVA worth the cost?**
A: For defense/federal careers, yes. Salaries offset cost of living. Consider commuting from outer areas.

**Q: What's the Hampton Roads military presence?**
A: Naval Station Norfolk (world's largest), Newport News Shipbuilding, Langley AFB, Fort Eustis.

**Q: Is VMSDEP a good education benefit?**
A: Excellent. Covers children/spouses of deceased/disabled veterans at Virginia public colleges.
