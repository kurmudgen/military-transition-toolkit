# South Carolina Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

South Carolina—the Palmetto State—offers veterans outstanding benefits with full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, excellent state veteran programs, and warm climate. With major military installations including Fort Jackson, Shaw AFB, and Joint Base Charleston, plus a low cost of living, South Carolina is a top destination for military retirees.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 400,000 veterans (10.0% of adult population—one of highest)
- **Key state benefits**: Full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, free higher education for SC National Guard, strong veteran preference
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state tax on military retirement pay
  2. Major military installations and defense jobs
  3. Low cost of living with warm climate
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Hot, humid summers
  2. Hurricane risk on coast
  3. Lower salaries compared to national average
- **Best cities for veterans**: Columbia, Charleston, Greenville, Myrtle Beach, Summerville

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Complete property tax exemption on primary residence
- Surviving spouses: May continue exemptions
- All veterans 65+: Additional exemption

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Military retirement pay: 100% exempt from SC income tax
- VA disability compensation: Not taxed
- SBP/DIC payments: Not taxed
- SC income tax: 0% to 6.4%

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates
- Medal of Honor: Free

### Education Benefits

**SC National Guard Free Tuition**
- Guard members: Free tuition at state schools
- Excellent benefit

**Tuition Assistance for Veterans' Children**
- Children of disabled/deceased veterans: Free tuition at state schools

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Choice Act compliant

**Public University Programs**
- USC: Veteran services
- Clemson: Military-friendly
- The Citadel: Military college

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- Veterans: Preference in state hiring
- Disabled veterans: Additional preference
- Strong state commitment

### Healthcare Benefits

**SC Veterans Homes (4 Facilities)**
- Anderson, Columbia, Florence, Walterboro

## Common Questions

**Q: Does South Carolina tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from SC income tax.

**Q: What military installations are in South Carolina?**
A: Fort Jackson (largest Army training base), Joint Base Charleston, Shaw AFB, Parris Island (Marine Corps), and more.

**Q: Is Charleston expensive?**
A: More expensive than rest of state but still reasonable. Downtown Charleston is pricier; suburbs like Summerville more affordable.

**Q: Is the coast hurricane-prone?**
A: Yes. Coastal SC has hurricane risk. Inland areas like Columbia and Greenville are safer.
