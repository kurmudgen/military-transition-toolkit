# North Dakota Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

North Dakota—the Peace Garden State—offers veterans excellent benefits with full military retirement tax exemption, property tax credits, tuition waivers, and one of the lowest unemployment rates in the nation. With affordable living, strong economy driven by energy and agriculture, and growing job market, North Dakota provides veterans solid opportunities despite cold winters.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 52,000 veterans (8.7% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: Full military retirement tax exemption, property tax credits, tuition waivers for dependents, hunting/fishing benefits
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state tax on military retirement pay
  2. Very low unemployment rate
  3. Affordable cost of living
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Extremely cold winters
  2. Limited VA healthcare facilities
  3. Remote location
- **Best cities for veterans**: Fargo, Bismarck, Grand Forks, Minot

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Credits**
- Disabled veterans: Property tax credit based on disability
- 100% disabled: Significant property tax exemption
- Surviving spouses: May continue credits

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Military retirement pay: 100% exempt from ND income tax
- VA disability compensation: Not taxed
- SBP/DIC payments: Not taxed
- ND income tax: 0% to 2.5% (one of lowest in nation)

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates

**Hunting and Fishing Licenses**
- Disabled veterans (50%+): Free licenses
- Resident veterans: Discounted combination licenses

### Education Benefits

**Dependents Tuition Waiver**
- Children of deceased/disabled veterans: Tuition waiver
- North Dakota public universities

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Choice Act compliant

**Public University Programs**
- NDSU: Veteran services
- UND: Military-friendly

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- Veterans: Preference in state hiring
- Disabled veterans: Additional preference

### Healthcare Benefits

**ND Veterans Home (Lisbon)**
- 150 beds
- Skilled nursing care

## Common Questions

**Q: Does North Dakota tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from ND income tax.

**Q: What military installations are in North Dakota?**
A: Minot AFB (B-52s, nuclear mission) and Grand Forks AFB (RQ-4 Global Hawk drones).

**Q: How cold is North Dakota?**
A: Very cold. January averages around 5-10°F. Wind chill can reach -30°F or colder.
