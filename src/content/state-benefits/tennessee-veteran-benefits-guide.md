# Tennessee Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

Tennessee—the Volunteer State—offers veterans no state income tax, property tax exemptions, and affordable living with southern hospitality. With Nashville's booming economy, Knoxville, Memphis, and Fort Campbell nearby, Tennessee is a top destination for military retirees seeking tax advantages and quality of life.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 450,000 veterans (8.4% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: No state income tax, property tax exemptions, free higher education for dependents, hunting/fishing benefits
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state income tax on any income
  2. Low cost of living
  3. Fort Campbell and growing job markets
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Hot, humid summers
  2. Tornado risk in middle/west TN
  3. Growing traffic in Nashville
- **Best cities for veterans**: Nashville, Knoxville, Clarksville, Chattanooga, Memphis

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Full property tax exemption
- Disabled veterans (10%+): Partial exemptions
- Surviving spouses: May continue exemptions

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Tennessee has NO state income tax
- Military retirement: Not taxed
- VA disability: Not taxed
- All income: Not taxed

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates

**Hunting and Fishing Licenses**
- Disabled veterans: Free licenses
- Resident veterans: Discounted licenses

### Education Benefits

**Dependents Education Benefits**
- Children of deceased/disabled veterans: Free college at state schools
- Vietnam Veterans Children: Free higher education

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Choice Act compliant

**Tennessee Promise**
- Free community college for qualifying students

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- Veterans: Preference in state hiring
- Disabled veterans: Additional preference

### Healthcare Benefits

**Tennessee State Veterans Homes (4 Facilities)**
- Clarksville, Humboldt, Knoxville, Murfreesboro

## Common Questions

**Q: Does Tennessee tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Tennessee has no state income tax on any income.

**Q: Is Fort Campbell in Tennessee?**
A: Fort Campbell straddles the KY-TN border. Clarksville, TN is the primary support city.

**Q: Is Nashville expensive?**
A: Getting more expensive, but still reasonable compared to comparable-sized metros.
