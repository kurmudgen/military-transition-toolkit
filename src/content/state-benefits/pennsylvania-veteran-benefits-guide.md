# Pennsylvania Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

Pennsylvania—the Keystone State—offers veterans excellent benefits including full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, one of the largest state veterans home systems, and strong veteran preference. With diverse job markets in Philadelphia, Pittsburgh, and throughout, Pennsylvania provides veterans solid opportunities in America's history-rich Mid-Atlantic region.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 750,000 veterans (7.5% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: Full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, free transit for disabled veterans, blind and paralyzed veteran assistance
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state tax on military retirement pay
  2. Strong VA presence with multiple medical centers
  3. Diverse job markets (Philadelphia, Pittsburgh)
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Higher property taxes in some areas
  2. Variable job markets by region
  3. Cold winters
- **Best cities for veterans**: Philadelphia, Pittsburgh, Harrisburg, Allentown, Scranton

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Full property tax exemption
- Wartime veterans: Various local exemptions
- Blind veterans: Special benefits

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Military retirement pay: 100% exempt from PA income tax
- VA disability compensation: Not taxed
- SBP/DIC payments: Not taxed
- PA income tax: 3.07% flat rate (doesn't apply to military retirement)

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates

**Transit Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free SEPTA (Philadelphia), PAT (Pittsburgh)
- Significant savings in urban areas

**Paralyzed Veterans Program**
- Grants for home modifications
- Vehicle grants

### Education Benefits

**Educational Gratuity Program**
- Children of deceased veterans: Financial assistance
- Covers PA colleges

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Choice Act compliant

**Public University Programs**
- Penn State: Veteran services, Yellow Ribbon
- Temple, Pitt: Strong veteran support
- All PASSHE schools have veteran services

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- 10 points for veterans
- Additional for disabled veterans
- Applies to state positions

### Healthcare Benefits

**Pennsylvania Veterans Homes (6 Facilities)**
- Hollidaysburg, Scranton, Pittsburgh, Erie, Spring City, Fort Indiantown Gap

## Common Questions

**Q: Does Pennsylvania tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from PA income tax.

**Q: What property tax exemption can I get?**
A: 100% disabled veterans get full property tax exemption. Various local exemptions for other veterans.

**Q: Is free transit available?**
A: Yes. Disabled veterans ride SEPTA (Philadelphia) and PAT (Pittsburgh) for free.

**Q: What military installations are in Pennsylvania?**
A: Fort Indiantown Gap (National Guard), Carlisle Barracks (Army War College), various reserve/guard facilities.
