# New Mexico Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

New Mexico—the Land of Enchantment—offers veterans outstanding benefits including full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, and access to major military installations including Kirtland AFB, White Sands Missile Range, and Holloman AFB. With affordable living, rich culture, and beautiful landscapes, New Mexico attracts veterans seeking southwestern living with strong military community.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 155,000 veterans (9.2% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: Full military retirement tax exemption, property tax exemptions, free tuition for dependents, veterans preference
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state tax on military retirement pay
  2. Major military installations and defense jobs
  3. Low cost of living and beautiful scenery
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Lower average salaries
  2. Limited job market outside military/government
  3. Remote location for some areas
- **Best cities for veterans**: Albuquerque, Las Cruces, Rio Rancho, Santa Fe, Alamogordo

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Full property tax exemption
- Disabled veterans: Partial exemptions based on disability
- Surviving spouses: May continue exemptions

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Military retirement pay: 100% exempt from NM income tax
- VA disability compensation: Not taxed
- SBP/DIC payments: Not taxed
- NM income tax: 1.7% to 5.9%

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates

**Hunting and Fishing Licenses**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Free licenses
- Resident veterans: Discounted licenses

### Education Benefits

**Vietnam Veterans Scholarship**
- Children of Vietnam veterans: Tuition waiver
- New Mexico public universities

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Active duty and families: In-state rates
- Choice Act compliant

**Wartime Veterans Scholarship**
- Covers children of wartime veterans who became disabled or died

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- 10 points for veterans
- Additional points for disabled veterans
- Strong state hiring preference

### Healthcare Benefits

**New Mexico State Veterans Home (Truth or Consequences)**
- 127 beds
- Skilled nursing care

## Common Questions

**Q: Does New Mexico tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from New Mexico income tax.

**Q: What national labs are in New Mexico?**
A: Sandia National Laboratories (Albuquerque) and Los Alamos National Laboratory offer many opportunities for veterans.

**Q: What military installations are here?**
A: Kirtland AFB, Holloman AFB, White Sands Missile Range, and Cannon AFB near Clovis.
