# Oklahoma Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

Oklahoma—the Sooner State—offers veterans outstanding benefits with full military retirement tax exemption, sales tax exemptions, property tax exemptions, and excellent tuition waivers. With Tinker AFB, Fort Sill, and a very low cost of living, Oklahoma attracts veterans seeking affordability and strong military community.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 290,000 veterans (9.3% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: Full military retirement tax exemption, sales tax exemption, property tax exemptions, Oklahoma Tuition Aid Grant
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state tax on military retirement pay
  2. Very low cost of living
  3. Major military installations (Tinker, Fort Sill)
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Tornado risk (Tornado Alley)
  2. Hot, humid summers
  3. Lower salaries compared to coasts
- **Best cities for veterans**: Oklahoma City, Tulsa, Lawton, Norman, Broken Arrow

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Full property tax exemption
- Disabled veterans: Partial exemptions
- Surviving spouses: May continue exemptions

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Military retirement pay: 100% exempt from Oklahoma income tax
- VA disability compensation: Not taxed
- SBP/DIC payments: Not taxed
- OK income tax: 0.25% to 4.75%

**Sales Tax Exemption**
- 100% disabled veterans: Sales tax exemption card
- Significant savings on purchases

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates

**Hunting and Fishing Licenses**
- Disabled veterans: Free licenses
- Resident veterans: Discounted licenses

### Education Benefits

**Oklahoma Tuition Aid Grant for Veterans**
- Tuition assistance for veterans
- Oklahoma public colleges

**Dependent Tuition Waivers**
- Children of deceased/disabled veterans: Tuition waiver

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Choice Act compliant

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- 5 points for veterans
- 10 points for disabled veterans
- Strong state preference

### Healthcare Benefits

**Oklahoma Veterans Centers (7 Facilities)**
- Ardmore, Claremore, Clinton, Lawton, Norman, Sulphur, Talihina

## Common Questions

**Q: Does Oklahoma tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from Oklahoma income tax.

**Q: What is Tinker AFB?**
A: Air Force installation in Oklahoma City. Largest single-site employer in state. Maintenance and logistics hub.

**Q: Is the sales tax exemption valuable?**
A: Yes. 100% disabled veterans receive a sales tax exemption card for personal purchases.
