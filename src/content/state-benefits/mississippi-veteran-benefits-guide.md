# Mississippi Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

Mississippi—the Magnolia State—offers veterans excellent benefits combined with one of the lowest costs of living in America. With complete exemption of military retirement pay, property tax exemptions, free tuition for dependents, and Southern hospitality, Mississippi provides veterans an affordable, veteran-friendly environment with strong community support.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 170,000 veterans (7.5% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: Full military retirement tax exemption, homestead exemption, free tuition for dependents, veterans license plate benefits
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state tax on military retirement pay
  2. Lowest cost of living in the nation
  3. Strong military presence (Stennis, Keesler, Columbus AFB)
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Lower average salaries
  2. Limited job market in some areas
  3. Hot, humid summers and hurricane risk on coast
- **Best cities for veterans**: Jackson metro, Gulfport/Biloxi, Hattiesburg, Columbus, Tupelo

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Homestead exemption: $75,000 assessed value exemption (all homeowners)
- Disabled veterans (100%): Additional exemptions available
- Surviving spouses: Continue exemptions

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Military retirement pay: 100% exempt from Mississippi income tax
- VA disability compensation: Not taxed
- SBP/DIC payments: Not taxed
- Mississippi income tax: 4% to 5% (does not apply to military retirement)

**Vehicle Registration Benefits**
- Disabled veterans: Free registration
- POW: Free plates
- Purple Heart: Free plates
- Medal of Honor: Free

**Hunting and Fishing Licenses**
- Disabled veterans (100%): Free lifetime license
- Resident veterans: Discounted licenses

### Education Benefits

**State Educational Assistance**
- Children of 100% disabled/deceased veterans: Free tuition at state schools
- Mississippi National Guard Tuition Assistance

**In-State Tuition Rules**
- Veterans eligible for in-state tuition immediately
- Active duty and families: In-state rates
- Choice Act compliant

**Public University Programs**
- University of Mississippi: Veteran Services
- Mississippi State University: G.V. "Sonny" Montgomery Center
- University of Southern Mississippi: Veteran Services

### Employment Benefits

**Veterans Preference**
- 5 points for veterans
- 10 points for disabled veterans
- Applies to state positions

**State Hiring Programs**
- Mississippi Department of Employment Security veteran services
- WIN Job Centers: Priority of Service

### Healthcare Benefits

**Mississippi State Veterans Homes**
- Jackson: 150 beds
- Collins: 150 beds
- Kosciusko: 150 beds
- Oxford: 150 beds

## Common Questions

**Q: Does Mississippi tax military retirement pay?**
A: No. Military retirement pay is 100% exempt from Mississippi income tax.

**Q: Is Mississippi really that affordable?**
A: Yes. Mississippi consistently has the lowest cost of living in the nation.

**Q: What military installations are in Mississippi?**
A: Keesler AFB (Biloxi), Columbus AFB, Stennis Space Center, and Camp Shelby.

**Q: Is the Gulf Coast hurricane-prone?**
A: Yes. The Mississippi Gulf Coast has hurricane risk June-November.
