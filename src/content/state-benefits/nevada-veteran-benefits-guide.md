# Nevada Veteran Benefits: Complete Guide

Nevada—the Silver State—offers veterans no state income tax on any income including military retirement, property tax exemptions, and year-round sunshine. With Nellis AFB, growing economy beyond gaming, and affordable living outside Las Vegas strip, Nevada attracts veterans seeking tax advantages and warm weather.

## Bottom Line Up Front

- **Total veteran population**: Approximately 210,000 veterans (8.5% of adult population)
- **Key state benefits**: No state income tax, property tax exemptions, veterans preference, Nevada Veterans Services Commission
- **Top 3 reasons veterans move here**:
  1. No state income tax on any income
  2. Warm weather year-round
  3. Strong military presence (Nellis, Creech, Fallon)
- **Top 3 challenges for veterans here**:
  1. Extreme summer heat in Las Vegas
  2. Limited VA facilities outside Vegas/Reno
  3. Higher cost of living than regional average
- **Best cities for veterans**: Las Vegas, Henderson, Reno, Carson City, North Las Vegas

## State Veteran Benefits Overview

### Financial Benefits

**Property Tax Exemptions**
- Disabled veterans: Tax exemption based on disability rating
- 100% disabled: Significant property tax reduction
- Surviving spouses: Continue exemptions
- Veterans tax exemption: Basic exemption for all veterans

**Income Tax Benefits**
- Nevada has NO state income tax
- Military retirement: Not taxed
- VA disability: Not taxed
- All other income: Not taxed

**Vehicle Registration**
- Disabled veterans: Reduced or free registration
- POW, Purple Heart, Medal of Honor: Free plates

### Education Benefits

**In-State Tuition**
- Veterans: Immediate in-state tuition
- Choice Act compliant

**Public University Programs**
- UNLV: Military and Veteran Services Center
- UNR: Veterans Services

### Healthcare Benefits

**Nevada State Veterans Home (Boulder City)**
- 180 beds
- Skilled nursing care
