[INTRO]
Captain Ryan O'Connor, Field Artillery Officer with math/physics background, transitioned to Data Scientist earning $135K at tech company. Military fire direction experience and analytical skills translated to data analytics career.

[MAIN CONTENT]
**Military:** 13A Field Artillery Officer, fire direction center operations, ballistic calculations, predictive modeling for fire missions.
**Education:** BS Math (pre-commissioning), completed online Master's Data Science during final 18 months service (using TA).
**Transition:** Built portfolio of data projects, learned Python/R/SQL, applied to 80+ positions, hired as Jr. Data Scientist ($95K), promoted to Data Scientist ($135K) after 2 years.
**Keys:** Math background accelerated learning, military analytical experience demonstrated problem-solving, portfolio projects proved technical skills, persistence through job search.

[CONCLUSION]
Ryan transitioned from Artillery Officer to $135K Data Scientist through online master's + portfolio building. Military analytical experience + technical education = high-paying data career.
