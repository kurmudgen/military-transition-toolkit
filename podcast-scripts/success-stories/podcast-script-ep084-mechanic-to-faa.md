[INTRO]
Tech Sergeant Tom Rodriguez, Air Force aircraft mechanic with 14 years experience, transitioned to FAA Aviation Safety Inspector earning $110K. Military maintenance experience and certifications made him ideal regulatory career candidate.

[MAIN CONTENT]
**Military:** 2A3X3 Tactical Aircraft Maintenance, F-16 crew chief, A&P license, QA inspector experience.
**Transition:** Targeted FAA inspector roles requiring aviation maintenance background. Hired as ASI-Maintenance within 5 months of separation.
**Current:** Conducts airworthiness inspections, certificate management, accident investigations. GS-13 level ($110K). Career path to GS-14/15 ($130K-150K).
**Keys:** A&P license critical, QA experience directly applicable, military maintenance records valued by FAA.

[CONCLUSION]
Tom transitioned from AF mechanic to $110K FAA inspector. Military aviation experience perfect pathway to federal aviation regulatory careers.
