[INTRO]
Sergeant First Class Carlos Rivera, Military Police with 14 years law enforcement and force protection experience, transitioned to Corporate Security Director for Fortune 500 company earning $140K managing enterprise security operations.

[MAIN CONTENT]
**Military:** 31B Military Police, installation law enforcement, force protection, physical security, deployed to high-threat environments, managed security operations.
**Transition:** Targeted corporate security roles at large companies. Hired as Security Manager ($95K), promoted to Director ($140K) after 3 years.
**Current:** Manages physical security for corporate campus (5,000 employees), oversees security staff, emergency management, executive protection, access control systems.
**Keys:** Military law enforcement background valued, force protection experience applicable to corporate threat assessment, leadership experience qualified for management roles.

[CONCLUSION]
Carlos transitioned from Military Police to $140K Corporate Security Director in 4 years. MP experience ideal for corporate physical security management careers.
