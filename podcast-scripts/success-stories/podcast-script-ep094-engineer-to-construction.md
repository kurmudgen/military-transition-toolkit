[INTRO]
Sergeant First Class James Martinez, Combat Engineer with horizontal and vertical construction experience, transitioned to Construction Project Manager earning $120K at major commercial contractor within 3 years.

[MAIN CONTENT]
**Military:** 12N Horizontal Construction Engineer, built roads, airfields, infrastructure in combat zones, managed construction crews, heavy equipment operations, project planning.
**Transition:** Used SkillBridge for 6-month construction management internship. Hired as Assistant Project Manager ($70K), promoted to Project Manager ($120K) after 2 years.
**Current:** Manages commercial construction projects ($5M-20M), coordinates subcontractors, scheduling, budget management, client relations.
**Keys:** Military construction experience directly applicable, project management skills valued, leadership of teams transferred to construction crews, SkillBridge internship provided industry connections.

[CONCLUSION]
James transitioned from Combat Engineer to $120K Construction Manager in 3 years. Military construction experience perfect path to commercial construction management careers.
