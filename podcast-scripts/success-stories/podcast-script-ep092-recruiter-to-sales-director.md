[INTRO]
Staff Sergeant Mike Johnson, Army Recruiter consistently exceeding mission goals, transitioned to corporate sales, progressed to Sales Director earning $175K at medical device company within 6 years.

[MAIN CONTENT]
**Military:** Recruiter for 5 years, exceeded quotas 95% of months, built pipeline systems, cold-calling expert, relationship management.
**Transition:** Targeted B2B sales roles. Hired as Account Executive at SaaS company ($75K base + commission = $110K). Promoted to Senior AE ($140K), then Sales Director ($175K+ equity).
**Career Path:** AE → Senior AE → Team Lead → Sales Director. Consistent top performer enabled rapid promotion.
**Keys:** Recruiting = pure sales experience, quota achievement record demonstrated results, pipeline management directly applicable, rejection tolerance from military recruiting.

[CONCLUSION]
Mike transitioned from Army Recruiter to $175K Sales Director in 6 years. Military recruiting experience is perfect foundation for high-earning corporate sales careers.
