[INTRO]
Sergeant First Class Maria Sanchez, Army Supply Sergeant managing $80M in equipment and supplies, transitioned to Fortune 500 Supply Chain Manager earning $115K at Target Corporation within 4 months of separation.

[MAIN CONTENT]
**Military:** 92A Automated Logistical Specialist, 10 years, battalion S-4 shop NCOIC, managed supply operations for 800-person battalion, deployed twice.
**Transition:** Targeted retail and manufacturing supply chain roles. Used LinkedIn to connect with corporate recruiters. Hired by Target through veteran recruiting program.
**Current:** Supply Chain Manager for Target distribution center, oversees inventory management, vendor relationships, logistics coordination for 3-state region.
**Keys:** Military supply experience directly applicable, budget management skills valued, leadership of teams translated to corporate supervisory role.

[CONCLUSION]
Maria transitioned from Supply Sergeant to $115K Supply Chain Manager in 4 months. Military logistics experience perfect fit for corporate supply chain management roles.
