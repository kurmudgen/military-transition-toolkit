[INTRO]
**Cold Open:** 3531 - motor vehicle operator. You've driven everything from 7-tons to LVSRs. Commercial trucking needs you - OTR at sixty-five thousand, specialized at eighty thousand, logistics management at ninety thousand plus. Path identical to Army 88M (Episode 9).

[MAIN CONTENT]

**Your 3531 Experience:** Operated heavy vehicles, convoy operations, vehicle maintenance, load planning, safety protocols. Professional driving experience translates directly to CDL careers.

**Career Paths (mirrors Episode 9):**

**OTR Trucking:** $55K-$80K+. Long-haul, weeks away. Pay per mile ($0.45-$0.65/mile typical).

**Regional/Local:** $50K-$70K. Home more frequently. Better work-life balance.

**Specialized (Hazmat/Tanker):** $70K-$90K+. Additional endorsements required. Higher pay for specialized loads.

**LTL Linehaul:** Old Dominion, FedEx Freight, XPO $70K-$100K+. Union jobs, excellent benefits.

**Logistics Coordinator:** $50K-$70K. Managing freight operations from office.

**Warehouse Manager:** $60K-$85K. Managing distribution operations.

**Military CDL Waiver:** Many states let Marines skip skills test, take written only. Saves $5,000 and weeks. Check your state DMV for "military CDL waiver" or "troops to truckers."

**Process:** Get military driving records (DA 348 equivalent for Marines), unit certification letter, pass written tests, receive CDL. Cost $50-$100 vs $5,000 truck school.

**Companies Hiring Marines:** Schneider National, J.B. Hunt, Werner, Roehl (all have Marine/veteran programs). Marine networks help - many trucking company managers are former Marines.

**Timeline:** 12mo research state CDL waiver, 6-9mo get CDL using waiver, 3-6mo apply trucking companies, separation start training/driving.

**Unique Marine Angle:** Marine discipline reputation particularly strong in trucking industry. Safety-focused companies value Marines. Convoy experience directly applicable.

**Beyond Driving:** Same logistics coordinator, warehouse manager, fleet manager paths as 88Ms. Your Marine leadership experience accelerates management track.

Resources at militarytransitiontoolkit.com (Episode 9 has detailed CDL guide). Next: Marine 6046 aviation maintenance.
