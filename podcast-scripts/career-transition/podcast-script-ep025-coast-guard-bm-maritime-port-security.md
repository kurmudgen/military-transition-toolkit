[INTRO]
**Cold Open:** Boatswain's Mate. Vessel operations, maritime law enforcement, search and rescue, port security. Maritime industry needs you - port security at sixty thousand, maritime operations at seventy-five thousand, port management at ninety thousand plus.

[MAIN CONTENT]

**Your BM Skills:** Boat operations, navigation, maritime law enforcement, port security, search and rescue, deck operations, vessel maintenance. Unique CG operational experience.

**Career Paths:**

**Port Security Officer:** Securing ports, vessels, facilities. $55K-$75K. TSA, port authorities, private security companies. TWIC required (easy for vets).

**Maritime Security Specialist:** Vessel security, cargo security, piracy prevention (commercial vessels). $65K-$90K.

**Harbor Pilot/Mate:** Navigating vessels in/out harbors (requires advancement, licensing). $80K-$150K+. Long path but high pay.

**Port Operations Coordinator:** Managing vessel traffic, cargo operations. $60K-$80K.

**Port Manager:** Overseeing port facilities operations. $80K-$120K. Requires experience plus management skills.

**Tug Boat Operator:** Commercial towing operations. $50K-$85K. Requires merchant mariner credentials.

**Yacht Captain/Crew:** Private/charter yachts. $45K-$100K+ depending on vessel size. Your CG boat handling valued.

**Companies/Agencies:** Port authorities (major ports), TSA Maritime, private security (G4S, Securitas maritime divisions), shipping companies (Maersk, MSC), towing companies, yacht management companies.

**Required Credentials:** TWIC (Transportation Worker Identification Credential) - required port access. $125, easy for CG vets. Merchant Mariner Credential (MMC) for commercial vessel operations - CG sea time often counts toward requirements.

**CG Advantage:** Maritime expertise civilians lack. CG law enforcement background. Port operations knowledge. Your experience unique and valued in maritime industry.

**Timeline:** 12mo research maritime industry target location (coastal cities obviously), obtain TWIC/MMC if needed, 6-9mo apply maritime security and operations positions, 3-6mo interview emphasizing CG maritime experience.

**Location Dependent:** Maritime jobs concentrated coastal cities - major ports (LA/Long Beach, NY/NJ, Houston, Seattle, Norfolk, Miami, Savannah). Relocate to coast if not already there.

Resources at militarytransitiontoolkit.com. Next: Coast Guard ME to marine engineering/HVAC.
