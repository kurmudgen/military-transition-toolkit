## 1. E-Commerce Business Owner (Amazon FBA, Etsy) ($20K-$200K+)

**Portable: 100% | Startup Cost: $500-$5,000 | Scalable**

**Income Potential:**
- Part-time: $1,000-$3,000/month
- Full-time: $3,000-$10,000/month
- Successful sellers: $10,000-$50,000+/month

**Business Models:**
- **Amazon FBA** (Fulfillment by Amazon): Source products, Amazon handles storage/shipping
- **Etsy**: Sell handmade, vintage, or craft supplies
- **Shopify**: Own e-commerce store
- **Print-on-Demand**: Merch by Amazon, Printful, Redbubble (no inventory)

**Getting Started - Amazon FBA:**
1. Research profitable product niche ($0)
2. Source products from suppliers (Alibaba, domestic wholesalers) ($500-$3,000 initial inventory)
3. Create Amazon seller account ($39.99/month)
4. Ship inventory to Amazon warehouse
5. Optimize listings, run ads, scale

**Getting Started - Etsy:**
1. Create Etsy shop ($0 to open, $0.20/listing)
2. Create or source products
3. Photograph and list items
4. Market via Pinterest, Instagram
5. Fulfill orders

**Military Spouse Advantage:** Business runs from anywhere, no physical location needed

## 2. Online Course Creator ($10K-$300K+)

**Portable: 100% | Startup Cost: $100-$2,000 | Scalable Passive Income**

**Income Potential:**
- Beginner: $500-$2,000/month
- Established: $5,000-$20,000/month
- Successful course creators: $20,000-$100,000+/month

**What to Teach:**
- Professional skills (project management, marketing, design)
- Hobbies and crafts
- Test prep, academic subjects
- Business skills (freelancing, entrepreneurship)
- Military spouse-specific topics (PCS organization, deployment coping, portable careers)

**Platforms:**
- **Teachable, Thinkific**: Host your own courses ($39-$99/month)
- **Udemy**: Marketplace with built-in audience (Udemy takes 50% revenue share)
- **Skillshare**: Subscription model, paid per minute watched
- **Kajabi**: All-in-one platform ($149-$399/month)

**Getting Started:**
1. Validate idea: Survey audience, test interest
2. Create course outline and film lessons (smartphone okay)
3. Edit videos (basic editing sufficient)
4. Upload to platform
5. Market via social media, email list, ads
6. Price: $47-$497+ depending on value

**Best for:** Military spouses with expertise to share

**Starting a Business:**

- **Bunker Labs**: Military entrepreneur support
- **SCORE**: Free small business mentoring

**Tax Benefits:**
- Home office deduction
- Equipment and supply deductions
- Business mileage
- Health insurance deduction (if self-employed)
