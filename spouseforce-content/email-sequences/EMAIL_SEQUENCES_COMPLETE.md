# Email Sequence Best Practices

## Subject Line Formulas

**Curiosity:**
- "The job board nobody talks about (but should)"
- "I made this mistake so you don't have to..."
- "Your spouse's rank might qualify you for $4,000"

**Benefit-Driven:**
- "Land 5 interviews in 2 weeks with this resume template"
- "Work remotely from ANY duty station (here's how)"
- "Turn PCS gaps into resume strengths"

**Personal:**
- "[First Name], your portable career starts here"
- "This changed everything for me..."
- "Your question answered inside"

## Email Structure Template

**Opening (Hook):**
- Relatable problem or question
- "Quick question: Have you ever..."
- "Meet [Name] who..."

**Value (Body):**
- 3-5 actionable tips
- Story or example
- Clear, skimmable formatting

**Call-to-Action:**
- ONE clear next step
- "[BUTTON: Download Free Guide]"
- "Reply and tell me..."
- "Join our free community"

**Closing (Personal):**
- Encouraging sign-off
- "You've got this"
- "Cheering for you"
- Signature

**P.S.:**
- Bonus tip or teaser for next email
- Additional CTA
- Personal touch

# Automation & Timing Strategy

**Welcome Series:**
- Trigger: New subscriber
- Frequency: Days 1, 3, 5, 7, 10, 14, 21
- Goal: Onboard, deliver value, build trust

**Specialized Series (MyCAA, Remote Work, etc.):**
- Trigger: Tag-based (subscriber interest)
- Frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly
- Goal: Deep-dive education, conversion

**Re-Engagement:**
- Trigger: 60 days inactive (no opens)
- Frequency: Days 1, 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 35
- Goal: Win back or clean list

**Weekly Broadcast:**
- Timing: Every Tuesday 9am
- Content: Career tips, job leads, resources
- Goal: Ongoing engagement
