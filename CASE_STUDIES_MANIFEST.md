# Veteran Transition Case Studies - Complete Manifest

## Overview

This document catalogs 100 veteran transition success story case studies designed to provide tactical lessons, timelines, and inspiration for transitioning service members.

**Status:** 8 detailed case studies completed; 92 outlined with titles and core concepts

**Format:** Each case study follows standardized format (2,000-3,000 words):
- Quick Facts
- Background Story
- The Strategy (broken into phases)
- The Results (with detailed financial impact)
- Key Tactics That Worked
- Obstacles Overcome
- What They'd Do Differently
- Timeline Breakdown

- Current Situation
- Key Takeaways
- Resources Mentioned

## Writing Standards Applied

Each case study follows these requirements:

**Length:** 2,000-3,000 words
**Format:** Standardized sections with consistent structure
**Voice:** Story-driven, conversational, tactical (not motivational)
**Research:** 5-8 web searches minimum per story for realistic salary/timeline data
**Numbers:** Specific salaries, timelines, costs, savings (not vague)
**Authenticity:** Includes mistakes, setbacks, and realistic challenges
**Actionability:** Clear tactical steps readers can replicate
