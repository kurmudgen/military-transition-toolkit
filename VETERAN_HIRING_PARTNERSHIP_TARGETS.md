# Corporate Veteran Hiring Program Research
## Partnership Target List for Employer Integrations

**Research Date:** November 17, 2025
**Purpose:** Identify corporate partners for integration with Military Transition Toolkit

---

## Executive Summary

This document provides comprehensive research on companies with veteran hiring initiatives, their commitments, resources, and partnership opportunities. The research identifies **315+ companies** actively hiring veterans through various coalitions and programs, representing opportunities for strategic partnerships.

### Key Findings:
- **Veteran Jobs Mission Coalition:** 315+ companies, 1M+ veterans hired since 2011, goal of 2M veterans + 200K military spouses by 2032
- **DoD SkillBridge:** Hundreds of corporate partners offering transition training programs
- **American Corporate Partners:** 90+ Fortune 500 companies providing mentorship
- **Hiring Our Heroes:** 150+ companies across 12 fellowship sites nationwide

## Contact Strategy & Outreach

### Primary Contact Channels

1. **Veteran Hiring Managers / Military Recruiting Teams**
   - Most companies have dedicated veteran recruitment teams
   - LinkedIn search: "[Company] veteran recruitment" or "military hiring"
   - Company career sites typically have military/veteran sections

2. **Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Teams**
   - Often manage veteran hiring initiatives
   - Partnership proposals align with CSR goals

3. **Talent Acquisition Leadership**
   - VP/Director of Talent Acquisition
   - Head of Diversity & Inclusion

4. **Military/Veteran Employee Resource Groups (ERGs)**
   - Internal veteran networks at companies
   - Can champion internal partnerships

### Sample Outreach Email Template

```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity: Military Transition Toolkit Integration

Dear [Veteran Hiring Manager/Military Recruitment Lead],

I'm reaching out regarding [Company]'s commitment to hiring and supporting veterans,
particularly your [specific program: SkillBridge participation / Veteran Jobs Mission
membership / Corporate Fellowship program].

The Military Transition Toolkit is a digital platform designed to help service members
transition to civilian careers. We provide:
- Career planning tools
- Education benefit guidance
- Healthcare transition resources
- Transition timeline planning

We're seeking to integrate employer partners who share our commitment to veteran success.
Partnership benefits include:
- Direct access to transitioning service members
- Integration with pre-transition planning tools
- Showcase your veteran programs and opportunities
- Alignment with your [hiring commitment / SkillBridge / fellowship] initiatives

Given [Company]'s [specific achievement: 14,000+ veterans hired / 400 fellows hosted /
15% veteran workforce], we believe there's strong alignment for collaboration.

Would you be available for a brief call to explore partnership opportunities?

Best regards,
[Name]
```
