## Key Principles

### 1. Execute > Perfect
- Done is better than perfect
- Ship fast, iterate based on data
- Don't let perfectionism delay launch

### 2. Data-Driven Decisions
- Measure everything from Day 1
- Review metrics weekly
- Double down on what works, cut what doesn't

### 3. Value First, Revenue Second
- Help veterans transition successfully
- Revenue follows value
- Build trust before asking for payment

### 4. Sustainable Pace
- 20-25 hours/week max (solo founder reality)
- Automate and outsource to avoid burnout
- Marathon, not sprint

### 5. Ruthless Prioritization
- Focus on 20% of tasks that drive 80% of results
- Say no to everything else
- Time and money are scarce, use wisely
