# Premium Training Courses for Military Transition Toolkit

## Overview

This directory contains **4 comprehensive premium training courses** designed to help E5-E9 service members successfully transition from military to civilian life. These courses are intended for sale as standalone products or as part of a premium membership bundle.

## Course Catalog

### Course 1: Military Resume Mastery
**Price:** $97
**Duration:** 10 lessons (2-3 hours total)
**Target Audience:** E5-E9 translating military experience to civilian resumes

Transform your military experience into a compelling civilian resume that gets past ATS systems and lands interviews.

**Key Learning Outcomes:**
- Translate military jargon into civilian language
- Optimize resumes for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)
- Quantify accomplishments with metrics and impact
- Create targeted resumes for different industries
- Master LinkedIn and cover letter strategies

---

### Course 2: VA Claims Success System
**Price:** $147
**Duration:** 12 lessons (3-4 hours total)
**Target Audience:** E5-E9 maximizing VA disability ratings

Navigate the VA disability claims process with confidence and maximize your rating.

**Key Learning Outcomes:**
- Understand VA disability rating system and calculations
- Gather compelling medical evidence and documentation
- Prepare for C&P exams strategically
- File effective secondary and increase claims
- Navigate the appeals process when needed
- Create a path to 100% disability rating

---

### Course 3: State Benefits Navigator
**Price:** $197
**Duration:** 60 lessons (8-10 hours total)
**Target Audience:** E5-E9 choosing retirement location strategically

Make informed decisions about where to retire based on comprehensive state-by-state benefits analysis.

**Key Learning Outcomes:**
- Compare tax treatment of military retirement and VA disability across all 50 states
- Understand property tax exemptions and homestead benefits
- Evaluate education benefits for dependents
- Assess healthcare access and VA facilities by state
- Calculate total cost of living vs benefits package
- Choose the best retirement location for your situation

---

### Course 4: Second Career Success Blueprint
**Price:** $247
**Duration:** 15 lessons (4-5 hours total)
**Target Audience:** E5-E9 planning second career after retirement

Launch a successful second career with strategic planning, networking, and interview preparation.

**Key Learning Outcomes:**
- Shift from military to corporate culture mindset
- Conduct effective job search and networking
- Leverage SkillBridge and transition programs
- Master behavioral interviews and salary negotiation
- Navigate first 90 days in civilian workplace
- Build long-term career growth strategy
