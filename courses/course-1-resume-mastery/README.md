## Course Description

Transform your military experience into a compelling civilian resume that bypasses Applicant Tracking Systems and lands you interviews with top employers. This comprehensive course teaches you to translate military jargon, quantify your accomplishments, and create targeted resumes for different industries.

### What You'll Learn

- Eliminate military jargon and speak the civilian hiring manager's language
- Optimize your resume to pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)
- Quantify your military accomplishments with metrics and impact
- Create multiple targeted resume versions for different industries
- Master LinkedIn profile optimization and cover letter writing
- Address employment gaps and medical issues strategically
- Negotiate salary effectively using your military background

### Who This Course Is For

- **E5-E6:** Team leaders and supervisors ready to translate tactical experience
- **E7-E9:** Senior leaders with significant management and strategic responsibilities
- **All branches:** Army, Navy, Marines, Air Force, Coast Guard, Space Force
- **Active duty, Guard, Reserve:** Transitioning or preparing to transition
- **Recently separated:** Struggling to get interviews with current resume
