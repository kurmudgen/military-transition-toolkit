# SkillBridge Companies List 2025

This comprehensive list includes 50 companies currently offering Department of Defense (DoD) SkillBridge internships for transitioning service members. The SkillBridge program allows active-duty service members to gain civilian work experience during the last 180 days of service while still receiving military pay and benefits.

## How to Apply for SkillBridge Programs

1. **Check Eligibility:** You must be within 180 days of separation or retirement from active duty
2. **Get Command Approval:** Obtain approval from your command before applying
3. **Research Companies:** Visit the official DoD SkillBridge website at **skillbridge.osd.mil** for the complete list
4. **Contact Companies:** Reach out to company coordinators directly using contact information above
5. **Submit Application:** Follow each company's specific application process
6. **Coordinate Timeline:** Ensure the program dates align with your separation timeline

## Important Notes

- All SkillBridge participants continue to receive military pay, benefits, and housing allowances during the internship
- Memoranda of Understanding (MOUs) are automatically extended through June 30, 2025
- New industry provider applications are processed during Fall Application Enrollment Period: October 1 - December 1
- Contact information and program details may change; always verify current information on the official DoD SkillBridge website
- This list represents a sampling of major SkillBridge partners; over 6,344 partner organizations offer more than 9,224 position announcements as of Q2 2025
