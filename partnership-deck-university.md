## Key Content (Slide Summaries)

### Slide 1: The Student Veteran Challenge

**Key Statistics:**
- 6% of undergrads, 7% of grad students are veterans (669,922 using VA education benefits)
- 37% of part-time and 16% of full-time veteran students drop out within 9 months
- 60% of student veterans report concerns about balancing school and other responsibilities
- Veterans face difficulty with admin of education benefits, academic engagement, social relationships

### Slide 2: Our Solution

**Military Transition Toolkit for Student Veterans:**
- Career planning tools connecting military skills to post-graduation careers
- GI Bill optimization and education benefits guidance
- Academic-to-career pathway planning
- State veteran benefits for students
- Resume building and internship preparation
- Ongoing career development through graduation and beyond

**Value Proposition:** Improve student veteran retention, graduation rates, and post-graduation career outcomes while differentiating your veterans services.
