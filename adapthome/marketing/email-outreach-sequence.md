## **Email Sequence Best Practices**

**Timing:**
- Send emails Tuesday-Thursday between 8-10 AM PST (best open rates)
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode)
- Space emails 3-4 days apart (not too aggressive)

**Personalization:**
- Use contractor's name
- Reference their company name
- Mention their location/county
- Reference their specialization (if known)

**Tracking:**
- Track opens, clicks, and replies
- A/B test subject lines
- Monitor conversion rates by email
- Optimize based on data

**Follow-Up:**
- Respond to replies within 1-2 hours
- Have a live person ready for phone calls
- Schedule demos/calls immediately
- Streamline signup process
