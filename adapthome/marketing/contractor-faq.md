### **1. How does AdaptHome verify that veterans have VA grants?**

**Answer:**

AdaptHome uses a multi-step verification process to ensure all veterans have legitimate VA grant approvals:

- **Grant Approval Documentation**: Veterans upload their official VA grant approval letter when registering
- **VA Database Cross-Reference**: We verify grant details against VA records (with veteran permission)
- **Expiration Date Tracking**: Grants must be active and not expired
- **Funding Confirmation**: We confirm the grant amount and remaining balance
- **Contact Verification**: We verify veteran contact information and VA facility assignment

**You will never receive a lead for a veteran who hasn't been verified through this process.**

Additionally, each lead includes:
- Copy of VA grant approval letter (with sensitive info redacted)
- Grant type (SAH, SHA, or HISA)
- Grant amount and remaining balance
- Approval date and expiration date
- Assigned VA facility

### **2. What is the average project size from these leads?**

**Answer:**

Project sizes vary by grant type:

**HISA Grants (Home Improvements & Structural Alterations)**
- **Average Grant Amount**: $6,500

- **Average Grant Amount**: $19,500

**SAH Grants (Special Adaptive Housing)**
- **Average Grant Amount**: $95,000
- **Average Project Cost**: $85,000-$105,000
- **Common Projects**: Complete home renovations, new accessible construction, elevator installation, multi-room modifications

**Additional Revenue Opportunities:**

- **Average out-of-pocket spending**: $5,000-$15,000 for upgrades (luxury fixtures, expanded scope, etc.)
- **Repeat business**: Veterans may receive grant adjustments or additional grants for new needs

### **7. Do I get a refund if the project doesn't start?**

**Answer:**

**Refunds are rare, but available in specific circumstances.**

**Refund Eligible (100% refund):**
- **Invalid lead**: Veteran doesn't have an active VA grant (our verification failed)
- **Duplicate lead**: You already received this veteran through another source
- **Incorrect information**: Lead details were substantially misrepresented
- **Unreachable contact**: All provided contact info is invalid (phone, email bounce, wrong address)

**Partial Refund Eligible (50% refund):**
- **Veteran already hired contractor**: They chose someone before receiving your lead
- **Project canceled by VA**: VA rescinds grant approval (rare but happens)
- **Veteran moved out of area**: No longer in your service area

**Not Refund Eligible:**
- Veteran chose another contractor after receiving your proposal
- Veteran decided to postpone the project
- Veteran didn't respond to your initial contact (if contact info was valid)
- You couldn't meet veteran's timeline or budget
- Veteran found another funding source

**Refund Process:**
1. Submit refund request within **7 days** of receiving lead
2. Provide documentation (call logs, emails, screenshots)
3. AdaptHome investigates (2-3 business days)
4. Refund issued to your payment method (if approved)

**Our Goal**: Less than 5% refund rate (high lead quality). Current refund rate: 3.2%.
