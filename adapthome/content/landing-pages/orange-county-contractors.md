## **Frequently Asked Questions**

### **Are Orange County contractors more expensive?**

**Generally, yes.** Orange County has higher labor and material costs than other Southern California counties:
- Labor rates: $75-$125/hour (vs. $50-$90 in Riverside or San Bernardino)
- Materials: 10-15% higher
- Permits: $500-$2,000 more

**But:** Orange County contractors often deliver higher-end work with premium finishes.

---

### **Can I use my VA grant for luxury upgrades?**

**Partially.** Your VA grant covers the **accessibility features** (roll-in shower, grab bars, lowered counters). You can add luxury finishes with personal funds:
- Use VA grant for shower conversion ($8,000)
- Upgrade to custom tile and fixtures (+$5,000 out-of-pocket)

Many Orange County veterans do this to match their home's style.

---

### **Do I need HOA approval for my VA project?**

**If you live in an HOA community, yes.** Most Orange County HOAs require:
- Architectural review application
- Project plans and specifications
- Approval before starting work

**AdaptHome contractors can help** prepare HOA applications and obtain approval.
