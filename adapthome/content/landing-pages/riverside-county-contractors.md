## **Unique Riverside County Considerations**

### **Desert Climate (Coachella Valley)**

Contractors in Palm Springs, Palm Desert, and surrounding desert cities have special expertise:

**Extreme Heat Challenges:**
- Work schedules (start early, finish by noon during summer)
- Material selection (heat-resistant, UV-resistant)
- HVAC considerations (cooling accessible spaces)
- Outdoor modifications (shaded ramps, covered entries)

**Desert-Specific Features:**
- Pool accessibility (very common in desert)
- Outdoor living space modifications
- Dust control during construction
- Water-efficient landscaping (accessible desert gardens)

### **Mountain Communities (Idyllwild, etc.)**

Higher elevation communities have different needs:
- Snow load requirements for ramps
- Heating considerations (accessible radiant heat)
- Limited contractor availability (may need to bring contractors from lower elevations)
- Longer timelines (weather-dependent)
