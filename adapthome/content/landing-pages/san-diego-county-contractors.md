## **Average VA Grant Project Costs in San Diego County**

San Diego has higher construction costs than many areas due to:
- Higher labor costs
- Strict permitting and code requirements
- Coastal zone regulations (for coastal properties)
- Higher cost of living

### **Typical Project Costs:**

**HISA Projects ($6,976 max)**
- Basic bathroom safety (grab bars, shower seat): $2,500-$4,000
- Exterior ramp installation: $3,500-$6,500
- Entry modifications + bathroom safety: $5,000-$6,976

**SHA Projects ($20,929 max)**
- Accessible bathroom remodel: $15,000-$22,000
- Kitchen accessibility upgrades: $18,000-$25,000
- Bathroom + ramp combo: $18,000-$20,929

**SAH Projects ($104,648 max)**
- Complete bathroom & kitchen remodel: $45,000-$65,000
- Elevator or lift installation: $25,000-$50,000
- Full home renovation: $85,000-$104,648
- New accessible ADU (backyard): $90,000-$104,648

**Note:** Costs are 10-15% higher in coastal areas (La Jolla, Del Mar, Coronado) and lower in East County and South Bay.

### **What Makes San Diego Contractors Different:**

**Coastal Regulations:**
If your home is in the coastal zone (Pacific Beach, La Jolla, Del Mar, Encinitas, etc.), expect:
- Additional coastal commission review
- Longer permit timelines (add 4-8 weeks)
- Higher costs (15-20% premium)

**Seismic Requirements:**
California has strict seismic codes, especially for:
- Elevators and lifts
- Structural modifications
- Ramp attachment to home

San Diego contractors are experienced with these requirements.
