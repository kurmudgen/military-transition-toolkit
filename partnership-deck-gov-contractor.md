# Government Contractor Partnership Deck
## VEVRAA Compliance + Veteran Retention Solution

## Presentation Overview

**Audience:** OFCCP Compliance Officers, CHRO, VP Talent Acquisition, Federal Contracts Leadership
**Duration:** 20-25 minutes
**Delivery Format:** In-person/Virtual
**Objective:** Position as solution for VEVRAA compliance and veteran retention
**Expected Outcome:** Partnership for veteran employee benefit program

---

## Key Content

### Slide 1: VEVRAA Compliance Challenge

**The Requirements (2024):**
- 5.2% veteran hiring benchmark for federal contractors
- Written affirmative action programs (AAPs) required (50+ employees)
- Good-faith recruiting and retention efforts
- Detailed record-keeping for 3+ years
- OFCCP audit readiness

**The Challenge:**
- Hiring veterans meets one requirement
- Retention demonstrates sustained good-faith effort
- 43% first-year veteran turnover hurts compliance posture
- Need documented veteran support programs

---

### Slide 2: Our Solution

**Dual-Purpose Platform:**

**1. VEVRAA Compliance Enhancement**
- Documented veteran support program
- Evidence of good-faith retention efforts
- Data tracking for OFCCP audits
- Continuous improvement demonstration

**2. Veteran Retention Driver**
- Career development for veteran employees
- Skills translation and advancement support
- Reduce 43% first-year turnover
- Improve ROI on veteran recruiting investment

**Value Proposition:** Meet compliance requirements while genuinely improving veteran employee outcomes.

---

### Slide 3: Benefits

**Compliance Benefits:**
- Strengthen AFCCP audit defense
- Document veteran support beyond minimum requirements
- Track veteran employee engagement
- Demonstrate continuous improvement
- Reduce compliance risk

**Business Benefits:**
- Reduce veteran turnover (43% to <25%)
- Save recruitment and replacement costs
- Better utilize veteran talent
- Enhance reputation with OFCCP
- Competitive advantage for contract awards

**ROI:** Platform cost significantly less than turnover costs and compliance risk mitigation.
