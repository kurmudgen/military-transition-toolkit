## IT & CYBERSECURITY CERTIFICATIONS

### 1. CompTIA Certifications (A+, Network+, Security+, etc.)

#### **Certification Body:** CompTIA
- **Website:** https://www.comptia.org
- **Certifications:** A+, Network+, Security+, CySA+, PenTest+, Cloud+, Linux+, Server+

#### **Partner Program:** CompTIA Authorized Partner Program (CAPP)
- **Program Type:** Training Delivery Partner (not traditional affiliate)
- **Target Partners:** Schools, training organizations, nonprofits
- **Benefits:**
  - Discounts on exam vouchers
  - Complimentary instructor exam vouchers
  - Discounted courseware
  - Marketing development fund
- **Commission:** ❓ Not traditional affiliate - B2B partner model
- **How to Apply:** https://partners.comptia.org
- **Contact:** Partners portal

#### **Alternative Affiliate Route:** Pearson IT Certification
- **Website:** https://www.pearsonitcertification.com/affiliates
- **What They Sell:** CompTIA training books and materials
- **Affiliate Program:** ✅ YES
- **Commission:** Available through affiliate signup
- **Recommendation:** Partner with training material publishers rather than CompTIA directly

#### **Military Benefits:**
- Free training through FedVTE (Federal Virtual Training Environment)
- Army COOL/Navy COOL funding available
- SkillBridge opportunities

#### **Pricing:**
- Exam vouchers: ~$250-350 per certification
- Training materials: $50-200
- Boot camps: $2,000-4,000

#### **Pass Rates:** Varies by certification (60-75% typical)
#### **Time to Complete:** 1-3 months per certification
#### **Recommendation:** MEDIUM PRIORITY - Indirect affiliate through training providers

## PROJECT MANAGEMENT CERTIFICATIONS

### 5. PMI Certifications (PMP, CAPM)

#### **Certification Body:** Project Management Institute (PMI)
- **Website:** https://www.pmi.org
- **Certifications:**
  - PMP (Project Management Professional)
  - CAPM (Certified Associate in Project Management)
  - PMI-ACP (Agile Certified Practitioner)
  - PgMP (Program Management Professional)

#### **Partner Program:** Authorized Training Partner (ATP) Program
- **Program Type:** ✅ YES - Multi-tier partner program
- **Target Partners:** Training organizations (not individual affiliates)
- **Tiers:** Multiple ATP tiers with increasing benefits

#### **Benefits:**
- Access to PMI global brand
- Directory listings
- Marketing support and ad placements
- PMI-developed course materials (instructor kits, student materials, digital assets)
- Higher tiers: Reseller rights for PMI products
- Expanded student capacity
- Exclusive support

#### **How to Apply:** https://www.pmi.org/business-solutions/become-an-authorized-training-partner

#### **Example ATP:** Project Management Training Institute (PMTI)
- **Website:** https://www.4pmti.com
- **Status:** Premier PMI Authorized Training Partner
- **Track Record:** 80,000+ PMP certification students
- **Affiliate for PMTI:** ❓ UNKNOWN - Contact directly

#### **Pricing:**
- PMP Exam: $405 (PMI member) / $555 (non-member)
- CAPM Exam: $225 (member) / $300 (non-member)
- Training courses: $1,500-3,000
- Study materials: $100-300

#### **Time to Complete:**
- CAPM: 1-2 months
- PMP: 2-4 months

#### **Military Benefits:**
- GI Bill eligible through many providers
- Military experience counts toward requirements
- Some free training through military programs

#### **Recommendation:** HIGH PRIORITY - Become ATP or partner with existing ATPs for referrals

## BUSINESS & QUALITY CERTIFICATIONS

### 8. Six Sigma Certifications (Multiple Bodies)

#### **Certifying Bodies:**

**The Council for Six Sigma Certification (CSSC)**
- **Website:** https://www.sixsigmacouncil.org
- **Affiliate Program:** ❓ UNKNOWN
- **Pricing:**
  - Green Belt: $399
  - Black Belt: $499
  - Combined: 40%+ less than IASSC/ASQ
- **Features:** Online certification, immediate access
- **Recommendation:** MEDIUM-HIGH PRIORITY - Affordable, good margin potential

**IASSC (International Association for Six Sigma Certification)**
- **Website:** https://iassc.org
- **Partner Program:** ✅ YES - Authorized Exam Administrators
- **Program Type:** Distributor/reseller model
- **Pricing for Administrators:**
  - Green Belt Exam Packet: $220 each
  - Black Belt Exam Packet: $300 each
  - Volume discounts: 5% (50 exams) to 25% (500 exams)
- **Retail Pricing:**
  - Green Belt exam: $295
  - Black Belt exam: $395
- **How to Apply:** IASSC Exam Administration Program
- **Recommendation:** HIGH PRIORITY for high-volume partners

**Training Providers:**
- Mississippi State CE: Lean Six Sigma Green + Black Belt (exam included)
- Tallahassee State College: Online Lean Six Sigma
- University of New Mexico: Lean Six Sigma programs
- Purdue University: LSS Online Certification
- Simplilearn: Six Sigma training and certification

#### **Pricing Range:**
- Green Belt: $300-2,000
- Black Belt: $500-5,000
- Combined programs: $1,000-8,000

#### **Time to Complete:**
- Green Belt: 1-2 months
- Black Belt: 2-4 months

#### **Military Benefits:**
- Highly valued in defense contracting
- GI Bill eligible through many providers
- Free/low-cost options through some military programs

#### **Recommendation:** HIGH PRIORITY - Multiple entry points, good pricing, high demand
