## ONLINE LEARNING PLATFORMS WITH AFFILIATE PROGRAMS

### 1. Coursera

#### **Platform Information:**
- **Website:** https://www.coursera.org
- **Content:** University-level courses, certificates, degrees
- **Partners:** Google, IBM, Stanford, etc.
- **Certifications:** 27+ certificates from Google, IBM, AWS

#### **Affiliate Program:** ✅ YES
- **Commission:** Available through affiliate signup
- **Cookie Duration:** Standard terms
- **Target Affiliates:** Educational content creators, influencers, websites

#### **Military Benefits:**
- **FREE ACCESS** through multiple military partnerships:
  - USO Partnership
  - ACT Now Education - 1 year free access
  - Hire Heroes USA Partnership
  - Veterans Transition Support
- **Courses:** 10,000+ courses, certificates, specializations
- **Duration:** 1 full year free access
- **Eligibility:** Military community (active duty, veterans, spouses)

#### **Pricing (Without Military Discount):**
- Individual courses: $49-99
- Specializations: $39-79/month
- Professional Certificates: $39-79/month
- Degrees: $15,000-45,000

#### **Affiliate Strategy:**
- **Challenge:** Many veterans get free access
- **Opportunity:** Market to family members, career changers post-service
- **Recommendation:** MEDIUM PRIORITY - Highlight free military access, affiliate for non-military family members

### 2. Udemy

#### **Platform Information:**
- **Website:** https://www.udemy.com
- **Content:** 200,000+ courses on tech, business, design, marketing
- **Model:** Individual course purchases

#### **Affiliate Program:** ✅ YES
- **Commission:** Negotiate exclusive rates for high-volume affiliates
- **Standard Commission:** ~15-20% typical
- **Cookie Duration:** 7 days (standard)
- **Network:** May be available on affiliate networks

#### **Military Benefits:**
- **FREE for Military Spouses:** 5,000+ courses through DoD SECO (Spouse Education and Career Opportunities)
- **Access Via:** Military OneSource
- **Eligibility:** Military spouses

#### **Pricing:**
- Courses: $12.99-199.99 (frequent sales $9.99-19.99)
- Business plan: $360-720/year per user

#### **Affiliate Strategy:**
- **Opportunity:** Huge course library, frequent sales, high conversion
- **Challenge:** Spouse free access through Military OneSource
- **Recommendation:** HIGH PRIORITY - Negotiate higher rates with volume commitment

- **HIGH VALUE** if partnerships established

- Program cost: $15,000-45,000

- Subscription: $20-50/month

- **RECURRING REVENUE** model

- Course cost: $10-200

- **VOLUME PLAY** - need high traffic

### DO:

✅ Focus on high-value partnerships (bootcamps, degrees)
