# 30-Day Free Marketing Roadmap for Military Transition Toolkit

## Executive Summary

This roadmap provides a comprehensive, zero-cost marketing strategy to promote the Military Transition Toolkit (MTT) across veteran and military communities. The plan leverages free directories, resource pages, guest posting, podcasts, PR opportunities, and military publications.

---

- **National Resource Directory (nrd.gov)** - Primary searchable database vetted for service members, veterans, families, and caregivers

### Directory Submission Template

**Short Description (50 words):**
"Military Transition Toolkit (MTT) is a free web application helping service members transition from military to civilian life. MTT provides career planning tools, education benefit calculators, healthcare guidance, and curated resources for veterans navigating post-service opportunities."

**Medium Description (100 words):**
"Military Transition Toolkit (MTT) is a comprehensive, free web application designed specifically for service members transitioning from military to civilian life. Built by veterans, for veterans, MTT offers career planning tools, education benefit calculators, retirement planning resources, and healthcare guidance. The platform features a curated resource directory, personalized transition dashboards, and expert guidance for navigating VA benefits, job searches, and educational opportunities. Whether you're retiring after 20 years or completing your first enlistment, MTT provides the tools and resources you need to successfully transition to civilian life."

**Long Description (250 words):**
"Military Transition Toolkit (MTT) is a comprehensive, free web application designed specifically for service members transitioning from military to civilian life. Founded by veterans who experienced the challenges of transition firsthand, MTT addresses the critical gap in accessible, centralized transition resources.

The platform offers:
- **Career Planning Tools**: Resume builders, job search resources, and civilian career translation guides
- **Education Benefits Calculator**: GI Bill comparison tools and education planning resources
- **Retirement Planning**: Financial calculators and retirement benefit guidance
- **Healthcare Navigation**: VA healthcare enrollment guidance and benefit explanations
- **Resource Directory**: Curated database of veteran services, organizations, and support programs

MTT serves all transitioning service members, from those completing their first enlistment to 20+ year retirees. The platform is completely free, with no ads or paywalls, ensuring every veteran has access to the resources they need.

Built with input from transition counselors, veterans service organizations, and hundreds of transitioning service members, MTT consolidates information that is typically scattered across dozens of government websites, PDFs, and outdated resources. The user-friendly interface guides veterans through each phase of transition, from initial planning to post-separation success.

Whether you're seeking employment, pursuing education, planning retirement, or navigating VA benefits, the Military Transition Toolkit provides the comprehensive support you need to successfully transition to civilian life."

## Tips for Maximum Impact

### Do's
✅ **Personalize every outreach** - Reference specific episodes, articles, or content from the target
✅ **Lead with value** - Explain what their audience will gain, not just what you want
✅ **Follow submission guidelines exactly** - Read and follow every publication's specific requirements
✅ **Follow up professionally** - One follow-up after 7-10 days is appropriate
✅ **Build relationships** - Think long-term partnerships, not one-time placements
✅ **Share published content** - Amplify any coverage you receive to show appreciation
✅ **Track everything** - Maintain detailed records of all outreach and responses

### Don'ts
❌ **Don't mass-blast generic pitches** - Quality over quantity
❌ **Don't be overly promotional** - Focus on helping veterans, not just promoting MTT
❌ **Don't ignore guidelines** - Publications receive hundreds of pitches; stand out by following rules
❌ **Don't give up after no response** - Many editors are overwhelmed; timing matters
❌ **Don't forget to say thank you** - Gratitude builds relationships
❌ **Don't neglect follow-through** - If you promise content by a date, deliver
❌ **Don't oversell** - Be authentic about what MTT can and can't do
