# 30 Free Resources for Veteran Entrepreneurs

## Overview
This document contains 30+ free resources for veteran entrepreneurs, including accelerators, mentorship programs, free tools, grants, and certifications. All resources listed are completely free or offer free tiers specifically for veterans.

## üìö Business Accelerators & Bootcamps

### 1. **Entrepreneurship Bootcamp for Veterans (EBV)**
- **Description**: Intensive entrepreneurship program with 30-day online course, 9-day residential bootcamp, and 12 months of mentorship
- **Cost**: Completely FREE (covers travel, lodging, meals)
- **Eligibility**: Post-9/11 veterans with service-connected disabilities
- **Application**: https://ivmf.syracuse.edu/programs/entrepreneurship/start-up/ebv/
- **Multiple Locations**: UCLA Anderson, Texas A&M, Syracuse University, and more

### 2. **Warrior Rising - SDVET Program**
- **Description**: 4-week web-based instruction followed by Warrior Academy and Warrior University programs
- **Cost**: FREE for service-disabled veterans
- **Eligibility**: Service-disabled veterans (proof of service-connected disability required)
- **Application**: https://www.warriorrising.org/programs-warrior-academy/
- **Contact**: programming@WarriorRising.org

### 3. **Warrior Rising - Warrior University**
- **Description**: 8-week online veteran business accelerator program
- **Cost**: FREE (requires DD214 or Government ID)
- **Eligibility**: All veterans
- **Application**: https://www.warriorrising.org/veteran-business-accelerator/

### 4. **Veteran-Owned Small Business Accelerator (VOSBA)**
- **Description**: SBA-partnered accelerator for veteran-owned businesses
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Military veterans and their spouses
- **Application**: https://www.venturecenter.co/accelerators/veteran-owned-small-business-accelerator/

### 5. **National Veterans Entrepreneurship Program (VEP)**
- **Description**: University of Tennessee Chattanooga's entrepreneur training program
- **Cost**: FREE (underwritten by sponsors)
- **Eligibility**: Selected veterans
- **Application**: https://www.utc.edu/gary-w-rollins-college-of-business/outreach-programs/national-veterans-entrepreneurship-program
- **Deadline**: Applications open September 17, 2025; deadline April 30, 2026

### 6. **VETtoCEO Accelerator Program**
- **Description**: Live online program with networking and business training (formerly "Entrepreneurship for Transitioning Warriors")
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Veterans, Active/Guard/Reserve military members
- **Application**: https://www.vettoceo.org/accelerator
- **Next Program**: September 23 - November 18, 2025
- **Contact**: help@vettoceo.org

### 7. **Bunker Labs - Veterans in Residence (VIR)**
- **Description**: 6-month business incubator with community, education, and resources
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Veteran and military spouse entrepreneurs
- **Application**: https://bunkerlabs.org/
- **Details**: Weekly huddles, monthly meetups, masterclasses, showcase event
- **Results**: 1,400+ participants, $25M+ raised, $50M+ revenue

### 8. **WeWork Veterans in Residence**
- **Description**: 6-month incubator with free WeWork membership and workspace (partnership with Bunker Labs)
- **Cost**: FREE workspace for 6 months
- **Eligibility**: Veterans and military spouses
- **Application**: Through Bunker Labs - https://bunkerlabs.org/
- **Locations**: 22 cities nationwide + virtual cohorts

### 9. **Patriot Boot Camp**
- **Description**: 3-day tech entrepreneurship bootcamp with mentorship and pitch competition
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Veterans, military spouses, technology-focused ventures
- **Application**: https://patriotbootcamp.org/
- **Impact**: 1,000+ alumni, $150M+ raised, 1,900+ jobs created

### 10. **PenFed Foundation Veteran Entrepreneur Program**
- **Description**: 3-day in-person seminar + 6-week virtual accelerator for scalable startups
- **Cost**: FREE (covers all travel expenses, takes zero equity)
- **Eligibility**: Veterans or military spouse founders/co-founders, early-stage companies
- **Application**: https://penfedfoundation.org/how-we-help/vep/
- **Cohorts**: Multiple per year (March, May, August 2025)

## üéì Training & Education Programs

### 16. **Boots to Business (B2B)**
- **Description**: 2-day entrepreneurship basics course (part of DoD TAP)
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Transitioning service members
- **Application**: https://www.sba.gov/local-assistance/resource-partners/veterans-business-outreach-centers-vboc
- **Offered**: On military installations worldwide

### 17. **Boots to Business Reboot**
- **Description**: B2B extended to veterans of all eras in communities
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: All veterans, National Guard, Reserve members
- **Application**: https://www.sba.gov/local-assistance/resource-partners/veterans-business-outreach-centers-vboc

### 18. **Boots to Business Revenue Readiness**
- **Description**: 6-week online program turning ideas into business models
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Must complete B2B or B2B Reboot first
- **Application**: Through VBOC - https://www.sba.gov/local-assistance/resource-partners/veterans-business-outreach-centers-vboc

### 19. **IVMF V-WISE**
- **Description**: Veteran Women Igniting the Spirit of Entrepreneurship - 15-day online + 3-day residential
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Women veterans, active duty women, women military spouses
- **Application**: https://ivmf.syracuse.edu/programs/entrepreneurship/
- **Coverage**: All costs including travel, lodging covered

### 20. **IVMF VETNET**
- **Description**: Free webinar series about careers and entrepreneurship after military service
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Anyone
- **Application**: https://ivmf.syracuse.edu/programs/entrepreneurship/

### 21. **IVMF Onward to Opportunity**
- **Description**: No-cost training and professional certifications
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Transitioning service members, veterans, military spouses
- **Application**: https://ivmf.syracuse.edu/

### 22. **VETRN Entrepreneurship Program**
- **Description**: 12-week online program for early-stage businesses (every other week)
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Veteran small business owners, service-disabled veterans, National Guard, family members
- **Application**: https://vetrn.org/entrepreneur-program/
- **Includes**: Industry/financial mentor from day one, 80 hours of training
- **Contact**: https://vetrn.org/apply/

### 23. **VETRN MBA Program**
- **Description**: Advanced program for established businesses (min $125K annual revenue)
- **Cost**: FREE (100% SBA funded)
- **Eligibility**: Businesses with 1+ year in business, $125K+ annual revenue
- **Application**: https://vetrn.org/
- **Includes**: 80 hours training, mentorship, professional resource network

### 24. **VETtoCEO - Pass the Guidon (ETA Program)**
- **Description**: 3-week program on Entrepreneurship Through Acquisition
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Veterans, Active/Guard/Reserve military
- **Application**: https://vettoceo.org/ptg
- **Focus**: How to buy and grow small businesses

### 25. **Career Forward - Google Career Certificates**
- **Description**: Free access to Google Career Certificates + 12-week paid job fellowships
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: 8,000 transitioning service members, veterans, military spouses
- **Application**: https://www.hiringourheroes.org/ (Hiring Our Heroes)
- **Benefits**: Self-paced online training, job search support, employer connections with 150+ companies

- **Cost**: FREE to apply
- **Eligibility**: Veteran and military spouse-owned small businesses

- **Deadline**: Before December 15 annually

## üè¢ Government Resources & Certification

### 28. **Veterans Business Outreach Centers (VBOC)**
- **Description**: Free business training, counseling, mentorship nationwide
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Service members, veterans, National Guard, Reserve, military spouses/families
- **Application**: https://www.sba.gov/local-assistance/resource-partners/veterans-business-outreach-centers-vboc
- **Services**: Workshops, concept assessments, business plans, pre-business plan workshops

### 29. **SBA VOSB/SDVOSB Certification**
- **Description**: Official certification for federal contracting opportunities
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: Veteran-owned (VOSB) or service-disabled veteran-owned (SDVOSB) businesses
- **Application**: https://veterans.certify.sba.gov/
- **Benefits**: Access to federal set-aside contracts (5%+ of federal dollars), surplus property programs
- **Timeline**: Up to 90 days for certificate
- **Support**: Call 800-862-8088 or email vetcert@sba.gov

### 30. **SBA Office of Veterans Business Development**
- **Description**: Central hub for all SBA veteran entrepreneur programs and resources
- **Cost**: FREE
- **Eligibility**: All veterans
- **Website**: https://www.sba.gov/business-guide/grow-your-business/veteran-owned-businesses
- **Resources**: Boots to Business, VBOCs, contracting assistance, loan programs
